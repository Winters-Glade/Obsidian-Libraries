# Colonial America

> **Source:** [History102](https://www.youtube.com/@History102-qg5oj) — [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xbrn5cA58x4)

## Key Themes

- First colonies: Jamestown (1607) and Plymouth (1620)
- Thirteen colonies: regional differences (New England, Middle, Southern)
- Indentured servitude and the shift to enslaved labor
- Relations with Native Americans: conflict and cooperation
- Colonial government and the seeds of self-rule
- Mercantilism and the Navigation Acts
- Religious diversity: Puritans, Quakers, Catholics
- Pre-Revolutionary tensions and the path to independence
