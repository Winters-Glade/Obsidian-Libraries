# African Colonialism

> **Source:** [History102](https://www.youtube.com/@History102-qg5oj) — [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=X6Zsq2DsMuw)

## Key Themes

- Berlin Conference (1884-85) and the Scramble for Africa
- Artificial borders and ethnic division
- Extractive economies: rubber, diamonds, gold, palm oil
- Congo Free State and Belgian atrocities
- British, French, German, Portuguese colonial administrations
- Indirect rule vs. direct assimilation policies
- Missionaries, education, and cultural transformation
- African resistance: wars, rebellions, and diplomatic strategies
