## Key Themes

- Conquest of the Americas: Cortes, Pizarro, and the Conquistadors
- Silver mines of Potosi and the global silver trade
- Encomienda system and indigenous labor exploitation
- Catholic missions and religious conversion
- Bourbon Reforms and administrative centralization
- Decline of Spanish power in the 17th-18th centuries
- Independence movements across Latin America
