# Malcolm Collins Interview: The Coming Population Collapse

> **Source:** [History102](https://www.youtube.com/@History102-qg5oj) — [Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qlhl1ZyZN10)

## Guest Biography

Malcolm Collins is a demography researcher and co-founder of The Collins Institute, which studies population trends, fertility rates, and the social and economic implications of demographic decline. He is known for his work on the causes and consequences of the global fertility crash, pronatalist policy, and the long-term trajectory of human population. He formerly worked as a venture capitalist in South Korea (director of strategy at the top early-stage firm), and previously worked on an exhibit still displayed at the Smithsonian's human origins department. He co-hosts the podcast *Based Camp* with his wife Simone, and has authored several books including a Wall Street Journal bestseller.

## Key Arguments & Discussion Points

### 1. The Scale of the Crisis
- South Korea's fertility rate is 0.72 — for every 100 Koreans alive today, there will be only ~6.1 great-grandchildren, assuming no further decline
- If the US continues its 2010-2020 rate of fertility decline, for every 100 Americans alive today there would be only 3.7 great-grandkids
- **There does not appear to be a floor on fertility decline** — not a single country has hit one yet
- Korea is actually getting worse: the "four-nose" (4B) movement — no sex, no dating, no men, no marriage — is growing among Korean women
- "Pig and snake" problem: Korea currently has ~60% of population over age 40; by the time 80% is over 50, it's statistically impossible to reverse collapse

### 2. Economic Unraveling
- The entire Western civilizational structure (and China, Japan, Korea) is a Ponzi scheme requiring constant growth
- Debt is leveraged to the floor at every level: individuals, states, cities, nations — amazing when growing, catastrophic when shrinking
- Productivity per worker has been increasing linearly (~200 years), not exponentially, while consumer and producer growth was exponential
- When those growth drivers level off or decline, the economy enters permanent contraction

### 3. Mass Cultural Extinction
- A "great dying" — a mass cultural extinction event where many cultures disappear over the next 100 years, likely exceeding the age of colonialism
- **Groups most resistant** to prosperity-induced collapse: conservative Christians and conservative Jews
- **Groups most vulnerable**: Native Americans (1.2 birth rate), East Asians, South Asians, progressive Jews
- Contrary to accusations of racial motivation: "Generally white conservative groups are the least fucked group in the entire world" — the groups collapsing fastest are non-white
- India has already fallen below replacement rate
- All of Central America, South America, and the Caribbean fell below replacement rate as of 2019 (per UN statistics)

### 4. Immigration Cannot Solve the Problem
- The argument "we can solve this with immigrants" fails because the source countries are also below replacement
- Progressives' "not racist plan" amounts to importing from Africa to support non-working old white people on Social Security
- This creates a system "reliant on keeping a few African countries so poor that they just naturally motivate reproductive rates" — treating them like "human farms"
- The world is on the Titanic: "no matter what we do we are hitting that iceberg"

### 5. Prosperity-Induced Fertility Collapse
- Most strongly correlated with prosperity (wealth), then education access, then gender equality
- Within and between countries: more wealth = fewer children, except at the very top (>$500K/year in US)
- The average citizen in a country above replacement rate earns under $5,000 USD per year
- Poor people and poor countries have more children — refuting the "we can't afford kids" narrative

### 6. Cultural Software & Religion
- Religions are "cultivars" (memetic cultural clusters) subject to biological evolutionary pressure
- They co-evolved with human brains as "firmware/software packages"
- Traditions like Lent, Ramadan, and arbitrary self-denial rituals likely strengthen inhibitory pathways in the prefrontal cortex
- Ripping out these packages removed the primary psychological motivation for reproduction
- Conservatives have been happier than progressives since Pew started recording data
- Negative externalities: homophobia appears in every long-lived tradition — traditions that were homophobic had more kids

### 7. Post-Scarcity & Self-Victimization
- The Mouse Utopia experiments suggest what happens to mammals in post-scarcity environments — population went to zero every time
- The highest luxury in a post-scarcity environment is not hedonism but **removal of personal responsibility through self-victimization**
- Declining sex rates accompany this, not extreme hedonism (which characterized late Roman Republic — a masculine post-scarcity society)
- The current era is a feminized post-scarcity environment

### 8. Dating Market Collapse
- Average male on Tinder gets less than 1% of women swiping right
- Dating apps broke the relationship market by creating a disconnect between sex marketplaces and marriage marketplaces
- Women have high value in sex marketplaces but overestimate their position in marriage marketplaces
- Manosphere/red pill advice optimizes for short-term sex — "dark triad shiz actually works at securing thots" but not marriageable partners
- Key traits to sort for in a partner: **gratitude** (biggest) and **work ethic** — not looks or status signaling
- Divorce rates (50%+; higher for college-educated women) and the financial devastation of divorce deter men from marriage
- Pronatalis Foundation working on arranged marriage markets (a new "London season")

### 9. Systemic Dysfunction at Elite Levels
- Urban monoculture (NY, Paris, Tokyo) is more similar to itself than to surrounding rural cultures — and is completely unable to motivate reproductive fertility
- Elite institutions are "the furthest gone" — psychology has become a cult building dependency in patients
- Elite culture is characterized by single-argument reasoning, no capacity to test counterarguments
- "The left is not Stalinism" only because they lack the competence, not the ideological similarity
- The new left is motivated by **negative utilitarianism**: removal of all negative emotions (trigger warnings, eliminating testing, fentanyl on street corners)
- "Justicalism": asking "what would be true in the most just world?" instead of "what does the evidence support?"

### 10. Hope as the Critical Variable
- The only persistent fertility gain in a low-fertility country: Georgia after the Rose Revolution — because people had hope after removing the communist political class
- Israel's high fertility: Jews feel they're having kids for their people, not for an economic elite
- China's "lying flat" movement and "we are the last generation" meme reflect hopelessness — people won't breed for the benefit of a ruling class

### 11. Biological Factors
- Male sperm rates declined by over 51% in the last 50 years
- Testosterone rates declined by ~20-30% in the last 20-30 years
- Endocrine disruptors (plastics, etc.) affect in-utero gender differentiation; male infants exposed show lower anogenital distance and less male-typical play at age 7
- Environmental regulation focused on carcinogens ignored fertility-disrupting chemicals

### 12. Civilizational Cycle Perspective
- Three civilizational cycles, each ~2,000 years: Bronze Age → Roman → current
- When macro-civilizational strategy fails, society fractures to local level (e.g., Rome → France, Italy, North Africa)
- "When a civilization is collapsing it pushes the exact opposite of what it should" — doubling down on self-destructive behaviors
- Examples: Hitler killing more Jews as he knew he'd lose; Aztecs increasing human sacrifice before Spanish arrival
- Current societies push anti-natal social norms harder precisely when survival requires the opposite

## Points of Agreement/Disagreement

- **Agreement** that demographic decline is one of the most important long-term challenges facing civilization
- **Agreement** that parts of the progressive movement substitute ideological reasoning ("justicalism") for evidence-based reasoning
- **Agreement** that the dating/relationship market is structurally broken for both men and women
- **Possible disagreement** with Collins' framing of religious/cultural "software" as evolved fitness-maximizers and his critique of secular modernity
- **Possible disagreement** with his negative utilitarianism thesis as the core driver of the new left
- Collins argues that **diversity** (within a country) is correlated with higher fertility among wealthy nations (US, Israel) — may challenge listeners' assumptions
