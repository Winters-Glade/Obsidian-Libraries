# Map of Content — 20th Century Wars & Ideologies

A collection of History 102 podcast notes covering major 20th century wars and ideologies.

## Wars

- [[World War 1]] — The war of prosperity, driven by the Industrial Revolution and shifting European power dynamics
- [[WW2 in Europe]] — Hitler's war, unresolved Versailles tensions, the fall of Europe to totalitarianism
- [[WW2 in the Pacific]] — Japan's drive for empire, the Japanese-Chinese War's Pacific extension, collective authoritarian spiral

## Ideologies & Regimes

- [[Communism]] — Marxism's invisible dominance, the 100-million-life cost, Illuminati origins of the modern left
- [[Soviet Union]] — The attempt to create a new civilization, continuation of Tsarist trends, materialist worldview
- [[Prussian Empire]] — Leadership-driven empire, Hohenzollern dynasty, Bismarck's pragmatism, Nietzsche's precursors

## Regional History

- [[20th Century East Europe]] — The greatest tragedy in human history, written out of core narratives
- [[Mexican History]] — Three tyrannies, Mesoamerican civilization, the struggle for escape velocity

## Cultural Decades

- [[1950s]] — America's mythological origin, the consolidation of managerial class dominance
- [[1960s]] — The most important cultural decade, the cultural revolution of the bureaucratic class
