# Genesis 41:55–57


## Passage

> **55** And when all the land of Egypt was famished, the people cried to Pharaoh for bread: and Pharaoh said unto all the Egyptians, Go unto Joseph; what he saith to you, do. **56** And the famine was over all the face of the earth: And Joseph opened all the storehouses, and sold unto the Egyptians; and the famine waxed sore in the land of Egypt. {all the storehouses: Heb. all wherein was} **57** And all countries came into Egypt to Joseph for to buy {corn}; because that the famine was {so} sore in all lands.
>
> — Genesis 41:55–57 (KJV)
