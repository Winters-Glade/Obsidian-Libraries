# Deuteronomy 23:3-6


## Passage

> **3** An Ammonite or Moabite shall not enter into the congregation of the LORD; even to their tenth generation shall they not enter into the congregation of the LORD for ever: **4** Because they met you not with bread and with water in the way, when ye came forth out of Egypt; and because they hired against thee Balaam the son of Beor of Pethor of Mesopotamia, to curse thee. **5** Nevertheless the LORD thy God would not hearken unto Balaam; but the LORD thy God turned the curse into a blessing unto thee, because the LORD thy God loved thee. **6** Thou shalt not seek their peace nor their prosperity all thy days for ever. {prosperity: Heb. good}
>
> — Deuteronomy 23:3-6 (KJV)
