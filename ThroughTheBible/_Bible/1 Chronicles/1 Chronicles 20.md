# 1 Chronicles 20


## Passage

> And it came to pass, that after the year was expired, at the time that kings go out {to battle}, Joab led forth the power of the army, and wasted the country of the children of Ammon, and came and besieged Rabbah. But David tarried at Jerusalem. And Joab smote Rabbah, and destroyed it. {after...: Heb. at the return of the year} ... These were born unto the giant in Gath; and they fell by the hand of David, and by the hand of his servants.
>
> — 1 Chronicles 20 (KJV)
