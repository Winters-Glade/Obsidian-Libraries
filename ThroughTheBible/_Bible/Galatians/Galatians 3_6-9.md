# Galatians 3:6-9


## Passage

> **6** Even as Abraham believed God, and it was accounted to him for righteousness. {accounted: or, imputed} **7** Know ye therefore that they which are of faith, the same are the children of Abraham. **8** And the scripture, foreseeing that God would justify the heathen through faith, preached before the gospel unto Abraham, {saying}, In thee shall all nations be blessed. **9** So then they which be of faith are blessed with faithful Abraham.
>
> — Galatians 3:6-9 (KJV)
