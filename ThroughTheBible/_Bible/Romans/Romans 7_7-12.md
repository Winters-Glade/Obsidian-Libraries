# Romans 7:7-12


## Passage

> **7** What shall we say then? {Is} the law sin? God forbid. Nay, I had not known sin, but by the law: for I had not known lust, except the law had said, Thou shalt not covet. {lust: or, concupiscence} **8** But sin, taking occasion by the commandment, wrought in me all manner of concupiscence. For without the law sin {was} dead. **9** For I was alive without the law once: but when the commandment came, sin revived, and I died. **10** And the commandment, which {was ordained} to life, I found {to be} unto death. **11** For sin, taking occasion by the commandment, deceived me, and by it slew {me}. **12** Wherefore the law {is} holy, and the commandment holy, and just, and good.
>
> — Romans 7:7-12 (KJV)
