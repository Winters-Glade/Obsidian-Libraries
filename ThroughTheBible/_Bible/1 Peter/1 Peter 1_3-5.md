# 1 Peter 1:3-5


## Passage

> **3** Blessed {be} the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, which according to his abundant mercy hath begotten us again unto a lively hope by the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead, {abundant: Gr. much} **4** To an inheritance incorruptible, and undefiled, and that fadeth not away, reserved in heaven for you, {for you: or, for us} **5** Who are kept by the power of God through faith unto salvation ready to be revealed in the last time.
>
> — 1 Peter 1:3-5 (KJV)
