# 2 Chronicles 9


## Passage

> And when the queen of Sheba heard of the fame of Solomon, she came to prove Solomon with hard questions at Jerusalem, with a very great company, and camels that bare spices, and gold in abundance, and precious stones: and when she was come to Solomon, she communed with him of all that was in her heart. ... And Solomon slept with his fathers, and he was buried in the city of David his father: and Rehoboam his son reigned in his stead.
>
> — 2 Chronicles 9 (KJV)
