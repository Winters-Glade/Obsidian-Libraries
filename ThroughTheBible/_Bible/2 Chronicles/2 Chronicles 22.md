# 2 Chronicles 22


## Passage

> And the inhabitants of Jerusalem made Ahaziah his youngest son king in his stead: for the band of men that came with the Arabians to the camp had slain all the eldest. So Ahaziah the son of Jehoram king of Judah reigned. ... And he was with them hid in the house of God six years: and Athaliah reigned over the land.
>
> — 2 Chronicles 22 (KJV)
