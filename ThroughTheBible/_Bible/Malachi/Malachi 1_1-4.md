# Malachi 1:1-4


## Passage

> **1** The burden of the word of the LORD to Israel by Malachi. {by...: Heb. by the hand of} **2** I have loved you, saith the LORD. Yet ye say, Wherein hast thou loved us? {Was} not Esau Jacob's brother? saith the LORD: yet I loved Jacob, **3** And I hated Esau, and laid his mountains and his heritage waste for the dragons of the wilderness. **4** Whereas Edom saith, We are impoverished, but we will return and build the desolate places; thus saith the LORD of hosts, They shall build, but I will throw down; and they shall call them, The border of wickedness, and, The people against whom the LORD hath indignation for ever.
>
> — Malachi 1:1-4 (KJV)
