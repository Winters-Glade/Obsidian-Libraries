# Obadiah 1


## Passage

> The vision of Obadiah. Thus saith the Lord GOD concerning Edom; We have heard a rumour from the LORD, and an ambassador is sent among the heathen, Arise ye, and let us rise up against her in battle. ... And saviours shall come up on mount Zion to judge the mount of Esau; and the kingdom shall be the LORD'S.
>
> — Obadiah 1 (KJV)
