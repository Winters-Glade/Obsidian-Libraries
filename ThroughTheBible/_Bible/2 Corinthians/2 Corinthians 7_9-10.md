# 2 Corinthians 7:9-10


## Passage

> **9** Now I rejoice, not that ye were made sorry, but that ye sorrowed to repentance: for ye were made sorry after a godly manner, that ye might receive damage by us in nothing. {after...: or, according to God} **10** For godly sorrow worketh repentance to salvation not to be repented of: but the sorrow of the world worketh death.
>
> — 2 Corinthians 7:9-10 (KJV)
