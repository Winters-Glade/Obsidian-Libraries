# Isaiah 7:7-12


## Passage

> **7** Thus saith the Lord GOD, It shall not stand, neither shall it come to pass. **8** For the head of Syria {is} Damascus, and the head of Damascus {is} Rezin; and within threescore and five years shall Ephraim be broken, that it be not a people. {that...: Heb. from a people} **9** And the head of Ephraim {is} Samaria, and the head of Samaria {is} Remaliah's son. If ye will not believe, surely ye shall not be established. {If...: or, Do ye not believe? it is because ye are not stable} **10** Moreover the LORD spake again unto Ahaz, saying, {Moreover...: Heb. And the LORD added to speak} **11** Ask thee a sign of the LORD thy God; ask it either in the depth, or in the height above. {ask it...: or, make thy petition deep} **12** But Ahaz said, I will not ask, neither will I tempt the LORD.
>
> — Isaiah 7:7-12 (KJV)
