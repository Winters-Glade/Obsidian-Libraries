# Isaiah 33


## Passage

> Woe to thee that spoilest, and thou {wast} not spoiled; and dealest treacherously, and they dealt not treacherously with thee! when thou shalt cease to spoil, thou shalt be spoiled; {and} when thou shalt make an end to deal treacherously, they shall deal treacherously with thee. ... And the inhabitant shall not say, I am sick: the people that dwell therein {shall be} forgiven {their} iniquity.
>
> — Isaiah 33 (KJV)
