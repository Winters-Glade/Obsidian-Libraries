# Leviticus 7:12-15


## Passage

> **12** If he offer it for a thanksgiving, then he shall offer with the sacrifice of thanksgiving unleavened cakes mingled with oil, and unleavened wafers anointed with oil, and cakes mingled with oil, of fine flour, fried. **13** Besides the cakes, he shall offer {for} his offering leavened bread with the sacrifice of thanksgiving of his peace offerings. **14** And of it he shall offer one out of the whole oblation {for} an heave offering unto the LORD, {and} it shall be the priest's that sprinkleth the blood of the peace offerings. **15** And the flesh of the sacrifice of his peace offerings for thanksgiving shall be eaten the same day that it is offered; he shall not leave any of it until the morning.
>
> — Leviticus 7:12-15 (KJV)
