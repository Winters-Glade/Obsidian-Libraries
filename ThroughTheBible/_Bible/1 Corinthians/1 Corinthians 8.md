# 1 Corinthians 8


## Passage

> Now as touching things offered unto idols, we know that we all have knowledge. Knowledge puffeth up, but charity edifieth. ... Wherefore, if meat make my brother to offend, I will eat no flesh while the world standeth, lest I make my brother to offend.
>
> — 1 Corinthians 8 (KJV)
