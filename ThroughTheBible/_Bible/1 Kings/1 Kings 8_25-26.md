# 1 Kings 8:25-26


## Passage

> **25** Therefore now, LORD God of Israel, keep with thy servant David my father that thou promisedst him, saying, There shall not fail thee a man in my sight to sit on the throne of Israel; so that thy children take heed to their way, that they walk before me as thou hast walked before me. {fail...: Heb. be cut off unto thee a man from my sight} {so that: Heb. only if} **26** And now, O God of Israel, let thy word, I pray thee, be verified, which thou spakest unto thy servant David my father.
>
> — 1 Kings 8:25-26 (KJV)
