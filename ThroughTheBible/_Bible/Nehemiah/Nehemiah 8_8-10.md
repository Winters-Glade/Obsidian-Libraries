# Nehemiah 8:8-10


## Passage

> **8** So they read in the book in the law of God distinctly, and gave the sense, and caused {them} to understand the reading. **9** And Nehemiah, which {is} the Tirshatha, and Ezra the priest the scribe, and the Levites that taught the people, said unto all the people, This day {is} holy unto the LORD your God; mourn not, nor weep. For all the people wept, when they heard the words of the law. {the Tirshatha: or, the governor} **10** Then he said unto them, Go your way, eat the fat, and drink the sweet, and send portions unto them for whom nothing is prepared: for {this} day {is} holy unto our Lord: neither be ye sorry; for the joy of the LORD is your strength.
>
> — Nehemiah 8:8-10 (KJV)
