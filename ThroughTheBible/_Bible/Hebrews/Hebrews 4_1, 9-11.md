# Hebrews 4:1, 9-11


## Passage

> **1** Let us therefore fear, lest, a promise being left {us} of entering into his rest, any of you should seem to come short of it. **9** There remaineth therefore a rest to the people of God. {rest: or, keeping of a sabbath} **10** For he that is entered into his rest, he also hath ceased from his own works, as God {did} from his. **11** Let us labour therefore to enter into that rest, lest any man fall after the same example of unbelief. {unbelief: or, disobedience}
>
> — Hebrews 4:1, 9-11 (KJV)
