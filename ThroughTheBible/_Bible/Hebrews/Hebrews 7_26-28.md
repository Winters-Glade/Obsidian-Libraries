# Hebrews 7:26-28


## Passage

> **26** For such an high priest became us, {who is} holy, harmless, undefiled, separate from sinners, and made higher than the heavens; **27** Who needeth not daily, as those high priests, to offer up sacrifice, first for his own sins, and then for the people's: for this he did once, when he offered up himself. **28** For the law maketh men high priests which have infirmity; but the word of the oath, which was since the law, {maketh} the Son, who is consecrated for evermore. {consecrated: Gr. perfected}
>
> — Hebrews 7:26-28 (KJV)
