# Zechariah 14


## Passage

> Behold, the day of the LORD cometh, and thy spoil shall be divided in the midst of thee. ... Yea, every pot in Jerusalem and in Judah shall be holiness unto the LORD of hosts: and all they that sacrifice shall come and take of them, and seethe therein: and in that day there shall be no more the Canaanite in the house of the LORD of hosts.
>
> — Zechariah 14 (KJV)
