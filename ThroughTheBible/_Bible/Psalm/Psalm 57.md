# Psalm 57


## Passage

> [To the chief Musician, Altaschith, Michtam of David, when he fled from Saul in the cave.] Be merciful unto me, O God, be merciful unto me: for my soul trusteth in thee: yea, in the shadow of thy wings will I make my refuge, until {these} calamities be overpast. {Altaschith: or, Destroy not} {Michtam: or, A golden Psalm} ... Be thou exalted, O God, above the heavens: {let} thy glory {be} above all the earth.
>
> — Psalm 57 (KJV)
