# Psalm 104


## Passage

> Bless the LORD, O my soul. O LORD my God, thou art very great; thou art clothed with honour and majesty. ... Let the sinners be consumed out of the earth, and let the wicked be no more. Bless thou the LORD, O my soul. Praise ye the LORD.
>
> — Psalm 104 (KJV)
