# Psalm 17


## Passage

> [A Prayer of David.] Hear the right, O LORD, attend unto my cry, give ear unto my prayer, {that goeth} not out of feigned lips. {the right: Heb. justice} {not...: Heb. without lips of deceit} ... As for me, I will behold thy face in righteousness: I shall be satisfied, when I awake, with thy likeness.
>
> — Psalm 17 (KJV)
