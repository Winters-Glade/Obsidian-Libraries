# Psalm 22


## Passage

> [To the chief Musician upon Aijeleth Shahar, A Psalm of David.] My God, my God, why hast thou forsaken me? {why art thou so} far from helping me, {and from} the words of my roaring? {Aijeleth...: or, the hind of the morning} {helping...: Heb. my salvation} ... They shall come, and shall declare his righteousness unto a people that shall be born, that he hath done {this}.
>
> — Psalm 22 (KJV)
