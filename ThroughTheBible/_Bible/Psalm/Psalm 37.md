# Psalm 37


## Passage

> [{A Psalm} of David.] Fret not thyself because of evildoers, neither be thou envious against the workers of iniquity. ... And the LORD shall help them, and deliver them: he shall deliver them from the wicked, and save them, because they trust in him.
>
> — Psalm 37 (KJV)
