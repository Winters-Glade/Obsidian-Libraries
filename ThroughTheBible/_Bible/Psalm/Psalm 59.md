# Psalm 59


## Passage

> [To the chief Musician, Altaschith, Michtam of David; when Saul sent, and they watched the house to kill him.] Deliver me from mine enemies, O my God: defend me from them that rise up against me. {Altaschith...: or, Destroy not} {Michtam: or, A golden Psalm} {defend...: Heb. set me on high} ... Unto thee, O my strength, will I sing: for God {is} my defence, {and} the God of my mercy.
>
> — Psalm 59 (KJV)
