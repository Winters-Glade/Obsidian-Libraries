# Psalm 89


## Passage

> [Maschil of Ethan the Ezrahite.] I will sing of the mercies of the LORD for ever: with my mouth will I make known thy faithfulness to all generations. {Maschil...: or, A Psalm for Ethan the Ezrahite, to give instruction} {to all...: Heb. to generation and generation} ... Blessed {be} the LORD for evermore. Amen, and Amen.
>
> — Psalm 89 (KJV)
