# Mark 12:41-44


## Passage

> **41** And Jesus sat over against the treasury, and beheld how the people cast money into the treasury: and many that were rich cast in much. {money: a piece of brass money} **42** And there came a certain poor widow, and she threw in two mites, which make a farthing. {mites: it is the seventh part of one piece of that brass money} **43** And he called {unto him} his disciples, and saith unto them, Verily I say unto you, That this poor widow hath cast more in, than all they which have cast into the treasury: **44** For all {they} did cast in of their abundance; but she of her want did cast in all that she had, {even} all her living.
>
> — Mark 12:41-44 (KJV)
