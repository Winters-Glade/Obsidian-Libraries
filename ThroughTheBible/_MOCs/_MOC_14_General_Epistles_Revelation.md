# _MOC_14 — General Epistles & Revelation

> T2000 Topical Series — Pastor Chuck Smith
> Batch 14: Hebrews through Revelation (23 teachings)

These New Testament books form the closing canon of Scripture — from the
superiority of Christ in Hebrews through the apocalyptic visions of Revelation.
The General Epistles (Hebrews, James, 1-2 Peter, 1-3 John, Jude) address
practical Christian living, perseverance, false teaching, fellowship, and
the hope of Christ's return. Revelation unveils the consummation of history.

*Hebrews*

priesthood, and the old covenant. The theme is "better": a better hope, a better
