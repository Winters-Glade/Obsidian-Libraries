# MOC — Restoration & Wisdom Literature

> Batch 5 of the T2000 Topical Series — 31 teachings covering the Post-Exilic Restoration (Ezra, Nehemiah, Esther) and the Wisdom & Poetry Books (Job, Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, Song of Solomon).

These teachings from Pastor Chuck Smith explore how God restores His people after judgment and how His wisdom shapes the life of faith. From the rebuilding of Jerusalem to the deepest questions of human existence, this collection spans the full range of biblical experience — suffering, worship, practical wisdom, love, and the meaning of life itself.

## Key Themes

- **Divine Providence** — God's hidden hand in history, seen in Esther's rise and the restoration from exile
- **The Fear of the Lord** — The foundation of all wisdom (Proverbs, Job, Psalms)
- **Suffering and Faith** — Job's journey from complaint to encounter; the psalms of lament
- **The Good Shepherd** — God's personal care and guidance (Psalm 23)
- **True Wisdom** — Not mere knowledge, but reverent relationship with God
- **The Mediator** — Job's longing for a "daysman" fulfilled in Christ
- **Joy and Strength** — "The joy of the Lord is your strength"
- **The Church as Bride** — The beauty and purpose of God's people (Song of Solomon)
- **Resurrection Hope** — Job's question "If a man die, shall he live again?"
- **Prayer and Action** — Nehemiah's model of praying while keeping watch

## About the T2000 Series

The T2000 Topical Series features 271 verse-by-topic teachings by Pastor Chuck Smith, founder of the Calvary Chapel movement. Each teaching examines a specific passage or theme from Scripture, ranging from Genesis to Revelation. This batch covers restoration books (Ezra through Esther) and wisdom/poetry books (Job through Song of Solomon).
