## Key Themes Across This Batch

- **Holiness of God** — The vision of God's holiness (Isaiah 6) and the consuming fire (Isaiah 33) reveal the awe-inspiring majesty of God that both terrifies and transforms
- **Trust vs. Fear** — Multiple lectures address the choice between trusting God and succumbing to fear (Isaiah 12, 41)
- **The God Who Carries** — Unlike idols that need human support, God carries His people from birth to old age (Isaiah 46)
- **Power of God's Word** — God's Word accomplishes its purpose like rain waters the earth (Isaiah 55)
- **The Gospel in Isaiah** — The Anointed Servant brings good news, liberty, and healing (Isaiah 61)
- **The Resurrection** — The credibility of the resurrection is a foundation of Christian faith (Acts 26)
- **Christ's Kingship** — Jesus enters Jerusalem as the humble King of Glory (Luke 19)
