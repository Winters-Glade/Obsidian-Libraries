## About This Batch

These 16 teachings from the T2000 Topical Series cover the book of Acts — from the ascension of Christ and the coming of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost (Acts 1-2) through to Paul's final days under house arrest in Rome (Acts 28). The series traces the birth of the early church, the ministry of the apostles, the conversion of Paul, and the spread of the gospel from Jerusalem to the ends of the earth.

Each teaching focuses on a specific passage and theme from Acts, offering topical insights into the history, theology, and application of the early church's story.
