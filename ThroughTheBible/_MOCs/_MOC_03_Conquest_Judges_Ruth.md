# Map of Content — Conquest, Judges & Ruth

> **T2000 Topical Series** — Pastor Chuck Smith  \
> Covering topical teachings from the conquest of Canaan (Joshua), the period of the Judges, and the story of Ruth

## Overview

This batch covers 7 topical teachings spanning from the conquest of Canaan under Joshua (with an introductory teaching on the Incarnation from John's Gospel), through the turbulent period of the Judges, to the beautiful story of redemption in Ruth. These teachings explore the themes of covenant faithfulness, the consequences of compromise, the danger of spiritual neutrality, and the hope of redemption.

## Key Themes

- **[[The nations.md]]** — The Word became flesh (John 1:14); God dwelling among His people
- **[[Angel of the Lord.md]]** — The fatal flaw of failing to seek God before making decisions (Joshua 9:14)
- **[[Covenant Renewal]]** — The necessity of deliberate choice in serving God (Joshua 24:15)
- **[[Spiritual Warfare]]** — The curse of Meroz: neutrality in spiritual battle is sin (Judges 5:23)
- **[[Repentance]]** — God desires genuine heart change, not merely cries for relief (Judges 10:13-14)
- **[[Presumption]]** — Samson's tragedy: spiritual strength is not automatic (Judges 16:15-22)
- **[[Redemption]]** — The Goel (Kinsman-Redeemer) as a type of Christ (Ruth 3:9)
- **[[Grace]]** — The fullness of God's character revealed in Christ (John 1:14)
