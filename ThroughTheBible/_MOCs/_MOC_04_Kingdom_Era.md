# Kingdom Era — 1 Samuel through 2 Chronicles

> **MOC for Batch 4 of the T2000 Topical Series**
> Covers the period from the establishment of the monarchy through the divided kingdom to the Babylonian captivity.

## Overview

The Kingdom Era spans the books of 1 Samuel through 2 Chronicles, covering approximately 600 years of Israel's history — from the final judges through the United and Divided Monarchies to the Babylonian Captivity. This era includes the lives of Samuel, Saul, David, Solomon, and the prophets Elijah and Elisha.

## Timeline

```
c. 1050 BC — Samuel anoints Saul as first king
c. 1010 BC — David becomes king
c. 970 BC  — Solomon becomes king
c. 930 BC  — Kingdom divides (Jeroboam / Rehoboam)
c. 860 BC  — Elijah's ministry
c. 722 BC  — Fall of Northern Kingdom (Israel) to Assyria
c. 586 BC  — Fall of Southern Kingdom (Judah) to Babylon
```
