# Map of Content — Luke & John (Gospels)

> **T2000 Topical Series** — Pastor Chuck Smith  \
> Covering topical teachings from the Gospels of Luke and John

## Overview

This batch covers 26 topical teachings from the Gospels of Luke (14) and John (12) from Pastor Chuck Smith's T2000 Topical Series. These are verse-specific teachings that draw out practical and doctrinal truths from key passages.

## Luke — The Gospel of the Son of Man

Luke presents Jesus as the [[Son of Man]] — the compassionate Savior for all people. These topical teachings highlight Luke's unique emphases on prayer, the Holy Spirit, salvation, and Jesus' heart for the outcast.

### Luke's Distinctives in These Teachings

- **Compassion for the outcast** — He Receives Sinners (Luke 15), Zacchaeus (Luke 19)
- **Prayer and the Holy Spirit** — A recurring emphasis throughout Luke's Gospel
- **Salvation as God's pursuit** — The lost sheep, coin, and son; Zacchaeus; the penitent thief
- **Cost of discipleship** — What Makes For Life (12:15), Directed Service (5:1-11)
- **Christ-centered Scripture** — Burning Hearts on the Emmaus Road (24:32)

- **The seven "I AM" statements** — Good Shepherd (10:11), Resurrection and Life (11:25)
