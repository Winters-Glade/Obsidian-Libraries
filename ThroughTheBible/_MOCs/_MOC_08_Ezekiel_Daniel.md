## [[Ezekiel]] — Babylonian Exile (593-571 BC)

Ezekiel, a priest and prophet called during the Babylonian Exile, delivered some
of the most vivid visions in all of Scripture — the glory of God departing the
temple, the valley of dry bones, Gog and Magog, and the millennial temple with
the river of life.

## [[Daniel]] — Babylonian & Persian Periods (605-536 BC)

Daniel served in the royal court of Babylon and Persia, interpreting dreams and
receiving visions that outline the succession of world empires and the coming of
the Son of Man. His prophecies provide the backbone of biblical eschatology.
