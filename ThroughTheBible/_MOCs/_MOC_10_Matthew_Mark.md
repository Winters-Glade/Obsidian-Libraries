### Matthew's Gospel in the T2000 Series

- Jesus presented as **King and Messiah** — the promised Son of David
- Heavy emphasis on fulfillment of Old Testament prophecy
- Key topics: Kingdom of Heaven, Sermon on the Mount, discipleship, the Second Coming
- The T2000 series draws out practical, topical lessons from Matthew's narrative

### Mark's Gospel in the T2000 Series

- Action-oriented — Jesus as the **Servant** and **Son of God**
- Emphasis on faith, healing, and the compassion of Christ
- Key topics: faith in action, servant leadership, the cross, the cry of dereliction
- Mark's fast-paced narrative gives vivid portraits of Jesus' ministry
