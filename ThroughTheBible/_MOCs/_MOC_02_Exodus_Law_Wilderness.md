## Overview

This Map of Content connects all 22 study notes from Batch 2 of the T2000 Topical Series, covering the second through fifth books of the Pentateuch. These teachings explore God's redemptive acts (Exodus), His holy standards (Leviticus), His faithful provision in the wilderness (Numbers), and His covenant call to choose life (Deuteronomy).

## Cross-Cutting Themes

### God's Redemptive Character
- God sees, hears, and knows our suffering (video 14)
- God acts with sovereign power to redeem (video 15)
- God provides substitutionary atonement (video 16)
- God gives His law graciously (video 17)
- God works gradually for lasting victory (video 18)
- God responds to intercessory prayer (video 19)
- God's presence is non-negotiable (video 20)

### Worship and Holiness
- God must be worshiped on His terms (video 21)
- God provides cleansing and restoration (video 22)
- God's covenant word is absolutely sure (video 23)

### Blessing and Provision
- God blesses His people with shalom (video 24)
- God warns against ingratitude (video 25)
- God heals through the lifted-up substitute (video 26)
- God provides refuge from judgment (video 27)

### Covenant and Choice
- Prosperity brings the peril of forgetfulness (video 28)
- The choice of blessing or curse is real (video 29)
- God's revelation is sufficient (video 30)
- God's word is near — accessible to all (video 31)
- Love God with all your heart, soul, and might (video 32)
- Worship at the place where God puts His name (video 33)

### Leadership and Legacy
- Joshua commissioned: be strong and courageous (video 34)
- The Song of Moses: God is the Rock, perfect in all His ways (video 35)
