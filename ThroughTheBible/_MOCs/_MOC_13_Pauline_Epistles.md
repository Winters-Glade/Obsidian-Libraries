# _MOC_13 — Pauline Epistles

> T2000 Topical Series — Pastor Chuck Smith
> Batch 13: Romans through Philemon (44 teachings)

The Pauline Epistles form the core of New Testament theology —
justification by faith, the nature of the church, spiritual gifts, the
fruit of the Spirit, the armor of God, the inspiration of Scripture, and
the anticipation of Christ's return. These 13 letters (or 14 if Hebrews is
included) represent the bulk of the New Testament canon. In this batch,
Pastor Chuck Smith draws from Romans, 1-2 Corinthians, Galatians, Ephesians,
Philippians, Colossians, 1-2 Thessalonians, 1-2 Timothy, Titus, and Philemon.
