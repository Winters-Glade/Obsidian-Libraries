---

## About This Batch

These 23 teachings from the T2000 Topical Series cover three of Paul's most significant epistles. The series begins with the book of **Romans** (lessons #205–213), Paul's theological masterpiece on justification by faith, the sovereignty of God, and practical Christian living. It continues through **1 Corinthians** (lessons #214–221), addressing issues in the local church — divisions, spiritual gifts, love, and the resurrection. It concludes with **2 Corinthians** (lessons #222–227), Paul's most personal letter, dealing with suffering, ministry, and the sufficiency of God's grace.

Each teaching focuses on a specific passage and theme, offering topical insights into the theology and application of Paul's letters.
