## About This Batch

These 15 teachings from the T2000 Topical Series trace the narrative of Acts from the ascension of Christ (Acts 1:8) through Paul's final witness in Rome (Acts 28). The series follows the spread of the gospel from Jerusalem — to Samaria, to the Gentiles, to Europe, and finally to the imperial capital itself.

Each teaching focuses on a specific passage and theme, emphasizing the work of the Holy Spirit, the defense of the resurrection, and the unstoppable advance of the gospel through apostolic preaching, persecution, and Paul's journey to Rome.
