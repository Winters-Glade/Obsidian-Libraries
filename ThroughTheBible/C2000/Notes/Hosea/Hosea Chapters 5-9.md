## Summary

# Hosea — Chapters 5-9 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa So we begin tonight with chapter 5. In chapter 5, Hosea the prophet speaks for the Lord unto the priest and unto the princes of Israel, unto the house of the king. Hear ye this, O priest, hearken, you house of Israel, give ear, house of the king, for judgment is toward you, because you have been a snare on Mizpah and have spread a net upon Tabor. Now Tabor is a mountain that is very prominent in the area of the Galilee. It is easy to spot because it is a round domed mountain.
