## Summary

# Hosea — Chapters 10-14 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa So we turn to Hosea chapter 10. It is God's purpose for our lives that we bring forth fruit. In Isaiah chapter 6, God likens the nation of Israel to a vineyard that was planted with good plants, that was hedged about, but yet failed to bring forth any good fruit. And as the result, the vineyard was let go and ultimately destroyed. Now again, in Joel chapter 10, the Lord uses that allegory of a vineyard and of a fruitless vineyard to speak of the condition of the nation of Israel.
