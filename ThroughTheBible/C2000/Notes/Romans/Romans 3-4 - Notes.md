# C2000 Romans 3-4 - Notes

> See [[Romans 3-4 – Summary & Themes|Romans 3-4 – Summary & Themes]] for detailed study notes.

Romans 3-4 continues Paul's argument for justification by faith, showing that both Jews and Gentiles are justified by faith apart from the works of the Law. Abraham is presented as the prime example of faith being credited as righteousness.
