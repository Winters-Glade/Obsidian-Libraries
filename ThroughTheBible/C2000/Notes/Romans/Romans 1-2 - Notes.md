> See [[Romans 1-2 – Summary & Themes|Romans 1-2 – Summary & Themes]] for detailed study notes.

Paul's epistle to the Romans, written during his stay in Corinth, is the fullest exposition of the gospel of grace. Romans expands on justification by faith and the role of the Law, addressing both Jews and Gentiles.
