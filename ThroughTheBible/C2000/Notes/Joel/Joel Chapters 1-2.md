## Overview

Teaching from **Joel chapters 1-2** by Pastor Chuck Smith, part of the **C2000 Series** verse-by-verse exposition of the Bible.

## Summary

# Joel — Chapters 1-2 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa Shall we turn in our Bibles to the book of Joel? Joel is a prophet of which we have no light of his background, except what he gives to us. He is the son of Petheuel, but who Petheuel is we don't know, so it really doesn't help us that much. Joel was a prophet to Judah. That is, the southern kingdom. He probably was familiar with Elijah and no doubt was well acquainted with Elisha because the time of his prophecy pretty much corresponds to the time that Elisha was prophesying to Israel, the northern kingdom.
