## Summary

# Zechariah — Chapters 6-10 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa Zechariah chapter 6. Now as we pointed out, the Lord gave to Zechariah 10 visions and visions are subject to interpretation. Oftentimes the meaning of them is rather veiled and unless the Lord gives the interpretation, then speculation is really foolishness because everyone can have their own idea and all of them without real credibility when you turn to speculation or the interpretation of the scriptures. You hear people say, but there are so many interpretations. Yes, that is true.
