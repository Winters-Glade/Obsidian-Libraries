## Overview

Teaching from **Zechariah chapters 11-12** by Pastor Chuck Smith, part of the **C2000 Series** verse-by-verse exposition of the Bible.

## Summary

# Zechariah — Chapters 11-12 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa Tonight, we have a fascinating area of scripture to cover. Pray the Lord will give me the gift of brevity, for we are surely getting into some exciting areas of biblical prophecy as the Lord begins to speak of the events that will be transpiring in these last days. Some of them we see already in the beginning of their fulfillment. Others that will be fulfilled very shortly. But before we get into the events of these last days, chapter 11 deals with the first coming of Jesus Christ and his rejection and his being sold for 30 pieces of silver.
