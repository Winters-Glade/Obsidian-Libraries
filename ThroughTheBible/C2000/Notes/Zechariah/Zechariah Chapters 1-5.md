# Zechariah — Chapters 1-5

## Overview

Teaching from **Zechariah chapters 1-5** by Pastor Chuck Smith, part of the **C2000 Series** verse-by-verse exposition of the Bible.

## Summary

# Zechariah — Chapters 1-5 Pastor Chuck Smith C2000 Series Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa Shall we tonight now get into our study of the book of Zechariah, beginning with chapter 1. Now if you remember when we studied the book of Haggai last week, Haggai was dating his prophecies in the second year of Darius the king. And his first prophecy was in, I think, the sixth month of the second year. He had another prophecy in the seventh month, and his last prophecy was in the ninth month. Now Zechariah also dates his prophecies in the reign of Darius, the Medo-Persian king.
