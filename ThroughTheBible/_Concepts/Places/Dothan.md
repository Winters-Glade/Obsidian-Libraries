# Dothan

An ancient city in northern Samaria, approximately 10 miles north of Samaria city, best known as the place where Joseph was sold into slavery by his brothers (Genesis 37:17) and where the prophet Elisha was surrounded by the Syrian army (2 Kings 6:13-17). Excavations at Tell Dothan have revealed continuous occupation from the Chalcolithic period through the Iron Age, with fortifications matching the biblical accounts. It was at Dothan that Elisha's servant saw the mountain full of horses and chariots of fire.
