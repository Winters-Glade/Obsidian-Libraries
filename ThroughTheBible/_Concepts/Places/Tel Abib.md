# Tel Abib

The location in Mesopotamia (near the Chebar Canal) where the prophet Ezekiel lived among the Jewish exiles after the deportation of 597 BC (Ezekiel 1:1, 3:15). Tel Abib means 'mound of the flood' and was a settlement of exiles by the river Chebar, where Ezekiel received his inaugural vision and commission from God.
