# House of Israel

A biblical designation for the collective nation of Israel, often used in prophetic contexts to distinguish the Northern Kingdom from the House of Judah. The term appears frequently in the prophets when addressing the people as a covenant community, calling them to repentance, and pronouncing both judgment and future restoration.
