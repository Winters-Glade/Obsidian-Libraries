# Peniel/Penuel

## Description

Peniel/Penuel is referenced in the T2000 Topical Series by Pastor Chuck Smith. Peniel (also called Penuel) was the place where Jacob wrestled with God throughout the night. There God renamed him Israel, and Jacob declared, 'I have seen God face to face' (Genesis 32:22-32).
