# Canaan

## Description

Canaan is the land promised by God to Abraham and his descendants. It was inhabited by various Canaanite tribes before Israel's conquest under Joshua. The land flows with milk and honey and represents both a physical inheritance for Israel and a type of the spiritual rest and inheritance believers receive in Christ.
