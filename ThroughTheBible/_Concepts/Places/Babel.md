# Babel

The biblical city and tower associated with humanity's first organized rebellion after the Flood (Genesis 11:1-9), where God confused human language and scattered the people across the earth. Babel represents human pride seeking to make a name apart from God, and its judgment (confusion of languages) explains the origin of diverse peoples and languages.
