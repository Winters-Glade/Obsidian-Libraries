# Sodom and Gomorrah

## Description

Sodom and Gomorrah were cities destroyed by God with fire and brimstone for their grievous sin (Genesis 19). Their judgment became a proverbial warning throughout Scripture. Jesus referenced them in teaching about judgment. Abraham's intercession for Sodom reveals God's willingness to show mercy for the sake of the righteous.
