# The Praetorium

The official residence of the Roman governor (praetorium) in Jerusalem, likely the former palace of Herod the Great, where Jesus was brought before Pontius Pilate for trial (Mark 15:16, John 18:28, 19:9). This was the site of Pilate's judgment hall where soldiers mocked Jesus with a crown of thorns and purple robe before His crucifixion.
