# Mount Moriah (Temple Mount)

The mountain in Jerusalem where Abraham was commanded to sacrifice Isaac (Genesis 22:2), and where Solomon later built the Temple (2 Chronicles 3:1). Known today as the Temple Mount (Haram al-Sharif), it is the most contested religious site in the world, revered in Judaism, Christianity, and Islam. David purchased the threshing floor of Ornan here as the Temple site.
