# Galilee of the Gentiles

A designation for the region of Galilee in northern Israel that had a significant Gentile population, prophesied by Isaiah (Isaiah 9:1-2) as a place that would see a great light. Matthew 4:15-16 applies this prophecy to Jesus' ministry, emphasizing that the Messiah's work began in this region mixed with Jews and Gentiles, symbolizing the gospel reaching beyond Israel.
