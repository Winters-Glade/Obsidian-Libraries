# Mount Sinai (Horeb)

## Description

Mount Sinai (also called Horeb) is the mountain where God gave the Law to Moses amidst thunder, lightning, and earthquake. It is also where God appeared to Moses in the burning bush. The covenant established at Sinai defined Israel's relationship with God. Mount Sinai represents the law, in contrast to Mount Zion representing grace in the New Testament.
