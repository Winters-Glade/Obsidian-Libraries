# Antioch

## Description

Antioch of Syria was the third-largest city in the Roman Empire and became a major center of early Christianity. It was where believers were first called Christians (Acts 11:26). The church in Antioch sent Paul and Barnabas on their missionary journeys. It served as a bridge between Jewish and Gentile Christianity.
