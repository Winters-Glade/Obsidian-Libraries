# The Road to Emmaus

The seven-mile journey from Jerusalem to the village of Emmaus where the resurrected Jesus appeared incognito to two disciples, explaining the Scriptures concerning Himself (Luke 24:13-35). Their hearts burned as He opened the Scriptures, and they recognized Him in the breaking of bread. The exact location of Emmaus is debated among scholars.
