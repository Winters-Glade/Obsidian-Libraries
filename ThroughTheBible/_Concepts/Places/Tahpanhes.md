# Tahpanhes

An ancient Egyptian city in the eastern Nile Delta (Tel Defenneh), identified as the place where the Jewish refugees led by Johanan fled after the assassination of Gedaliah, taking the prophet Jeremiah with them against God's warning (Jeremiah 43:7-9). Jeremiah buried large stones at Tahpanhes as a sign that Nebuchadnezzar would spread his royal canopy there.
