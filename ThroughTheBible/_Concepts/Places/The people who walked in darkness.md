# The people who walked in darkness

The people described by Isaiah (Isaiah 9:2) who saw a great light—a messianic prophecy applied by Matthew to Jesus' ministry in Galilee (Matthew 4:16). This phrase describes those living in spiritual darkness, oppression, and hopelessness who encountered the light of Christ. It represents the condition of humanity before the gospel dawns.
