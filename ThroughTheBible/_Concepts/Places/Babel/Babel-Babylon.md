# Babel/Babylon

## Description

Babel/Babylon is referenced in the T2000 Topical Series by Pastor Chuck Smith. Babel (later Babylon) was an ancient city in Mesopotamia where humanity attempted to build a tower to heaven. God confused their languages, scattering them across the earth (Genesis 11).
