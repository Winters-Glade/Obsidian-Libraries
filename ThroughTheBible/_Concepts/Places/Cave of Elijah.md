# Cave of Elijah

The cave on Mount Horeb where the prophet Elijah took refuge after fleeing from Queen Jezebel's threat, following his victory over the prophets of Baal on Mount Carmel (1 Kings 19:9-18). In this cave, God spoke to Elijah not in the wind, earthquake, or fire, but in a 'still small voice,' teaching that His power often works through quiet faithfulness.
