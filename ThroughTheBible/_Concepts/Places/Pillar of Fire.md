# Pillar of Fire

The supernatural manifestation of God's presence that led Israel through the wilderness by night, accompanying the pillar of cloud by day (Exodus 13:21-22). The pillar of fire provided light and guidance while also shielding Israel from the Egyptians at the Red Sea. It represents God's visible guidance and protective presence with His people.
