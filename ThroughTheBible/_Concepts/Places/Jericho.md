# Jericho

## Description

Jericho is one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world. In the Old Testament, its walls fell after the Israelites marched around them for seven days (Joshua 6). In the New Testament, Jesus passed through Jericho, healing blind Bartimaeus and dining with Zacchaeus the tax collector.
