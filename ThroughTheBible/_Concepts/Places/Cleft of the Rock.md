# Cleft of the Rock

The place on Mount Sinai where God placed Moses in the cleft of a rock and covered him with His hand as His glory passed by (Exodus 33:21-23). This theophany showed Moses God's goodness and proclaimed His name and character. The 'Rock' is a metaphor for God as a refuge and hiding place for His people.
