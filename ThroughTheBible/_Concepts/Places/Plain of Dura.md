# Plain of Dura

The location in the province of Babylon where King Nebuchadnezzar erected a massive golden image, approximately 90 feet high and 9 feet wide, commanding all peoples to worship it (Daniel 3:1). This plain near Babylon was the setting of the famous test of faith where Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego refused to bow.
