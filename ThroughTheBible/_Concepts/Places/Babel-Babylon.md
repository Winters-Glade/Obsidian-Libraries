# Babel/Babylon

## Description

Babel/Babylon represents both the historical city and the spiritual system of human pride, idolatry, and rebellion against God. Throughout Scripture, Babylon stands in contrast to Jerusalem — the city of man versus the city of God. Chuck Smith traces Babylon from the Tower of Babel through the Babylonian Empire's destruction of Jerusalem to its symbolic role in Revelation as the embodiment of worldly power opposed to God. The ultimate triumph belongs to the New Jerusalem.
