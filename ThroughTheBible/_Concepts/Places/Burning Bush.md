# Burning Bush

The bush on Mount Horeb (Sinai) that burned with fire but was not consumed, through which God called Moses to deliver Israel from Egypt (Exodus 3:1-15). This theophany revealed God's holiness, His covenant name Yahweh ('I AM WHO I AM'), and His compassion for His oppressed people. It marks the beginning of the Exodus story.
