# Nazareth

## Description

Nazareth was the hometown of Jesus in Galilee. It was a small, insignificant village, prompting Nathanael's question: 'Can anything good come out of Nazareth?' (John 1:46). Jesus grew up there and was rejected by His hometown synagogue after reading from Isaiah in a dramatic declaration of His messianic mission.
