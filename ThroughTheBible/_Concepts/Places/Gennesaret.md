# Gennesaret

A fertile plain on the northwestern shore of the Sea of Galilee, sometimes called the 'Garden of Galilee' for its rich soil and abundant agriculture. Jesus landed here after walking on water (Matthew 14:34, Mark 6:53), and the people immediately recognized Him and brought the sick to be healed. The plain gives its name to the Sea of Gennesaret (Luke 5:1).
