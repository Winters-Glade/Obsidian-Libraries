# Lamb's Victory

The ultimate triumph of Jesus Christ, the Lamb of God, over all opposing powers as depicted in Revelation 17:14. The Lamb shall overcome His enemies because He is Lord of lords and King of kings, and those who are with Him are called, chosen, and faithful. This victory is not achieved through worldly force but through the Lamb's sacrificial death and resurrection.
