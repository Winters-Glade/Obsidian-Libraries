# Agagite

The designation of Haman in the book of Esther (Esther 3:1, 10, 8:3, 5), likely referring to his descent from Agag, the king of the Amalekites whom King Saul failed to destroy (1 Samuel 15). This connection links Haman to the ancient enmity between Israel and Amalek. The term may also indicate Haman was a descendant of the Amalekite royal line.
