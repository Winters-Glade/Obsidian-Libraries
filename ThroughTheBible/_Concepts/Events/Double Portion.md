# Double Portion

The request of Elisha to inherit a double portion of Elijah's spirit as his successor (2 Kings 2:9), referring to the inheritance right of the firstborn son under Jewish law. Elisha was not asking for twice the power but to be recognized as Elijah's true spiritual heir. This request was granted as Elisha performed twice as many recorded miracles as Elijah, demonstrating God's abundant spiritual enabling.
