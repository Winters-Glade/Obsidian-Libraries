# Head of the Church

The exalted position of Christ as the supreme authority over the church, which is described as His body (Colossians 1:18, Ephesians 1:22-23, 5:23). As head, Christ gives direction, life, and unity to the body. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that no human leader, denomination, or institution stands above Christ's authority in the church.
