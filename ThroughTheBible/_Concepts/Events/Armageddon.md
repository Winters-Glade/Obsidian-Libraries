# Armageddon

The final battlefield where the forces of the world gather for war against God at the end of the age (Revelation 16:16). The term derives from Har-Magedon (Mount Megiddo), a strategic valley in northern Israel where many historic battles were fought. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that Armageddon represents the climactic confrontation where Christ returns to defeat the gathered armies and establish His millennial kingdom.
