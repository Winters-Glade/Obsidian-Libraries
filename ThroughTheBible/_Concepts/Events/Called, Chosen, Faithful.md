# Called, Chosen, Faithful

The description of those who accompany the Lamb in His final victory (Revelation 17:14). These believers have been called by the gospel, chosen by God before the foundation of the world (Ephesians 1:4), and proved faithful through perseverance. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that while many are called, the faithful are those who endure to the end by trusting in Christ's keeping power.
