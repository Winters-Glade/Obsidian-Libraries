# Final Conflict

The climactic battle between the forces of good and evil at the end of the age, culminating in Christ's return to defeat His enemies (Revelation 16:14-16, 19:11-21, 20:7-10). The Beast and the False Prophet are thrown into the lake of fire, and Satan is bound for a thousand years. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that believers should live with expectant hope, knowing the outcome is already determined.
