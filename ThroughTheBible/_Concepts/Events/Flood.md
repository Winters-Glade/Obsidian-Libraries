# The Flood

## Description

The Flood was God's judgment on a corrupt and violent world, sparing only Noah and his family (eight people) through the ark (Genesis 6-9). After the flood, God established the Noahic covenant with the rainbow as its sign. Jesus referenced the flood as a type of His second coming (Matthew 24:37-39).
