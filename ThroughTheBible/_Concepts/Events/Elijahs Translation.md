# Elijah's Translation

The supernatural taking up of the prophet Elijah into heaven by a whirlwind, accompanied by a chariot of fire and horses of fire (2 Kings 2:1-12). Elijah did not experience death but was translated directly into God's presence, prefiguring the rapture of believers. Elisha witnessed this event and received Elijah's mantle, symbolizing the transfer of prophetic authority.
