# Jacob/Israel

The patriarch Jacob, son of Isaac and grandson of Abraham, whose name was changed to Israel after wrestling with God at Peniel (Genesis 32:22-32). As 'Jacob' he was the supplanter; as 'Israel' he became the father of the twelve tribes. Pastor Chuck Smith traced God's transforming work in Jacob's life—from schemer to prince with God—as a pattern of how God shapes character through trials and encounters with Himself.
