# Empowerment for Service

The enabling of believers by the Holy Spirit for effective ministry and witness (Acts 1:8, 6:8). In the early church, empowerment came through the baptism of the Holy Spirit, transforming ordinary people into bold witnesses. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that the same empowerment is available to believers today for carrying out the Great Commission.
