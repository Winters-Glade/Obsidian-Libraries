# The Exodus

## Description

The Exodus is the foundational event of Israel's history: God's deliverance of His people from slavery in Egypt through Moses. It included the ten plagues, the Passover, the crossing of the Red Sea, and the journey to Mount Sinai. The Exodus became the defining symbol of God's redemption and is referenced throughout Scripture as a type of salvation in Christ.
