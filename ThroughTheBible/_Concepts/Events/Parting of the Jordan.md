# Parting of the Jordan

The miracle in which Elijah struck the Jordan River with his mantle and the waters parted (2 Kings 2:8), and later Elisha performed the same miracle after receiving Elijah's mantle (2 Kings 2:14). This event testified that Elisha had indeed received Elijah's spirit and prophetic authority, evoking the earlier parting of the Red Sea and Jordan under Moses and Joshua.
