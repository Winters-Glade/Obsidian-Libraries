# The Beast

The apocalyptic figure in Daniel 7 and Revelation 13, a world ruler empowered by Satan who will lead a global rebellion against God in the last days. The Beast rises from the sea with ten horns and seven heads, receiving worship from those whose names are not written in the Lamb's Book of Life. He will make war with the saints but will ultimately be defeated by Christ at His coming.
