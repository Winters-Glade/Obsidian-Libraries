# Ascension

## Description

The Ascension of Jesus Christ occurred 40 days after His resurrection when He was taken up into heaven from the Mount of Olives. The Ascension marked the end of His earthly ministry and the beginning of His heavenly intercession at the right hand of the Father. It also preceded the outpouring of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost.
