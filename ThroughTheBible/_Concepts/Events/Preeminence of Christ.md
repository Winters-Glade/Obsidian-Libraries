# Preeminence of Christ

The doctrine that Christ holds first place in all things—in creation, in the church, and in redemption (Colossians 1:18). He is before all things and by Him all things consist. The goal of Paul's theology in Colossians is to demonstrate that Christ is sufficient and supreme over all competing powers and philosophies.
