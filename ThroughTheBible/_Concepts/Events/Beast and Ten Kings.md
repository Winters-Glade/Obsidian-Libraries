# Beast and Ten Kings

The prophetic imagery from Daniel 7 and Revelation 13, 17 describing a future world ruler (the Beast) who will lead a coalition of ten kings in rebellion against God. They will wage war against the Lamb but will be defeated. This eschatological scenario depicts the final political alignment before Christ's return, culminating in the destruction of anti-God power.
