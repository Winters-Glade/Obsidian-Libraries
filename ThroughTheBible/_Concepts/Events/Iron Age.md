# Iron Age

The archaeological period (c. 1200-586 BC in Israel) characterized by the widespread use of iron for tools and weapons. In biblical history, the Iron Age spans the period from the settlement of Canaan through the divided monarchy to the Babylonian exile. The Philistines' iron chariots and weapons gave them a military advantage over Israel (Joshua 17:16, Judges 1:19). The fortified city of Dothan, excavated by archaeologists, dates to this period.
