# Scattering

## Description

The Scattering refers to the dispersion of humanity from Babel after God confused their languages. It also describes God's judgment of scattering His people when they were unfaithful (the Diaspora). Chuck Smith teaches that while scattering can be a judgment, God also uses dispersion to spread His truth — as with the early church scattered from Jerusalem, who preached the gospel wherever they went.
