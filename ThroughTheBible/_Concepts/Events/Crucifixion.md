# Crucifixion

## Description

The Crucifixion of Jesus Christ is the central event of redemptive history. Jesus was crucified at Golgotha (Calvary) outside Jerusalem under Pontius Pilate. His death fulfilled Old Testament prophecy (Psalm 22, Isaiah 53) and provided atonement for sin. The Crucifixion demonstrates both God's justice and His love, reconciling sinners to Himself.
