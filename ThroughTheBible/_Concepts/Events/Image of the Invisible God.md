# Image of the Invisible God

The description of Jesus Christ as the perfect representation and revelation of the invisible God (Colossians 1:15). As the image of God, Christ is not merely a reflection but the exact expression of God's being (Hebrews 1:3). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that to see Jesus is to see the Father—He is the visible manifestation of the invisible God.
