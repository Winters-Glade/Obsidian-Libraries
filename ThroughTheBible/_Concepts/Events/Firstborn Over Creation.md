# Firstborn Over Creation

The title of Christ from Colossians 1:15, indicating His preeminence and priority over all creation, not His being the first created being. As the 'firstborn,' Christ holds the rights and status of the heir over all creation. This affirms His deity and His role as the agent through whom all things were created (Colossians 1:16).
