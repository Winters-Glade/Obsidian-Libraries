# Jerusalem-Judea-Samaria-Uttmost

The geographical progression of the gospel commission given by Jesus in Acts 1:8, outlining the expansion of Christian witness from Jerusalem outward to Judea, Samaria, and ultimately to the ends of the earth. This structure also serves as the outline for the book of Acts, documenting the church's missionary expansion.
