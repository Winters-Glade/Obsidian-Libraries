# Burning Bush

## Description

The Burning Bush was the theophany (divine appearance) where God revealed Himself to Moses in a flame of fire from within a bush that burned but was not consumed. At Mount Horeb (Sinai), God called Moses to deliver Israel from Egypt, revealing His name as 'I AM WHO I AM.' Chuck Smith highlights this encounter as a profound revelation of God's nature — He is the self-existent, eternal God who is present with His people in their affliction and who calls ordinary people to extraordinary missions through His empowering presence.
