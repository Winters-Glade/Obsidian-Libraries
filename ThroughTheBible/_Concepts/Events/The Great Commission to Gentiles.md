# The Great Commission to Gentiles

## Description

The Great Commission to Gentiles refers to the expansion of the gospel beyond Jewish boundaries to the Gentile world, particularly through the ministry of Paul and the early church in Antioch. While the risen Christ gave the Great Commission to all nations (Matthew 28:18-20), the specific breakthrough came through Peter's vision at Joppa, the conversion of Cornelius, and Paul's missionary journeys. Chuck Smith emphasizes this as the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecies that the nations would be blessed through Abraham's seed.
