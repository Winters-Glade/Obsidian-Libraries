# Pentecost

## Description

Pentecost was the Jewish feast of weeks celebrated 50 days after Passover. On the first Christian Pentecost (Acts 2), the Holy Spirit was poured out on the disciples with tongues of fire and speaking in tongues. Peter's sermon led to the conversion of 3,000 people, marking the birth of the Church. Pentecost fulfilled Joel's prophecy (Joel 2:28-32).
