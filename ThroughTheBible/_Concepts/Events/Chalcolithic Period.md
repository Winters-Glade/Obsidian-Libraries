# Chalcolithic Period

The Copper-Stone Age archaeological period (c. 4500-3500 BC in the Levant), transitional between the Stone Age and the Bronze Age. In the context of biblical archaeology, sites like Tell Dothan and others in Israel show evidence of Chalcolithic occupation, providing background for the earliest biblical patriarchs.
