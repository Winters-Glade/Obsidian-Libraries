# The Ten Kings

The ten rulers or kingdoms that will ally with the Beast in the final world empire before Christ's return, described in Daniel 7:7, 24 and Revelation 17:12-14. They receive authority as kings for one hour and are of one mind to give their power to the Beast. However, they will make war with the Lamb, and the Lamb will overcome them.
