# Lord of Lords

The exalted title of Jesus Christ as the supreme ruler over all earthly and heavenly powers (Revelation 17:14, 19:16, 1 Timothy 6:15). This title emphasizes Christ's absolute sovereignty over every human authority and spiritual power. At His return, Christ will demonstrate this Lordship by defeating all opposing forces and establishing His kingdom.
