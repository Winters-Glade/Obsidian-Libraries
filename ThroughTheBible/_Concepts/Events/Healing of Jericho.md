# Healing of Jericho

The miracle of Elisha purifying the water supply of Jericho by casting salt into the spring at the word of the Lord (2 Kings 2:19-22). Though the land was pleasant, the water was bad and caused miscarriages. Elisha's healing of the water symbolizes God's power to bring life and blessing from barrenness and curse.
