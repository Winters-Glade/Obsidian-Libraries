# Confusion of Languages

## Description

The Confusion of Languages was the divine intervention at the Tower of Babel where God confused the speech of humanity, causing them to speak different languages and scattering them across the earth. This event ended the united, rebellious effort of humanity to build a tower to the heavens and make a name for themselves apart from God. Chuck Smith uses this to demonstrate God's sovereignty over human pride and ambition, and how God's judgment at Babel ultimately served His redemptive purpose of filling the earth.
