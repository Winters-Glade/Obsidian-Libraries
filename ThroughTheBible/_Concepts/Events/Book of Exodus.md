# Book of Exodus

The second book of the Torah, recording Israel's deliverance from Egyptian slavery, the giving of the Law at Mount Sinai, and the construction of the Tabernacle. Key events include the ten plagues, the Passover, the crossing of the Red Sea, the Ten Commandments, and the golden calf incident. Pastor Chuck Smith taught Exodus as a foundational picture of redemption.
