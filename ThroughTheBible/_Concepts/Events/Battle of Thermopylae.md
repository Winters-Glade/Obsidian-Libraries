# Battle of Thermopylae

The famous battle in 480 BC where a small Greek force, led by King Leonidas of Sparta, held off the massive invading Persian army of Xerxes I (Ahasuerus) at a narrow mountain pass. Though ultimately a Persian victory, it became a symbol of courage against overwhelming odds. This battle provides historical context for understanding the Persian military might during the time of Esther and Ezra.
