# Christ as Creator

The biblical doctrine that Jesus Christ was the divine agent through whom God created all things (John 1:3, Colossians 1:16, Hebrews 1:2). All things in heaven and earth, visible and invisible, were created by Him and for Him. This truth establishes Christ's supreme authority over creation and His right to redeem and restore it.
