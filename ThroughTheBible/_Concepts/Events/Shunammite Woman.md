# Shunammite Woman

## Description

The Shunammite Woman refers to the miracle where Elisha raised the son of a prominent woman from Shunem. She had shown hospitality to Elisha, building a room for him in her home, and Elisha promised she would bear a son. When the boy later died suddenly, Elisha came and raised him back to life. Chuck Smith uses this account to illustrate the blessings of hospitality, God's faithfulness to those who serve His servants, and His power over death.
