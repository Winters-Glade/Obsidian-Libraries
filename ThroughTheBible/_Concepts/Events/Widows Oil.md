# Widow's Oil

The miracle where Elisha multiplied a widow's single jar of oil to fill many vessels, enabling her to pay her debts and live on the remainder (2 Kings 4:1-7). The prophet instructed her to borrow empty vessels from neighbors and pour—the oil stopped only when the vessels ran out. This miracle demonstrates God's provision for the faithful and His care for the vulnerable.
