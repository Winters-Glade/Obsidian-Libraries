# Battle of Salamis

The decisive naval battle in 480 BC where the Greek fleet defeated the Persian navy under Xerxes I (Ahasuerus) in the straits of Salamis. This battle turned the tide of the Persian Wars and limited Persian expansion into Europe. It provides historical context for the reign of Ahasuerus (Xerxes) during the time of Esther, showing the challenges he faced.
