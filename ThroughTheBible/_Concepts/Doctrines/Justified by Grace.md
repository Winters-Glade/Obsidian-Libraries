# Justified by Grace

The legal declaration that a sinner is righteous before God solely through faith in Jesus Christ, made possible by God's unmerited favor (Romans 3:24, Titus 3:7). Justification is not earned by good works but received through faith, as Christ's righteousness is imputed to the believer. This doctrine, central to the Reformation, assures believers of their standing before God.
