# Worship

## Description

Worship is the act of ascribing worth to God through praise, prayer, song, and obedient living. Both the Old Testament (with its system of sacrifice, tabernacle, and temple worship) and the New Testament (with worship in spirit and truth) emphasize that worship is central to the believer's relationship with God. Jesus taught that true worshipers worship the Father in spirit and truth (John 4:23-24).
