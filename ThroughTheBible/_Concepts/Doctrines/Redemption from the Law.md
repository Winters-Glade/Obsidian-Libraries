# Redemption from the Law

The liberation of believers from the condemnation and requirements of the Mosaic Law through Christ's sacrificial death (Galatians 4:4-5, Romans 7:1-6). Christ was born under the Law to redeem those under the Law, not to abolish it but to fulfill its righteous requirements on behalf of sinners. Pastor Chuck Smith taught this freedom enables believers to serve God in newness of Spirit, not in oldness of letter.
