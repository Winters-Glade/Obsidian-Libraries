# Reproach of Christ

The disgrace, rejection, and suffering that believers experience for identifying with Christ, considered by Moses as greater wealth than all of Egypt's treasures (Hebrews 11:26). To bear Christ's reproach is to share in His sufferings and rejection by the world. Chuck Smith taught that this reproach is actually a mark of honor and identification with the Suffering Servant.
