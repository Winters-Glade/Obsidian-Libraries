# God's sovereignty

The supreme authority and power of God over all creation, history, and human affairs. He reigns as King over everything and nothing happens outside His will and purposes (Psalm 103:19, Daniel 4:34-35). Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that God's sovereignty is a source of comfort—our lives are in the hands of a loving and all-powerful Father who works all things for good.
