# Christ Came to Save Sinners

The foundational gospel declaration Paul called 'a faithful saying, worthy of all acceptation' (1 Timothy 1:15). This statement defines the very purpose of Christ's incarnation—to save sinners, not the righteous. Paul placed himself first among sinners, demonstrating that the worst offender can receive mercy. Pastor Chuck Smith often emphasized this as the heart of the gospel.
