# The Gentiles

A term used in Scripture for all nations other than Israel. The Old Testament anticipated Gentiles coming to faith (Isaiah 49:6), and the New Testament records the dramatic expansion of the gospel to the Gentile world through Peter's ministry to Cornelius and Paul's missionary journeys. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that the inclusion of Gentiles fulfills God's promise to Abraham that all nations would be blessed through him.
