# Brother Beloved

Paul's affectionate designation for Onesimus in his letter to Philemon (Philemon 1:16), describing the transformed relationship between a slave and his master through the gospel. Onesimus, once a runaway slave and 'unprofitable' servant, became a beloved brother in Christ. This illustrates the gospel's power to transform all human relationships, breaking down barriers of social status.
