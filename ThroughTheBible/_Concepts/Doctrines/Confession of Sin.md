# Confession of Sin

The practice of acknowledging one's sins to God, agreeing with His assessment of wrongdoing, and receiving His forgiveness (1 John 1:9). Confession is not about informing God of what He doesn't know but about humbly admitting the truth. God's promise is that if we confess our sins, He is faithful and just to forgive us and cleanse us from all unrighteousness.
