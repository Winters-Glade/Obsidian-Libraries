# Paul's House Arrest in Rome

The two-year period (c. 60-62 AD) during which Paul was under house arrest in Rome, chained to a Roman soldier, yet continued preaching the gospel and writing his Prison Epistles (Ephesians, Philippians, Colossians, Philemon) (Acts 28:16-31). Despite his chains, the Word of God was not bound, and Paul's imprisonment advanced the gospel throughout the entire Praetorian guard.
