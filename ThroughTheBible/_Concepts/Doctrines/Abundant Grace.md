# Abundant Grace

The superabundant, overflowing grace of God that superabounds beyond even the worst of sin (Romans 5:20, 1 Timothy 1:14). Where sin increased, grace abounded all the more. Paul saw himself as the supreme demonstration of Christ's patience and abundant grace—if God could save the chief of sinners, He can save anyone.
