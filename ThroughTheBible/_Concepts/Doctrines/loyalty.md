# loyalty

## Description

Loyalty in Scripture is faithful commitment to God and His people, demonstrated through consistent devotion even in difficult circumstances. Ruth's loyalty to Naomi is the classic example. Chuck Smith teaches that loyalty to God is the foundation of all other loyalties and that it must be tested to be proven genuine. True loyalty persists through trials and remains steadfast when easier paths present themselves.
