# Model of Conversion

The pattern of genuine conversion exemplified by the Thessalonian believers: they turned to God from idols to serve the living and true God and to wait for His Son from heaven (1 Thessalonians 1:9-10). This threefold pattern—repentance, service, and expectation—serves as a benchmark for authentic faith. Pastor Chuck Smith held this as a template for what genuine conversion looks like.
