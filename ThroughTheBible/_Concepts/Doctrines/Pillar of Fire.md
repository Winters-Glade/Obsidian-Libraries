# Pillar of Fire

## Description

The Pillar of Fire was the visible manifestation of God's presence that guided Israel through the wilderness by night. Along with the Pillar of Cloud by day, it led the nation, provided light, and protected them from the Egyptians at the Red Sea. Chuck Smith teaches that the Pillar of Fire demonstrates God's desire to lead His people visibly and personally — He does not merely give directions but accompanies His people as their guide and protector.
