# Temple

## Definition

The sacred building in Jerusalem where God's presence dwelt among His people. The First Temple was built by Solomon; the Second Temple was rebuilt after the Exile. In the New Testament, believers are described as the temple of the Holy Spirit, and Christ is the true temple.
