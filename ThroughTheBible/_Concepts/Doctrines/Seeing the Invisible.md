# Seeing the Invisible

The spiritual perception that enabled Moses and the heroes of faith to endure by seeing Him who is invisible (Hebrews 11:27). This is not physical sight but the eyes of faith that discern spiritual realities beyond the material world. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that believers develop this sight through meditating on God's Word, prayer, and the Holy Spirit's illumination.
