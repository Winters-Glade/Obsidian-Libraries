# The Ten Kings

## Description

The Ten Kings are the rulers described in Revelation 17 who give their power and authority to the Beast. They represent a confederation of nations or rulers who unite under the Antichrist's leadership in the end times. Chuck Smith teaches that while the specific identities of these kings remain future, the pattern of nations uniting against God is a recurring theme in biblical prophecy.
