# The Unstoppable Word

The theme from Acts 6:7 and throughout Acts that the gospel of Jesus Christ spreads irresistibly despite opposition, persecution, and human limitations. As Pastor Chuck Smith taught, 'the Word of God is not bound' (2 Timothy 2:9)—it advances through prison, persecution, and resistance, demonstrating that God's purposes cannot be thwarted by human authorities.
