# Immanuel

The prophetic name meaning 'God with us,' given in Isaiah's prophecy of the virgin birth (Isaiah 7:14) and fulfilled in the birth of Jesus Christ (Matthew 1:22-23). This title declares the incarnation—God taking on human flesh to dwell among His people. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that Immanuel is not merely a theological concept but the reality of God's personal presence with His people through Jesus Christ, the fulfillment of God's promise to never leave nor forsake His own.
