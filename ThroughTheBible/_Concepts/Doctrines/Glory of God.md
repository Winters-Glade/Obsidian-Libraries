# Glory of God

## Definition

The manifest presence, weight, and splendor of God's attributes. God's glory is the visible expression of His holiness, power, love, and majesty. It was seen in the pillar of cloud and fire, the Tabernacle, the Temple, and most fully in Jesus Christ. Believers are called to glorify God.
