# Covenant Renewal

## Definition

The periodic reaffirmation of the covenant relationship between God and His people. In the Old Testament, this occurred at significant moments (Joshua 24, Josiah's reform, Nehemiah 8-10). It involved recommitment to obedience, worship, and the terms of God's covenant.
