# Heaven

## Definition

The dwelling place of God, the realm of His perfect presence, and the eternal home of the redeemed. Heaven is described as a place of joy, worship, rest, and the absence of sin, suffering, and death. The New Jerusalem is the ultimate expression of heaven coming to earth.
