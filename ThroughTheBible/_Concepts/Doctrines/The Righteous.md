# The Righteous

Those who are declared righteous by faith in God and live in accordance with His will. In the Old Testament, the righteous are characterized by trust in God, obedience to His law, and care for the poor and oppressed. In the New Testament, righteousness is first imputed through faith in Christ and then manifested in a life of holiness. The righteous live by faith (Habakkuk 2:4).
