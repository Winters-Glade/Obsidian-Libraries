# Hope

## Description

Biblical hope is not mere wishful thinking but confident expectation based on God's promises. It is an anchor for the soul (Hebrews 6:19), grounded in the resurrection of Christ and the certainty of His return. Hope produces endurance in suffering and purifies the believer's life (1 John 3:3).
