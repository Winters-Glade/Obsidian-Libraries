# Covenant Faithfulness

## Description

Covenant faithfulness (Hebrew: *hesed*) refers to God's steadfast, loyal love and commitment to His covenant promises. It describes both God's unwavering fidelity to His people and the reciprocal loyalty He requires from them. This theme permeates the Old Testament, especially in the prophetic books where Israel's unfaithfulness is contrasted with God's enduring covenant love.
