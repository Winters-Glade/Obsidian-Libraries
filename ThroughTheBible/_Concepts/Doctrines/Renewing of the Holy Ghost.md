# Renewing of the Holy Ghost

The ongoing work of the Holy Spirit who transforms believers from within, renewing their minds and conforming them to Christ's image (Titus 3:5, Romans 12:2). This renewal is not a one-time event but a continuous process where old patterns of thinking are replaced with God's truth. It is the Spirit's work of making believers new creations in Christ.
