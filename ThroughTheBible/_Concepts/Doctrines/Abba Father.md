# Abba Father

The intimate Aramaic term for father that Jesus used in His prayers and that believers are empowered to use through the Holy Spirit (Mark 14:36, Romans 8:15, Galatians 4:6). 'Abba' conveys the affectionate closeness of a child addressing a loving parent, yet retains the reverence due to God as Father. This term expresses the heart of the believer's relationship with God through adoption.
