# Giving

## Description

Giving in Scripture refers to the cheerful, generous, and sacrificial offering of resources to God and others. Chuck Smith teaches that giving is an act of worship reflecting God's own generous nature. He emphasizes the principle that God loves a cheerful giver (2 Corinthians 9:7) and that believers should give proportionately, systematically, and joyfully—not under compulsion but as an expression of gratitude for God's abundant provision. Giving also encompasses the offering of one's time, talents, and self in service to God.
