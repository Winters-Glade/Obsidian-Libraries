# offering

## Description

Offering in Scripture is the presentation of gifts to God as an act of worship, gratitude, and atonement. The Old Testament system of offerings (burnt, grain, peace, sin, trespass) pointed to the ultimate sacrifice of Christ. Chuck Smith teaches that while the Old Testament offerings are no longer required, believers are called to present their bodies as living sacrifices (Romans 12:1) — the spiritual worship of surrendered lives.
