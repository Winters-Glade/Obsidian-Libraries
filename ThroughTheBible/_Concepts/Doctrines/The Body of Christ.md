# The Body of Christ

## Definition

A Pauline metaphor for the church, describing believers as members of one spiritual body united under Christ as the head. Each member has distinct gifts and functions, all necessary for the body's health and mission. The term emphasizes unity, diversity, interdependence, and the organic connection between Christ and His people.
