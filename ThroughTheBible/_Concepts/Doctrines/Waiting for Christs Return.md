# Waiting for Christ's Return

The eager anticipation of Jesus Christ's second coming that characterized the early Thessalonian church (1 Thessalonians 1:10). Along with turning from idols and serving God, waiting for God's Son from heaven was a hallmark of authentic conversion. This expectant hope shaped the believers' lives, giving them endurance in persecution and motivation for holy living.
