# The Church

## Definition

The assembled community of believers in Jesus Christ, also called the Body of Christ and the Bride of Christ. The church is both universal (all believers across all time) and local (specific congregations). Its purposes include worship, discipleship, fellowship, ministry, and evangelism. Christ is its head and foundation.
