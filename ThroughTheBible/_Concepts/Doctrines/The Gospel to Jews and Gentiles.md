# The Gospel to Jews and Gentiles

The unfolding revelation that the good news of Jesus Christ is for all people regardless of ethnic background. Paul's missionary strategy was 'to the Jew first and also to the Greek' (Romans 1:16). The early church wrestled with this inclusion at the Jerusalem Council (Acts 15), ultimately affirming that salvation is through grace alone for both Jews and Gentiles.
