# Sanctification

## Definition

The process by which believers are made holy, set apart for God's purposes. It has three aspects: positional (set apart at conversion), progressive (growing in holiness through the Spirit's work), and ultimate (final perfection at glorification). Sanctification involves both God's work and the believer's active cooperation.
