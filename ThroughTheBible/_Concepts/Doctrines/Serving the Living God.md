# Serving the Living God

The new orientation of life that results from genuine conversion—believers turn from idols to serve the living and true God (1 Thessalonians 1:9). This service is not merely external but proceeds from a transformed heart, empowered by the Holy Spirit. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that genuine faith always produces active, devoted service to God.
