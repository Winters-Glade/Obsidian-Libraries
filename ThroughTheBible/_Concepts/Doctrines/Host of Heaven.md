# Host of Heaven

The organized multitude of angelic beings who serve God and carry out His commands. The term appears in both Old and New Testaments, describing the heavenly armies that worship God, deliver messages, protect believers, and execute divine judgment. Elisha's vision at Dothan revealed the host of heaven as chariots of fire—a tangible manifestation of God's protective power.
