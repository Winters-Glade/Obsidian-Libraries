# Restoration

## Definition

The divine work of bringing back what was lost, broken, or exiled into its intended state. In Scripture, restoration applies to individuals (repentance and renewal), Israel (return from exile), and all creation (the final renewal). God is the ultimate restorer who makes all things new.
