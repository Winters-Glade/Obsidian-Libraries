# Redemption

## Description

Redemption is the act of buying back or setting free by payment of a price. In the Old Testament, it referred to the liberation of slaves or property. Theologically, it describes Christ's work of purchasing believers' freedom from sin and death through His blood. Key passages include Mark 10:45, Ephesians 1:7, and 1 Peter 1:18-19.
