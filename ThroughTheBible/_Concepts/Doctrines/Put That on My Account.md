# Put That on My Account

Paul's offer to Philemon regarding Onesimus's debts or wrongs (Philemon 1:18), which serves as a beautiful picture of Christ's substitutionary atonement. Just as Paul offered to personally cover Onesimus's debts, Christ takes our spiritual debts upon Himself. This phrase embodies the essence of the gospel—one person assuming the liability of another.
