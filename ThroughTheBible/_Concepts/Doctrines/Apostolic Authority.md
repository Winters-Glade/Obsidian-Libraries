# Apostolic Authority

## Definition

The divine authority given by Christ to the apostles to establish and govern the early church, proclaim the gospel, write Scripture, and provide foundational teaching for the faith. This authority is expressed in their teaching, discipline, leadership, and confirmation of God's revelation.
