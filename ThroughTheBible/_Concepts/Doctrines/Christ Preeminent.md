# Christ Preeminent

## Definition

The doctrine that Christ is supreme over all creation, the head of the church, and the firstborn from the dead. In all things Christ has preeminence (Colossians 1:18). He is the image of the invisible God, the creator and sustainer of all things, and the ultimate revelation of God.
