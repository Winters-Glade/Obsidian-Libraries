# The Beast

## Description

The Beast is the prophetic figure in Revelation (also called the Antichrist) who leads the final rebellion against God. He receives power from the dragon (Satan), speaks blasphemies, and persecutes the saints. Chuck Smith teaches that the Beast represents the culmination of human government in opposition to God — ultimately defeated by the return of Christ, the Lamb's victory is complete over the Beast and all his followers.
