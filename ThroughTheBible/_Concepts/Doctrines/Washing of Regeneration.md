# Washing of Regeneration

The cleansing work of the Holy Spirit at the moment of salvation that brings new spiritual life (Titus 3:5). This washing is not external or ceremonial but an internal transformation where the sinner is cleansed from sin's guilt and power. Along with the 'renewing of the Holy Ghost,' it describes the complete renovation of the believer by divine grace.
