# Pauline Theology

## Definition

The body of theological teaching found in the epistles of the Apostle Paul, forming a major portion of New Testament doctrine. Key themes include justification by faith, union with Christ, the role of the Law, the nature of the church as the Body of Christ, eschatology, and the centrality of the cross and resurrection.
