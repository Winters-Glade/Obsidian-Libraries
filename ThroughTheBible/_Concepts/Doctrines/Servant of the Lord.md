# Servant of the Lord

## Definition

A title applied to key figures in Scripture, most significantly to Jesus Christ as the suffering servant described in Isaiah 42-53. The servant is chosen by God, empowered by the Spirit, and brings justice and salvation through humble service and atoning suffering.
