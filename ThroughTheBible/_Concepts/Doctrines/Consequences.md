# Consequences

The results or outcomes of human choices, particularly in relation to obedience and disobedience to God. Scripture teaches the principle of sowing and reaping (Galatians 6:7-8) and that divine discipline is an expression of God's love (Hebrews 12:5-11). Consequences in this life are not always final judgment but serve as opportunities for repentance and growth in holiness.
