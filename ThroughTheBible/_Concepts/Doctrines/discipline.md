# Discipline

## Description

Discipline in Scripture encompasses both self-discipline and the loving correction of God the Father toward His children. Chuck Smith teaches that discipline is not punishment but training—it is God's means of shaping believers into the likeness of Christ. Divine discipline, though painful at the time, produces the peaceful fruit of righteousness (Hebrews 12:11). Self-discipline, cultivated through the Holy Spirit, enables believers to resist sin, pursue godliness, and remain faithful in their walk with God.
