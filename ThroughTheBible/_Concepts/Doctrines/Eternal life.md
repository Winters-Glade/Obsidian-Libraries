# Eternal life

## Definition

The quality of life that comes from knowing God and Jesus Christ, beginning now and continuing forever. It is a gift of God through faith, not earned by works. Eternal life involves intimate knowledge of God, freedom from sin's power, and resurrection to everlasting joy.
