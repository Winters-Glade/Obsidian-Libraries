# Faith Defined

The biblical definition of faith from Hebrews 11:1 as 'the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.' Faith gives tangible reality ('substance') to future hopes and provides conviction ('evidence') for what cannot be empirically verified. It is the lens through which believers understand God's invisible work and the assurance that His promises are true.
