# Gnosticism

## Definition

A heretical movement in the early church that taught salvation through secret knowledge (gnosis). It typically denied the goodness of material creation and the true humanity of Christ. The New Testament epistles (especially John, Colossians, and 1 Timothy) contain refutations of proto-gnostic ideas.
