# Spiritual Warfare

## Definition

The ongoing conflict between the kingdom of God and the forces of evil — Satan, demons, and spiritual powers of darkness. Believers are called to stand firm in God's armor, prayer, and the word of God. The victory is ultimately secured through Christ's death and resurrection.
