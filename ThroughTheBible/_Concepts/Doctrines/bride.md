# Bride

## Description

The Bride is a biblical metaphor for the people of God in covenant relationship with Him. In the Old Testament, Israel is portrayed as the bride of Yahweh—unfaithful at times, yet still loved. In the New Testament, the Church is presented as the Bride of Christ, for whom Christ gave Himself, whom He cleanses, and whom He will present to Himself in glory. Chuck Smith often uses the Song of Solomon to illustrate the intimate love between Christ and His Church, and teaches that the marriage supper of the Lamb represents the ultimate union between Christ and His redeemed people.
