# Law

## Definition

The divinely given instruction, particularly the Mosaic Law (Torah), which reveals God's holy standards, defines sin, and serves as a tutor to lead people to Christ. While the Law cannot save, it remains useful for instruction in righteousness and reveals the character of God.
