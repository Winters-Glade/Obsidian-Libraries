# Sufficiency of Christ

The doctrine that Christ's grace, presence, and power are completely adequate for every need, trial, and circumstance (2 Corinthians 12:9, Philippians 4:13, 19). When Paul pleaded for relief from his thorn in the flesh, Christ's answer was that His grace was enough. Chuck Smith taught that discovering Christ's sufficiency is the secret of contentment in every situation.
