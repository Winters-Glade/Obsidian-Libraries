# Thorn in the Flesh

The unspecified affliction that Paul experienced, described as a messenger of Satan sent to buffet him (2 Corinthians 12:7-10). Paul prayed three times for its removal, but God answered that His grace was sufficient and His power is perfected in weakness. The thorn served to keep Paul humble amid the abundance of revelations he received.
