# All Humanity

The biblical teaching that all people, regardless of ethnicity, nationality, or social status, share a common origin in Adam and a common need for salvation (Acts 17:26, Romans 3:23). At Babel, humanity was scattered and divided by language, but at Pentecost the gospel began reversing that division. The unity of humanity is a biblical foundation for the universal call of the gospel.
