# Strength in Weakness

The paradoxical spiritual principle that God's power is most fully displayed through human weakness (2 Corinthians 12:9-10). When believers acknowledge their limitations, God's grace becomes their sufficiency. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that Paul learned to boast in his infirmities because Christ's power rested upon him—transforming weakness into a platform for divine strength.
