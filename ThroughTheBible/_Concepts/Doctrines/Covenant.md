# Covenant

## Definition

A divinely initiated agreement or bond between God and humanity, establishing relationship with promises and obligations. Major biblical covenants include: Adamic, Noahic, Abrahamic, Mosaic (Old Covenant), Davidic, and the New Covenant in Christ's blood. God's covenants reveal His redemptive plan.
