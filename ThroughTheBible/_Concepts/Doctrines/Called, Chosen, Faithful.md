# Called, Chosen, Faithful

## Description

Called, Chosen, Faithful is the description of those who overcome with the Lamb (Revelation 17:14). Chuck Smith teaches that God calls many through the gospel, but those who are chosen respond, and of those, the faithful endure to the end. This three-fold description describes the journey of salvation: God's sovereign call, His election of believers in Christ, and the perseverance of the saints by faith.
