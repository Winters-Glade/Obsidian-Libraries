# Lord of Lords

## Description

Lord of Lords is the exalted title of Jesus Christ, declaring His supreme authority over all earthly and heavenly rulers. Chuck Smith emphasizes that whatever title earthly rulers may claim, Jesus is the Lord of Lords and King of Kings. This title appears in Revelation 17:14 and 19:16, where Christ returns in glory to defeat His enemies. In the end, every knee will bow and every tongue confess that Jesus Christ is Lord.
