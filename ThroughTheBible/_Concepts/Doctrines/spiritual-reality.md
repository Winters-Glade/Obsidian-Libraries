# spiritual-reality

The unseen realm of God's activity that exists beyond physical perception, which can only be discerned through spiritual eyes. In 2 Kings 6, Elisha prayed for his servant to see the heavenly army surrounding Dothan—a spiritual reality more significant than the visible Syrian army. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that faith opens believers to this higher reality where God's power and presence are constantly at work.
