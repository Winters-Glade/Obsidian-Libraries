# Jerusalem-Judea-Samaria-Uttmost

## Description

Jerusalem-Judea-Samaria-Uttmost is the geographical outline of the Great Commission from Acts 1:8 — the progressive expansion of the gospel from the home base (Jerusalem) to the surrounding region (Judea), to the marginalized (Samaria), and to the ends of the earth. Chuck Smith teaches this as both a historical pattern of the early church and a model for personal evangelism: start where you are and expand outward.
