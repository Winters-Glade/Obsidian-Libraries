# Rooted in Love

The condition of being firmly established and grounded in the love of Christ (Ephesians 3:17), like a tree with deep roots drawing nourishment from rich soil. Paul prayed believers would be 'rooted and grounded in love,' knowing that the love of Christ is the foundation for spiritual growth and the context in which believers comprehend the fullness of God.
