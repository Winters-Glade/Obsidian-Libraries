# The Armor of God

## Definition

The spiritual resources God provides for believers to stand against spiritual attacks: the belt of truth, breastplate of righteousness, shoes of the gospel of peace, shield of faith, helmet of salvation, and sword of the Spirit (Ephesians 6:10-18). Prayer accompanies the armor.
