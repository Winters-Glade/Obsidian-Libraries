# Christ as Creator

## Description

Christ as Creator is the biblical teaching that Jesus Christ, the eternal Word, was the agent through whom all things were made. John 1:3 declares, 'All things were made through Him.' Chuck Smith emphasizes that the One who died on the cross is the same One who spoke the universe into existence. This truth elevates the significance of Christ's work — the Creator entered His creation to redeem it.
