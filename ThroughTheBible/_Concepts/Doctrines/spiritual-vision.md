# spiritual-vision

The capacity granted by the Holy Spirit to perceive spiritual truths and realities beyond natural sight. Like Elisha's servant whose eyes were opened to see the horses and chariots of fire (2 Kings 6:17), believers are called to pray for spiritual vision to recognize God's activity in their circumstances. Chuck Smith taught that spiritual vision develops through prayer, Scripture meditation, and the Holy Spirit's illumination.
