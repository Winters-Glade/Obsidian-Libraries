# Righteousness

## Description

Righteousness in Scripture refers to the quality of being morally right or just. Imputed righteousness is the righteousness of Christ credited to the believer through faith (justification). Imparted righteousness is the work of the Holy Spirit in sanctifying the believer. God Himself is the ultimate standard of righteousness.
