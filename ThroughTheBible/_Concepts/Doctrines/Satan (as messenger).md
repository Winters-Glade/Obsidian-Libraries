# Satan (as messenger)

## Description

Satan as messenger refers to Paul's description of a 'messenger of Satan' sent to buffet him — a thorn in the flesh permitted by God to keep him humble. Chuck Smith teaches that Satan can work through circumstances, people, or physical affliction to oppose God's servants, but God sovereignly uses even Satan's attacks for His purposes. Paul's weakness became the stage for God's strength to be displayed.
