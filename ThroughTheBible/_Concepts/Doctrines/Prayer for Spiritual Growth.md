# Prayer for Spiritual Growth

Paul's pattern of intercessory prayer for believers to grow in their knowledge of God, understand their calling, and experience His power (Ephesians 1:15-23, 3:14-21, Philippians 1:9-11). These prayers focus not on material needs but on spiritual illumination, inner strength, and deeper comprehension of Christ's love. Pastor Chuck Smith held these as a model for how to pray for others.
