# Refuge

## Description

Refuge is a biblical metaphor for God as the place of safety, protection, and shelter for His people. Chuck Smith draws on the Psalms' frequent depiction of God as a refuge—a fortress, a strong tower, a hiding place—in times of trouble. Unlike physical refuges that can fail, God is an unfailing sanctuary for the soul. Believers are called to run to Him for protection from spiritual enemies, life's storms, and the pressures of the world. The cities of refuge in the Old Testament are types of Christ, the ultimate refuge for sinners.
