# Ark of the Covenant

## Definition

The sacred chest constructed at God's command, containing the tablets of the Law, Aaron's rod, and manna. It symbolized God's throne and presence among Israel, resting in the Holy of Holies. It prefigured Christ as the meeting place between God and humanity.
