# Jews

The descendants of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, the chosen people of God through whom the Messiah came. In Paul's teaching, being a Jew outwardly is less important than being one inwardly by circumcision of the heart (Romans 2:28-29). The gospel came 'to the Jew first and also to the Greek' (Romans 1:16), and Pastor Chuck Smith taught that God's covenant with Israel remains irrevocable (Romans 11:29).
