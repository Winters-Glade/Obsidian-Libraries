# Moses' Choice

## Description

Moses' Choice refers to the decision of Moses recorded in Hebrews 11:24-26, where he refused to be called the son of Pharaoh's daughter, choosing rather to suffer affliction with the people of God than to enjoy the passing pleasures of sin. Chuck Smith presents this as a defining moment of faith — choosing the reproach of Christ over the treasures of Egypt. Moses valued the reward of God more than the riches of the world.
