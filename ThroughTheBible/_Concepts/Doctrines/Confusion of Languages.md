# Confusion of Languages

The divine judgment at the Tower of Babel where God confused human language and scattered the people across the earth (Genesis 11:1-9). This event explains the origin of diverse languages and nations. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that this judgment was also an act of mercy—preventing humanity from uniting in rebellion. The reversal began at Pentecost when the gospel was heard in every language.
