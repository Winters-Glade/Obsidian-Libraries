# shame

## Description

Shame is the painful emotion of humiliation or disgrace arising from consciousness of guilt or failure. In Scripture, shame entered the world with sin (Adam and Eve's nakedness). Chuck Smith teaches that the gospel addresses both guilt and shame — while guilt is removed by forgiveness, shame is overcome by the believer's new identity in Christ. Believers will ultimately stand before God without shame because they are clothed in Christ's righteousness.
