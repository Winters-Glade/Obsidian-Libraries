# Day of the Lord

## Definition

A biblical phrase describing a time of divine intervention in history for judgment and salvation. In the Old Testament, it referred to judgment against Israel and the nations. In the New Testament, it points to the final return of Christ, the judgment of the world, and the establishment of God's eternal kingdom.
