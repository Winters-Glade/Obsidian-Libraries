# Heavenly Army

The angelic host of heaven that God deploys in defense of His people, as revealed to Elisha's servant at Dothan (2 Kings 6:15-17). The prophet's assurance that 'those who are with us are more than those who are with them' rests on the reality of this invisible army. This concept demonstrates that divine power always exceeds any earthly opposition and that believers are surrounded by spiritual resources.
