# Witness

## Description

Witness (Greek: martys) in the New Testament refers to one who testifies to what they have seen and heard, particularly to the truth of the gospel and the resurrection of Jesus Christ. Chuck Smith emphasizes that every believer is called to be a witness — not necessarily a preacher or missionary, but someone who personally testifies to what Christ has done in their life. The power for witness comes from the Holy Spirit.
