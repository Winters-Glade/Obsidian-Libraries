# Comfort in Affliction

## Definition

The divine consolation and encouragement God provides to believers during times of suffering, trials, and persecution. God is described as 'the God of all comfort' (2 Corinthians 1:3). Believers are comforted so they can comfort others with the same comfort they have received.
