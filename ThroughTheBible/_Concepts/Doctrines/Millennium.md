# Millennium

## Description

The Millennium refers to the thousand-year reign of Christ on earth described in Revelation 20:1-6. Premillennialists interpret this as a literal future period of peace and righteousness when Christ rules from Jerusalem. Amillennialists see it as the present church age. Postmillennialists view it as a future golden age brought about through the spread of the gospel.
