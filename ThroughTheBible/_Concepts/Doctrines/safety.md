# safety

## Description

Safety in Scripture is ultimately found in God alone, who is the believer's refuge and fortress. The Psalms repeatedly declare that those who dwell in the shelter of the Most High are safe (Psalm 91). Chuck Smith teaches that safety is not the absence of danger but the assurance that one is in God's hands regardless of circumstances. True safety is spiritual — the security of eternal life in Christ.
