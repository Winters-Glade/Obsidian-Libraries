# Divine Sovereignty

## Description

Divine sovereignty is the biblical doctrine that God possesses supreme authority and power over all creation. He reigns over history, nations, and individuals, accomplishing His purposes without being thwarted by human actions or evil. This sovereignty is exercised in conjunction with human responsibility, a mystery that Scripture affirms without fully explaining.
