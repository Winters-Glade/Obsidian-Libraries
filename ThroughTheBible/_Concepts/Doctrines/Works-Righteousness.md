# Works-Righteousness

The attempt to earn salvation or favor with God through human effort and obedience to the law, which Scripture declares impossible for salvation (Romans 3:20, 9:31-32, Galatians 2:16). Works-righteousness stands in contrast to imputed righteousness received by faith. Chuck Smith taught that any system of self-salvation by works denies the sufficiency of Christ's finished work on the cross.
