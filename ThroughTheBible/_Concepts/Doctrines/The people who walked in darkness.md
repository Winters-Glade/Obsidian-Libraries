# The People Who Walked in Darkness

## Description

"The people who walked in darkness have seen a great light" is a prophetic declaration from Isaiah 9:2 that refers to the tribes of Zebulun and Naphtali in the region of Galilee — areas that experienced the darkness of Assyrian conquest and Gentile occupation. Isaiah prophesied that these darkened regions would be the first to witness the coming of the Messiah's light. Chuck Smith highlights how this prophecy was literally fulfilled when Jesus began His ministry in Galilee, bringing spiritual light to those living in the shadow of death.
