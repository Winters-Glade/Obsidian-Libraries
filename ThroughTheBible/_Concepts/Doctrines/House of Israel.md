# House of Israel

## Description

House of Israel refers to the collective nation of Israel, descended from Jacob (Israel). In Scripture, it distinguishes the northern kingdom (Israel) from the southern kingdom (Judah) but is also used prophetically for the reunited people of God. Chuck Smith teaches that God's relationship with the House of Israel demonstrates His faithfulness despite their unfaithfulness, and that the promises to Israel are irrevocable (Romans 11:29).
