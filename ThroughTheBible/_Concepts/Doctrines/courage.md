# courage

## Description

Courage in Scripture is not the absence of fear but the strength to act rightly in the face of fear, empowered by trust in God. Joshua was commanded to 'be strong and of good courage' (Joshua 1:6-9) because God was with him. Chuck Smith teaches that biblical courage is rooted in God's promises and presence, not in self-confidence. The same courage available to Joshua is available to believers who trust God's Word.
