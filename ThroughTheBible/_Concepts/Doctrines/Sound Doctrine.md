# Sound Doctrine

## Definition

Teaching that is healthy, correct, and consistent with the apostolic faith as revealed in Scripture. Sound doctrine promotes godliness, guards against error, and provides the framework for Christian belief and practice. It is essential for church health and spiritual growth.
