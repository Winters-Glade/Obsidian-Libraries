# Perspective

The godly viewpoint that enables believers to see their circumstances from an eternal vantage point rather than being overwhelmed by temporal troubles. Paul wrote that 'our light affliction, which is but for a moment, works for us a far more exceeding and eternal weight of glory' (2 Corinthians 4:17). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that biblical perspective transforms how believers face trials.
