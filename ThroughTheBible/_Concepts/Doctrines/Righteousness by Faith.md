# Righteousness by Faith

## Definition

The righteousness that comes from God through faith in Jesus Christ, apart from works of the Law. This righteousness is imputed to believers — Christ's perfect obedience is credited to their account — and becomes the basis for their standing before God. It is both a legal declaration (justification) and a transformative reality.
