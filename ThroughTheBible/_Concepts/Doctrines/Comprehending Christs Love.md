# Comprehending Christ's Love

The spiritual understanding of the vast dimensions of Christ's love—its breadth, length, depth, and height (Ephesians 3:18-19). Though the love of Christ surpasses knowledge, believers grow in comprehending it through the Spirit's revelation. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that experiencing God's love is the deepest need of the human heart and the key to spiritual fullness.
