# encounter-with-God

The life-transforming experience of coming into God's manifest presence, exemplified by Isaiah's vision in the Temple (Isaiah 6:1-8). When Isaiah saw the Lord exalted and heard the seraphim declare 'Holy, holy, holy,' he was immediately convicted of his sinfulness, cleansed by a live coal from the altar, and commissioned for service. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that authentic encounter with God always produces humility, cleansing, and a willingness to respond to His call.
