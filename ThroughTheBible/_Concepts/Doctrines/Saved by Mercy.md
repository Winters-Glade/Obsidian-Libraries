# Saved by Mercy

The biblical truth that salvation is entirely an act of God's mercy, not human merit or works (Titus 3:5, Ephesians 2:8-9). God's mercy withholds the judgment we deserve and extends compassion instead. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that understanding salvation as pure mercy produces humility and gratitude in believers—they have nothing to boast about except the Lord.
