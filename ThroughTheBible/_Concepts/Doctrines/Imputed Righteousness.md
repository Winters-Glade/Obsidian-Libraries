# Imputed Righteousness

The legal declaration by which the perfect righteousness of Jesus Christ is credited to the believe's account through faith (Romans 3:21-22, 4:6-8, 2 Corinthians 5:21). This is a cornerstone of Protestant theology—righteousness is not infused through works but imputed through faith. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that believers stand before God not in their own righteousness but in Christ's perfect righteousness.
