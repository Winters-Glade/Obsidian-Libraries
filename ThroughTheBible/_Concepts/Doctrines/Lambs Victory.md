# Lamb's Victory

## Description

Lamb's Victory is the central theme of Revelation — that Jesus Christ, the Lamb who was slain, has triumphed over all the forces of evil. The Lamb is worthy to open the scroll, receives the worship of heaven, and ultimately defeats the beast and the kings of the earth. Chuck Smith highlights this as the great reversal: the Victim is the Victor, the slain Lamb is the conquering King. Believers share in His victory through faith.
