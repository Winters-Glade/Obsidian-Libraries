# Hardening of Heart

The condition of spiritual stubbornness and resistance to God that results from persistent disobedience and unbelief (Romans 9:18, Hebrews 3:7-15). Pharaoh's heart was hardened in response to Moses' demands, and Paul discusses how Israel experienced a partial hardening until the fullness of the Gentiles comes in (Romans 11:25). Hardening can be both a divine judgment and a human choice.
