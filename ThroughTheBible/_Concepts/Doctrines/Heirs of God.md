# Heirs of God

The status of believers as joint-heirs with Christ, entitled to the full inheritance of God's kingdom (Romans 8:16-17, Galatians 4:7). Through adoption as sons, believers are not merely servants but children and heirs. Martin Luther called this the 'great exchange'—Christ takes our sin, and we receive His righteousness and all the privileges of sonship.
