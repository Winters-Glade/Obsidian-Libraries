# Walking in the Light

The lifestyle of living in truth, holiness, and fellowship with God as revealed in Christ (1 John 1:5-7). Walking in the light means living transparently before God and others, confessing sin, and obeying God's commands. It stands in contrast to walking in darkness, which is characterized by sin, deception, and separation from fellowship with God.
