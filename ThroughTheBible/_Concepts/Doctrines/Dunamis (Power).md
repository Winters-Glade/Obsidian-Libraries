# Dunamis (Power)

## Description

Dunamis is the Greek word for power, from which the English word 'dynamite' derives. In the New Testament, it describes the supernatural power of God at work in believers, particularly the power of the Holy Spirit. Chuck Smith teaches that Jesus promised His disciples would receive dunamis when the Holy Spirit came upon them (Acts 1:8), empowering them to be witnesses — not human ability but divine enablement for supernatural living.
