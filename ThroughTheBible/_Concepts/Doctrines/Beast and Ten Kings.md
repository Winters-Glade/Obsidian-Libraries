# Beast and Ten Kings

## Description

The Beast and Ten Kings is the prophetic imagery from Revelation 17 describing the final world empire that opposes God. The Beast represents the Antichrist, and the ten kings represent a confederation of rulers who give their power and authority to the Beast. Chuck Smith teaches that these prophecies point to a future world government that will persecute God's people before being destroyed by Christ at His coming.
