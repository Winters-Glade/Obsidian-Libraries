# Christ

## Definition

From Greek 'Christos' (Anointed One), the New Testament title for Jesus as the promised Messiah. Christ is the eternal Son of God, fully divine and fully human, who died for sins, rose again, ascended to heaven, and will return. He is the head of the church, the mediator between God and humanity.
