# Resurrection

## Definition

The bodily raising of the dead to new life. Central to Christian faith is the resurrection of Jesus Christ on the third day, which validates His deity, secures salvation, and guarantees the future resurrection of all believers. The resurrection is the cornerstone of Christian hope and the defeat of death.
