# Ministry of Reconciliation

## Definition

The divinely given mission of proclaiming peace between God and humanity through Jesus Christ. Believers are entrusted with this ministry as ambassadors for Christ, calling others to be reconciled to God. It is rooted in Christ's work of reconciling the world to Himself.
