# New Creation

## Definition

The spiritual transformation that occurs when a person is united with Christ through faith. The believer becomes 'a new creation; old things have passed away; behold, all things have become new' (2 Corinthians 5:17). It also refers to the future renewal of all creation at Christ's return.
