# Adoption as Sons

The theological doctrine that believers are granted full legal status as children of God through faith in Jesus Christ (Galatians 4:4-7, Romans 8:14-17). Unlike physical birth, adoption in Roman and Jewish contexts conferred full inheritance rights. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that through Christ's redemptive work, believers receive the Spirit of adoption, enabling them to cry out 'Abba, Father.'
