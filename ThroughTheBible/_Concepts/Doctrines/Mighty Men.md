# Mighty Men

## Description

Mighty Men (Gibborim) were David's elite warriors who performed extraordinary feats of bravery and loyalty. The list in 2 Samuel 23 and 1 Chronicles 11-12 names about 50 men, including the 'Three' (Josheb-Basshebeth, Eleazar, Shammah) and the 'Thirty.' Chuck Smith uses David's mighty men as an illustration of devoted loyalty and sacrificial service, drawing parallels to believers' loyalty to Christ, the greater David.
