# Darkness to Light

The transformation of conversion described as being turned from the power of Satan to God, from darkness to light (Acts 26:18, 1 Peter 2:9). This was Paul's commission from Christ—to open blind eyes so people could receive forgiveness and an inheritance among those sanctified by faith. The imagery depicts the radical change at salvation: spiritual blindness replaced by divine illumination.
