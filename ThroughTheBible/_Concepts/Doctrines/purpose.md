# purpose

## Description

Purpose refers to God's sovereign intentions for creation and for individual lives. Scripture reveals that God works all things according to the counsel of His will (Ephesians 1:11) and that believers are created in Christ Jesus for good works prepared beforehand (Ephesians 2:10). Chuck Smith teaches that discovering and living in God's purpose brings meaning, direction, and fulfillment — the ultimate answer to the question of why we exist.
