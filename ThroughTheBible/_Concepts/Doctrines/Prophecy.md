# Prophecy

## Definition

Divinely inspired communication from God through human messengers (prophets). Prophecy includes both foretelling (predicting future events) and forthtelling (declaring God's word to the present situation). Biblical prophecy centers on the person and work of Jesus Christ and the establishment of God's kingdom.
