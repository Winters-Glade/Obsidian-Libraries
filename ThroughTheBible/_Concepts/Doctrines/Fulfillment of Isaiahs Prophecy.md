# Fulfillment of Isaiah's Prophecy

The New Testament affirmation that many of Isaiah's prophecies were fulfilled in the life, ministry, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ. This includes the virgin birth (Isaiah 7:14), the great light in Galilee (Isaiah 9:1-2), the ministry of the Suffering Servant (Isaiah 53), and the outpouring of the Spirit (Isaiah 44:3). Pastor Chuck Smith frequently showed how Isaiah pointed forward to Christ.
