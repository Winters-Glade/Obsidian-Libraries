# Providence of God

The continuous activity of God in sustaining and governing all creation to accomplish His purposes. Divine providence means that God not only created the world but actively guides history, provides for His people, and works all things together for good (Romans 8:28). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that trusting God's providence brings peace amid life's uncertainties.
