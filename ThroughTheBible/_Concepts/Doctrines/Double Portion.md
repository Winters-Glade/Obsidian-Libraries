# Double Portion

## Description

Double Portion is a biblical concept referring to a special measure of blessing, anointing, or inheritance given to the firstborn or to a particularly favored servant of God. Elisha asked for a double portion of Elijah's spirit, signifying not twice as much but the portion of the heir and successor. Chuck Smith teaches that the double portion represents the fullness of God's Spirit available to those who are willing to pay the price of following hard after God—not a selfish desire for more, but a desire to be more effective in God's service.
