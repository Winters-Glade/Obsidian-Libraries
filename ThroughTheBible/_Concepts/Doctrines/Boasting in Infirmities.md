# Boasting in Infirmities

Paul's radical perspective of rejoicing in weaknesses, hardships, and limitations because they create space for Christ's power to be displayed (2 Corinthians 11:30, 12:9-10). Rather than hiding or resenting human frailty, Paul learned to see them as opportunities for God's grace to shine. This upside-down value system is central to the gospel message.
