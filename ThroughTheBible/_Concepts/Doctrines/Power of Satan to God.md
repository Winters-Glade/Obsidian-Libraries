# Power of Satan to God

The transfer of allegiance and authority that occurs at conversion—believers are delivered from the domain of darkness and transferred into the kingdom of God's beloved Son (Acts 26:18, Colossians 1:13). Before salvation, all people are under Satan's power; through faith in Christ, they are set free. Pastor Chuck Smith taught this is nothing less than a spiritual liberation.
