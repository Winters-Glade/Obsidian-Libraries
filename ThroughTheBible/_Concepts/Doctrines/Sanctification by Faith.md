# Sanctification by Faith

The process by which believers are progressively made holy through their ongoing trust in Christ and the work of the Holy Spirit (Acts 26:18, Romans 6:22). Sanctification is both positional (set apart for God at conversion) and progressive (growing in holiness through faith). Just as justification is by faith, sanctification is also by faith—living in dependence on the Spirit's work.
