# Faithful Saying

A phrase Paul uses to introduce key doctrinal statements that summarize essential gospel truths, appearing in 1 Timothy 1:15, 3:1, 4:9, 2 Timothy 2:11, and Titus 3:8. One of the most significant is 'Christ Jesus came into the world to save sinners' (1 Timothy 1:15). These 'faithful sayings' were likely early Christian creedal statements.
