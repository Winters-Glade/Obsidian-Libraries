# Spiritual Blessings in Christ

## Definition

The abundant spiritual riches and privileges that believers possess through their union with Christ (Ephesians 1:3). These include being chosen, adopted, redeemed, forgiven, sealed with the Spirit, and given an inheritance. They are the foundation of the believer's identity.
