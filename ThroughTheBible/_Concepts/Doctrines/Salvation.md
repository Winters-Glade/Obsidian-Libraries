# Salvation

## Definition

Deliverance from sin and its consequences through faith in Jesus Christ. Salvation includes past (justification), present (sanctification), and future (glorification) dimensions. It is entirely by grace through faith, not of works, and encompasses forgiveness, redemption, reconciliation, and eternal life.
