# Deliverance from Wrath

The salvation of believers from the coming wrath of God—both the temporal judgments in history and the final judgment at the end of the age (1 Thessalonians 1:10, Romans 5:9). Jesus delivers believers from the wrath to come, a promise in which Paul encouraged the Thessalonians amid their persecution. This assurance of future deliverance fuels patient endurance in the present.
