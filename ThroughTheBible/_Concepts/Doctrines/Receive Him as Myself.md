# Receive Him as Myself

Paul's request to Philemon to welcome Onesimus as he would welcome Paul himself (Philemon 1:17). This illustrates how God receives believers in Christ—not on their own merits but as He receives His Son. It pictures acceptance and fellowship restored through another's mediation, a powerful analogy of how God welcomes repentant sinners through Christ's righteousness.
