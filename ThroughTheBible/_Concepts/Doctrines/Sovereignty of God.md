# Sovereignty of God

## Definition

The biblical doctrine that God is the supreme ruler and authority over all creation. He exercises His will in heaven and on earth, ordaining whatsoever comes to pass. God's sovereignty is compatible with human responsibility and is a source of comfort, assurance, and worship for believers.
