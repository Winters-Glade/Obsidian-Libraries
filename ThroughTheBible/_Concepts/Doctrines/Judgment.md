# Judgment

## Definition

The divine evaluation and sentencing of humanity according to God's righteous standards. Scripture speaks of the judgment of believers (at the Bema Seat of Christ for rewards) and of unbelievers (at the Great White Throne Judgment). Judgment reveals God's justice and holiness.
