# Millennial Kingdom

## Description

The Millennial Kingdom is the eschatological reign of Christ on earth. According to premillennial interpretation, after Christ's return He will reign for a thousand years from Jerusalem, fulfilling the Davidic covenant. This period will be characterized by peace, righteousness, and the restoration of Israel. After the millennium comes the final judgment and the eternal state.
