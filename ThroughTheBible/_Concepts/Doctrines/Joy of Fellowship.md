# Joy of Fellowship

The mutual joy and communion that believers share with God and with one another through the gospel (1 John 1:3-4). John wrote his letter so that believers could have fellowship with the apostles, with the Father, and with Jesus Christ—and that their joy would be full. Fellowship is both vertical (with God) and horizontal (with other believers).
