# Faith

## Definition

Trust and reliance upon God and His promises, particularly in Jesus Christ for salvation. Faith is the substance of things hoped for and the evidence of things not seen (Hebrews 11:1). It is the means by which believers receive God's grace and are justified, not by works but through trusting in Christ's finished work.
