# Image of the Invisible God

## Description

Image of the Invisible God is the title of Christ from Colossians 1:15, declaring that Jesus perfectly reveals the invisible God. Just as an image or icon represents what it depicts, Christ is the exact representation of God's being. Chuck Smith teaches that anyone who wants to know what God is like should look at Jesus — His compassion, holiness, power, and love perfectly reflect the Father, making the invisible God visible to humanity.
