# New Covenant

## Definition

The covenant established by Jesus Christ through His sacrificial death, promised in Jeremiah 31:31-34 and inaugurated at the Last Supper. It supersedes the Old Covenant (Mosaic Law), providing complete forgiveness of sins, the indwelling of the Holy Spirit, and a direct relationship with God for all who believe.
