# Scattering

The dispersion of humanity from Babel as God confused their languages and scattered them over the face of the earth (Genesis 11:8-9). This scattering was both a judgment on human pride and the mechanism by which humanity filled the earth as originally commanded. The term also applies to the Jewish Diaspora—God's people scattered among the nations.
