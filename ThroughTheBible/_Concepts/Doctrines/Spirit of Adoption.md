# Spirit of Adoption

The work of the Holy Spirit who places believers into God's family and gives them the inner assurance that they are His children (Romans 8:15-16). Unlike a spirit of fear that characterizes slavery, the Spirit of Adoption produces confidence, intimacy, and the joyful cry 'Abba, Father.' This doctrine assures believers of their secure position in God's family.
