# New Heaven and New Earth

## Description

The new heaven and new earth is the final state of the redeemed creation, described in Isaiah 65:17, 66:22, and Revelation 21-22. After the final judgment, God will create a new heaven and a new earth where righteousness dwells, free from sin, death, and sorrow. The New Jerusalem descends from heaven, and God dwells with His people forever.
