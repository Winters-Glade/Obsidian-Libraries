# Unity of the Spirit

## Definition

The spiritual oneness that believers share in Christ through the Holy Spirit. It is not created by human effort but maintained by walking in humility, patience, and love. The goal is unity of faith and knowledge of Christ, with diversity of gifts serving the one body.
