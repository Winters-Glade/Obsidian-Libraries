# Cleansing from Sin

God's ongoing work of purifying believers from the defilement of sin through the blood of Jesus Christ (1 John 1:7, 9). This cleansing is both instantaneous (the initial forgiveness at salvation) and continuous (the daily cleansing needed as believers walk in the light). The blood of Christ is sufficient for both—it not only forgives but also purifies the conscience.
