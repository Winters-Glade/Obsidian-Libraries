# Fruit of the Spirit

## Definition

The nine-fold character quality produced in believers by the Holy Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control (Galatians 5:22-23). This fruit is the evidence of the Spirit's work and stands in contrast to the works of the flesh.
