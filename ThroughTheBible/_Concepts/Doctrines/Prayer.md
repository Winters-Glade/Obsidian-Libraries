# Prayer

## Description

Prayer is the means by which believers communicate with God, offering praise, confession, thanksgiving, and supplication. Scripture presents prayer as both a privilege and a responsibility of the children of God. Jesus modeled a life of prayer and taught His disciples to pray (the Lord's Prayer). Paul exhorts believers to pray without ceasing (1 Thessalonians 5:17).
