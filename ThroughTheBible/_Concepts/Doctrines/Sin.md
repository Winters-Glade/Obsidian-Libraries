# Sin

## Definition

Any thought, word, or action that falls short of God's holy standards and transgresses His law. Sin originated with Adam's rebellion and has infected all humanity. It separates people from God, brings guilt and death, and can only be atoned for through the sacrifice of Jesus Christ.
