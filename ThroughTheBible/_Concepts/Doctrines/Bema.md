# Bema

## Definition

The judgment seat of Christ (from Greek 'bema') where believers will appear after the resurrection to receive rewards for faithful service. This judgment is not about salvation (which is secure in Christ) but about evaluating works done in the body. It is distinct from the Great White Throne Judgment of unbelievers.
