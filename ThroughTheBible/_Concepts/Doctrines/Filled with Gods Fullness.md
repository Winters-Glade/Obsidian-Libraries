# Filled with God's Fullness

The ultimate goal of Paul's prayer in Ephesians 3:19—that believers might be filled with all the fullness of God. This is not the believer becoming God, but being so saturated and controlled by His presence, character, and power that there is room for nothing else. It represents the maximum possible experience of God's indwelling presence this side of eternity.
