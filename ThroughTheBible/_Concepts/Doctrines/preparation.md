# preparation

## Description

Preparation in Scripture refers to both God's preparation of His people for service and the believer's responsibility to prepare spiritually. John the Baptist prepared the way for the Lord; the early church prepared for persecution; Paul prepared Timothy for ministry. Chuck Smith teaches that preparation involves prayer, study of Scripture, character development, and dependence on the Holy Spirit — being ready for whatever God calls believers to do.
