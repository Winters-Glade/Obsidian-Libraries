# Atonement

## Description

Atonement is the work of Christ on the cross by which He reconciled sinners to God. In the Old Testament, atonement was made through animal sacrifices on the Day of Atonement (Yom Kippur). Christ's death fulfilled these types, providing once-for-all atonement for sin. Key aspects include propitiation (satisfying God's wrath), expiation (removing sin's guilt), and reconciliation.
