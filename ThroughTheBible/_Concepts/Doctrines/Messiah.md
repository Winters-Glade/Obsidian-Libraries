# Messiah

## Definition

From the Hebrew 'Mashiach' (Anointed One), translated as 'Christ' in Greek. The promised deliverer and king prophesied in the Old Testament, fulfilled in Jesus of Nazareth. Jesus is the Messiah who brings salvation, establishes God's kingdom, and will return to reign in glory.
