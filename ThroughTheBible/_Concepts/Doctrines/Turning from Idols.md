# Turning from Idols

The foundational step of conversion for Gentiles, who turned from worthless idols to serve the living and true God (1 Thessalonians 1:9, Acts 14:15). This turning involves repentance from false worship and allegiance to the one true God. The Thessalonian believers became a model of genuine conversion, demonstrating that saving faith produces a visible break with idolatry and sin.
