# God

## Definition

The supreme being, creator, and ruler of the universe. In Christian theology, God is one in essence but three in person — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (the Trinity). God is eternal, omniscient, omnipotent, omnipresent, holy, just, loving, merciful, and gracious. He is the source of all life, truth, and goodness.
