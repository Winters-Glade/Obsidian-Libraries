# No Longer a Slave

The transformed status of believers from spiritual bondage to freedom in Christ (Galatians 4:7, Philemon 1:16). Through the gospel, Onesimus moved from being a slave to a beloved brother—just as all believers move from being slaves to sin to being sons and daughters of God. This transformation illustrates the radical change the gospel produces in human identity and relationships.
