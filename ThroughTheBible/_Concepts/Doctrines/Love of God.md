# Love of God

## Definition

The essential attribute of God's character — His eternal, unconditional, and sacrificial love for humanity. The love of God is most fully revealed in the sending of His Son Jesus Christ for the salvation of sinners. It is unearned, unfailing, and the foundation of the believer's security.
