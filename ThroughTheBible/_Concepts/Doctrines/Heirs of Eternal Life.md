# Heirs of Eternal Life

The promise that believers who are justified by grace become heirs according to the hope of eternal life (Titus 3:7). This inheritance is not earned but received through God's grace, secured by Christ's resurrection, and guaranteed by the Holy Spirit. Eternal life begins now as a quality of relationship with God and culminates in the age to come.
