# Nature of Faith

The essential character of biblical faith as confident trust in God and His promises, based on His character and word rather than visible evidence (Hebrews 11:1). Faith is not wishful thinking but a firm conviction about unseen realities, grounded in God's faithfulness. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that faith takes God at His word and acts on it, even when circumstances contradict the promise.
