# Inner Strength

The spiritual power and endurance that God supplies to believers through the Holy Spirit in their inner being (Ephesians 3:16). Paul prayed for believers to be strengthened with might by God's Spirit, enabling them to grasp the vast dimensions of Christ's love and be filled with all the fullness of God—a prayer for deep, internal spiritual resources.
