# Holy Spirit

## Definition

The third person of the Trinity, fully divine, who convicts the world of sin, righteousness, and judgment, regenerates believers, indwells them, seals them for redemption, empowers for service, and produces spiritual fruit. The Holy Spirit inspired Scripture and guides the church into all truth.
