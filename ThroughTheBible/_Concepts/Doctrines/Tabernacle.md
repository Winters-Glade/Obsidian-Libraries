# Tabernacle

## Definition

The portable sanctuary constructed by Israel under Moses' leadership, serving as the dwelling place of God's presence during the wilderness journey. It was a shadow and type of Christ, who 'tabernacled' among us (John 1:14). It depicted God's provision for worship, sacrifice, and fellowship.
