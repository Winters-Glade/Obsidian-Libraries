# Empowerment for Service

## Description

Empowerment for Service is the Holy Spirit's equipping of believers for effective ministry. Acts 1:8 promises that believers will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes upon them for the specific purpose of being witnesses. Chuck Smith teaches that spiritual gifts, talents, and opportunities all flow from the Spirit's empowerment — not human ability but divine enablement for the work God calls believers to do.
