# Gospel of Reconciliation

The message that through Christ, God has reconciled the world to Himself, not counting their trespasses against them, and has committed to believers 'the ministry of reconciliation' (2 Corinthians 5:18-19). Reconciliation means that the enmity between God and humanity caused by sin has been removed through Christ's sacrifice. Pastor Chuck Smith taught this is the heart of the gospel message.
