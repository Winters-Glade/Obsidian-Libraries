# Love (Agape)

## Definition

The highest form of love in Christian theology — selfless, sacrificial, unconditional love that seeks the highest good of others. Agape is the very nature of God (1 John 4:8) and is demonstrated supremely in Christ's atoning sacrifice. It is the greatest of the spiritual virtues.
