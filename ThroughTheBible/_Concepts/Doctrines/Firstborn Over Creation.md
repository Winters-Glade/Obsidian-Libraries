# Firstborn Over Creation

## Description

Firstborn Over Creation is the title of Christ from Colossians 1:15, declaring His preeminence and sovereignty over all creation. 'Firstborn' here does not mean created but refers to rank and priority — Christ is the heir and ruler over everything created. Chuck Smith emphasizes that Christ is not part of creation but the Creator Himself, and He holds the position of supreme authority over the entire universe.
