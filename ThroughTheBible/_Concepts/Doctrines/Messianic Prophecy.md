# Messianic Prophecy

## Description

Messianic prophecy refers to Old Testament prophecies that foretell the coming of the Messiah (Christ). These prophecies span the Law, Prophets, and Writings, predicting His birth, life, ministry, suffering, death, resurrection, and future reign. Major messianic passages include Isaiah 53, Psalm 22, Daniel 9, and Micah 5:2.
