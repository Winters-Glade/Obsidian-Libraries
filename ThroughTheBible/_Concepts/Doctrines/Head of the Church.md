# Head of the Church

## Description

Head of the Church is the biblical title expressing Christ's supreme authority and leadership over His body, the Church. As the head, Christ directs, nourishes, and cares for His body. Chuck Smith teaches that the Church has no other head — not a pope, council, or denomination. Christ alone is the head, and believers are to submit to His authority individually and collectively as His body.
