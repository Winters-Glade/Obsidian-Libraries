# Preeminence of Christ

## Description

The Preeminence of Christ is the teaching that Christ must have first place in all things — in creation, in the church, and in every believer's life. Colossians 1:18 declares that Christ should 'have the preeminence.' Chuck Smith emphasizes that Christ is supreme over everything and that believers must constantly guard against anything — even good things — taking His rightful place of supremacy.
