# The Great Commission to Gentiles

The mandate given to the church to make disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19-20), which propelled the gospel beyond Jewish boundaries. Paul's entire ministry was dedicated to bringing Gentiles to faith, establishing churches throughout Asia Minor and Greece. The book of Acts documents this gradual but decisive expansion from Jerusalem to the ends of the earth.
