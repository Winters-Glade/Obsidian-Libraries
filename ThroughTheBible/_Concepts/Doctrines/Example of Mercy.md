# Example of Mercy

Paul's understanding that his own salvation served as a pattern or example of God's saving mercy for all who would believe (1 Timothy 1:16). In showing mercy to the 'chief of sinners,' Christ displayed His unlimited patience as an encouragement. If the persecutor Saul could be transformed into the apostle Paul, no one is beyond the reach of God's grace.
