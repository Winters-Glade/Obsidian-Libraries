# Bible

## Description

The Bible (from Greek biblia, 'books') is the inspired Word of God, consisting of the Old and New Testaments. Chuck Smith teaches throughout the T2000 series that the Bible is not merely a human book but God-breathed (theopneustos, 2 Timothy 3:16), fully authoritative, inerrant in its original manuscripts, and sufficient for all matters of faith and practice. It is the believer's guide, comfort, and source of spiritual life.
