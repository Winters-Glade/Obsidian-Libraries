# Unity

The biblical ideal of oneness among God's people, beginning with the original unity of humanity before Babel and ultimately fulfilled in the body of Christ where there is neither Jew nor Gentile (Galatians 3:28). At Babel, human unity apart from God was shattered. The gospel restores true unity—not through human effort but through reconciliation in Christ, who makes all one.
