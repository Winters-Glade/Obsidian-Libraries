# Spiritual Gifts

## Definition

Grace-given abilities (charismata) bestowed by the Holy Spirit upon believers for the edification of the church and the work of ministry. These include gifts of teaching, leadership, service, prophecy, healing, tongues, administration, and others. All gifts are to be exercised in love for the common good.
