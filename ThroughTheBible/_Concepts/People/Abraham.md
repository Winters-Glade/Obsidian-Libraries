# Abraham

## Description

Abraham is the father of the Hebrew nation and the central patriarch of the Old Testament. Chuck Smith highlights Abraham as the supreme example of justification by faith, citing Genesis 15:6 where Abraham believed God and it was counted to him as righteousness. Abraham's willingness to offer Isaac on Mount Moriah is seen as a type of God the Father offering His Son Jesus Christ. His life illustrates the walk of faith, including obedience, intercessory prayer, and trusting God's promises despite impossible circumstances.
