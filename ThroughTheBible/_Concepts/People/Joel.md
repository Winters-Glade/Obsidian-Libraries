# Joel

## Description

Joel prophesied to Judah, using a devastating locust plague as a metaphor for the coming Day of the Lord. His book contains the famous prophecy quoted by Peter at Pentecost: the outpouring of the Holy Spirit on all flesh (Joel 2:28-32). Joel calls for repentance and promises restoration.
