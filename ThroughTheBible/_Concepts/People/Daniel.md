# Daniel

## Description

Daniel was a Jewish exile who served in the courts of Babylonian and Persian kings. Known for his faithfulness to God in a pagan culture, his book contains both historical narratives (fiery furnace, lion's den) and apocalyptic visions of future kingdoms and the end times. His prophecies provide a timeline for the coming of the Messiah.
