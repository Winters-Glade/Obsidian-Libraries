# Jehoshaphat

## Description

Jehoshaphat was the king of Judah who sought the Lord and implemented religious reforms. Chuck Smith highlights his prayer when facing the Moabite-Ammonite alliance, where God instructed them to 'stand still and see the salvation of the Lord.' His alliance with wicked Ahab, however, serves as a warning about unequal yoking with unbelievers.
