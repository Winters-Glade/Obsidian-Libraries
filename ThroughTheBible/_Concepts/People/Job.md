# Job

## Description

Job is the central figure of the book of Job, described as a blameless and upright man who feared God. He endured devastating loss and suffering while maintaining his integrity. His story explores the problem of innocent suffering and the sovereignty of God, culminating in a direct encounter with the Almighty.
