# Jacob/Israel

## Description

Jacob/Israel is referenced in the T2000 Topical Series by Pastor Chuck Smith. Jacob (later renamed Israel by God) was the patriarch of the twelve tribes of Israel. His life, recorded in Genesis 25-50, reveals God's sovereign choice and transforming grace.
