# Mighty Men

The elite warriors of King David, also known as David's Gibborim or mighty warriors (2 Samuel 23, 1 Chronicles 11-12). These were roughly 30-37 distinguished fighters who performed extraordinary feats of valor. Their loyalty and skill established and secured David's kingdom.
