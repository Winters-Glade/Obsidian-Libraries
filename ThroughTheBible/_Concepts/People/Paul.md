# Paul

## Description

Paul, formerly Saul of Tarsus, was the apostle to the Gentiles and the author of thirteen New Testament epistles. Chuck Smith's teaching is deeply Pauline, emphasizing justification by faith, grace, the indwelling Holy Spirit, the Body of Christ, and the believer's position in Christ. Paul's conversion on the Damascus road, his missionary journeys, his prison epistles, and his unwavering commitment to the gospel are central themes. Pastor Smith often highlights Paul's transition from the law to grace and his revelation of the church as the Body of Christ.
