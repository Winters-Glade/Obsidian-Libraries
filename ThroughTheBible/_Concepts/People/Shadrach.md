# Shadrach

One of three Hebrew youths along with Meshach and Abednego who refused to bow to Nebuchadnezzar's golden image in Babylon (Daniel 3). They were thrown into a fiery furnace but miraculously preserved by God. Their faithful defiance is a classic example of standing firm under pressure.
