# Zephaniah

## Description

Zephaniah prophesied during the reign of King Josiah of Judah. His message focuses on the coming Day of the Lord as a time of judgment on Judah and the nations, but also promises restoration and rejoicing. He concludes with the joyful image of God singing over His redeemed people (Zephaniah 3:17).
