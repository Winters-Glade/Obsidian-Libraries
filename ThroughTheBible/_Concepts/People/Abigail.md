# Abigail

## Description

Abigail was the wise and beautiful wife of Nabal who intervened to prevent David from taking vengeance. Chuck Smith highlights Abigail's wisdom, diplomacy, and faith in God's plan for David's future kingship. She became one of David's wives after Nabal's death.
