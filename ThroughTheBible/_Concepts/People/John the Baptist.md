# John the Baptist

## Description

John the Baptist was the forerunner of Jesus, prophesied by Isaiah, who prepared the way for the Messiah by calling Israel to repentance. Chuck Smith highlights John's humility in declaring 'He must increase, I must decrease,' and his boldness in confronting sin. John's ministry of baptism pointed to the greater baptism of the Holy Spirit that Jesus would bring.
