# The Eleven Apostles

The remaining eleven apostles of Jesus after Judas Iscariot's betrayal and death. They were the foundational witnesses of Christ's resurrection and the recipients of the Great Commission. After the Emmaus road encounter, the two disciples returned to find the Eleven gathered in Jerusalem.
