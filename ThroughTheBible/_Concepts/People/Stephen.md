# Stephen

## Description

Stephen was one of the first deacons and the first Christian martyr. Chuck Smith highlights Stephen's powerful sermon before the Sanhedrin, which traced God's redemptive history and indicted Israel's leaders for rejecting the Righteous One. His vision of Jesus standing at the right hand of God and his prayer for his persecutors echo Christ's own death.
