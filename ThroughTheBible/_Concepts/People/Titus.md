# Titus

## Description

Titus was a Greek believer and trusted companion of Paul. He served as Paul's representative to the church in Corinth and later was left in Crete to set in order the churches there. Paul's letter to Titus provides instructions for church leadership and sound doctrine, emphasizing good works as the fruit of faith.
