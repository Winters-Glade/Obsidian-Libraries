# Herod the Great

## Description

Herod the Great was the Roman-appointed king of Judea who built the Second Temple and attempted to kill the infant Jesus. Chuck Smith notes Herod's political cunning, his massive building projects, and his paranoid cruelty as background to the Nativity narrative.
