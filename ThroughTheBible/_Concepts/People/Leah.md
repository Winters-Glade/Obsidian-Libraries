# Leah

## Description

Leah was the elder daughter of Laban and the first wife of Jacob, given to him through Laban's deception. Though Jacob loved Rachel more, Leah bore him six sons (Reuben, Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Zebulun) and a daughter (Dinah). Through her son Judah, she became an ancestor of King David and Jesus Christ. Her story illustrates God's compassion for the overlooked.
