# Josiah

## Description

Josiah was the king of Judah who rediscovered the Book of the Law and led a great revival. Chuck Smith presents Josiah as an example of what happens when God's Word is recovered and obeyed. His reforms cleansed the land of idolatry, though judgment was only delayed, not averted.
