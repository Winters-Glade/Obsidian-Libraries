# Amasai

A leader of David's mighty men who switched allegiance from Saul's house to David at Hebron (1 Chronicles 12:18). Moved by the Spirit, he declared loyalty to David, becoming chief of the thirty captains. His name appears among the warriors who helped make David's kingdom secure.
