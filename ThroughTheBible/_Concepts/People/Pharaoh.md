# Pharaoh

## Description

Pharaoh was the king of Egypt who opposed Moses and God's command to let Israel go. Chuck Smith presents Pharaoh as an example of the hardened heart that resists God. The ten plagues demonstrated God's power over the gods of Egypt and His determination to redeem His people. Pharaoh's stubbornness ultimately led to the destruction of his army at the Red Sea.
