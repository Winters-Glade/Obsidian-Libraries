# Zerubbabel

## Description

Zerubbabel was the governor of Judah who led the first group of exiles back to Jerusalem and oversaw the rebuilding of the Temple. Chuck Smith teaches Zerubbabel's role in fulfilling Haggai and Zechariah's prophecies. He is part of the genealogy of Jesus Christ in both Matthew and Luke.
