# All Humanity

## Description

All Humanity refers to the entire human race descended from Adam and Eve. Scripture teaches that all humanity shares a common origin in Adam, a common sin nature, and a common need for redemption. Chuck Smith emphasizes that the gospel is for all people regardless of race, nationality, or status — 'God so loved the world' (John 3:16). The unity of the human race is established in creation and restored in Christ.
