# Moses

## Description

Moses was the great lawgiver who led Israel out of Egyptian bondage and received the Law at Mount Sinai. Chuck Smith presents Moses as a type of Christ — a prophet whom God raised up, a mediator between God and the people, and a faithful servant in God's house. The Law given through Moses serves as a schoolmaster to bring us to Christ, revealing sin and driving us to grace. Moses' encounter with God at the burning bush, the plagues, the Red Sea crossing, and the wilderness journey are key episodes that demonstrate God's power and faithfulness.
