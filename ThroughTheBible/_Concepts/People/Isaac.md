# Isaac

## Description

Isaac was the long-promised son of Abraham and Sarah, and the second patriarch. Chuck Smith teaches Isaac as a type of Christ — the beloved son offered on Mount Moriah. Isaac's quiet trust, his marriage to Rebekah, and his life as a sojourner in the land of promise illustrate the life of faith.
