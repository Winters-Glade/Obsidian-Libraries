# Mephibosheth

## Description

Mephibosheth was the crippled son of Jonathan, to whom David showed covenantal kindness for Jonathan's sake. Chuck Smith uses this account as a picture of God's grace — we, like Mephibosheth, are crippled and unworthy, yet invited to eat at the King's table because of the covenant.
