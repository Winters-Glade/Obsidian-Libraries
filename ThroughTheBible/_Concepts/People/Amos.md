# Amos

## Description

Amos was a shepherd and fig farmer from Tekoa whom God called to prophesy to the northern kingdom of Israel during the 8th century BC. His message pronounced judgment on the nations and Israel for social injustice and empty religiosity. He famously declared, 'Let justice roll down like waters' (Amos 5:24).
