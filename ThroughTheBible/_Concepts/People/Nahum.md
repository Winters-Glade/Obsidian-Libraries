# Nahum

## Description

Nahum prophesied against Nineveh, the capital of Assyria, about 100 years after Jonah's mission. While Jonah showed God's mercy, Nahum proclaims God's judgment. His book vividly describes the fall of Nineveh and affirms that God is slow to anger but will not leave the guilty unpunished.
