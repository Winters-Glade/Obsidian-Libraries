# Matthias

## Description

Matthias was chosen by the eleven apostles to replace Judas Iscariot after his betrayal and death (Acts 1:15-26). He had been a follower of Jesus from the beginning of His ministry and was a witness of the resurrection. The choice was made by casting lots after prayer, fulfilling the prophecy in Psalm 109:8 that another would take Judas' office.
