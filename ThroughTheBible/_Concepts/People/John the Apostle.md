# John the Apostle

## Description

John the Apostle was the beloved disciple of Jesus, author of the fourth Gospel, three epistles, and the Book of Revelation. Chuck Smith emphasizes John's unique perspective on the deity of Christ, his emphasis on love, and the prophetic visions given to him on Patmos. John's writings are treasured for their depth of spiritual insight.
