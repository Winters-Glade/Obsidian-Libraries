# Elisha

## Description

Elisha succeeded Elijah as prophet in Israel, ministering with a double portion of his spirit. Chuck Smith notes the many miracles Elisha performed, including healing Naaman of leprosy, multiplying oil for the widow, and raising the Shunammite's son. His ministry demonstrates God's compassion and power working through a faithful servant.
