# Deborah

## Description

Deborah was a prophetess and the only female judge of Israel. She led Israel to victory over Canaanite oppression under King Jabin, alongside Barak. Her song of deliverance (Judges 5) is one of the oldest poetic passages in Scripture. She exemplifies godly leadership and courage.
