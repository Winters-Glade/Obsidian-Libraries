# Shunammite Woman

The wealthy woman from Shunem who showed hospitality to the prophet Elisha by preparing a room for him (2 Kings 4:8-37). In gratitude, Elisha prophesied she would bear a son, and when the child later died, she sought Elisha who raised him back to life. Her persistent faith is a model of determination.
