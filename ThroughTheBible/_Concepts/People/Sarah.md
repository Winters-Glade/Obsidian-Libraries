# Sarah

## Description

Sarah was the wife of Abraham and mother of Isaac. Chuck Smith notes Sarah's journey from barrenness to faith, her initial laughter at God's promise, and her eventual role as the mother of the promised seed. Peter holds Sarah up as an example of holy women who trusted God.
