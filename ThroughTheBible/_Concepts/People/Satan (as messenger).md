# Satan (as messenger)

The reference in 2 Corinthians 12:7 where Paul describes a 'thorn in the flesh' as a messenger of Satan sent to buffet him. Paul understood this affliction as something permitted by God despite its satanic origin, and Pastor Chuck Smith taught that God uses even the enemy's attacks to accomplish His purposes of humility and grace in believers.
