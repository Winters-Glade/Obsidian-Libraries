# Elihu

## Description

Elihu the Buzite was a younger man who spoke to Job after the three friends had finished. He was angry at both Job for justifying himself rather than God and at the three friends for failing to answer Job. Elihu's speeches emphasize that God uses suffering to teach and discipline, preparing the way for God's own appearance. God did not rebuke Elihu as He did the three friends.
