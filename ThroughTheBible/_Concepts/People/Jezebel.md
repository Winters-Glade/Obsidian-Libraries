# Jezebel

## Description

Jezebel was the Phoenician wife of King Ahab who promoted Baal worship in Israel and persecuted the prophets of God. Chuck Smith presents Jezebel as the epitome of wickedness and idolatry. She is referenced in Revelation as a symbolic name for a false prophetess who leads God's people into immorality and idolatry.
