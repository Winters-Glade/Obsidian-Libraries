# Jonah

## Description

Jonah was a reluctant prophet sent to Nineveh, the capital of Assyria. His story illustrates God's compassion for all nations and His sovereignty over creation. After fleeing from God's call, being swallowed by a great fish, and being vomited onto dry land, Jonah reluctantly preached to Nineveh, and the entire city repented.
