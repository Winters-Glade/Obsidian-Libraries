# Barabbas

The notorious prisoner whom Pontius Pilate released instead of Jesus at the crowd's demand during Passover (Matthew 27:15-26, Mark 15:6-15). A rebel and murderer, Barabbas represents the guilty sinner set free while the innocent Christ took his place—a powerful picture of substitutionary atonement.
