# Micah

## Description

Micah was an 8th century BC prophet from Moresheth who prophesied to both Israel and Judah. His book contains the famous prophecy of Bethlehem as the birthplace of the Messiah (Micah 5:2) and the classic summary of true religion: 'to do justly, to love mercy, and to walk humbly with your God' (Micah 6:8).
