# Zophar

## Description

Zophar the Naamathite was the third of Job's three friends, known for being the most harsh and dogmatic in his speeches. He insisted that Job deserved even greater punishment than he received, accusing him of getting less than his sins merited. His rigid theology allowed no room for innocent suffering, and God later rebuked him alongside the other friends.
