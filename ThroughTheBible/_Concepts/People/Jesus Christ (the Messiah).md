# Jesus Christ (the Messiah)

Jesus of Nazareth, recognized in Christian faith as the Messiah (Christ) prophesied in the Old Testament. He is the central figure of Christianity, whose life, death, and resurrection are the foundation of salvation. Pastor Chuck Smith consistently taught that Jesus is the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecy, the Son of God, and the only way to salvation.
