# Elijah

## Description

Elijah was a mighty prophet of God who confronted the worship of Baal in Israel during the reign of Ahab and Jezebel. Chuck Smith highlights Elijah's dramatic victory on Mount Carmel as a demonstration of God's power over false gods. Elijah's subsequent depression and flight to Horeb reveals the humanity of God's servants. He appeared with Moses at the Transfiguration of Jesus.
