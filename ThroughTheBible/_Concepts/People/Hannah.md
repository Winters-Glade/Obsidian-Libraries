# Hannah

## Description

Hannah was the mother of Samuel, whose fervent prayer for a son was answered by God. Chuck Smith uses Hannah's prayer as a model of heartfelt petition and her song of thanksgiving as a prototype of Mary's Magnificat. Her dedication of Samuel to the Lord exemplifies wholehearted commitment.
