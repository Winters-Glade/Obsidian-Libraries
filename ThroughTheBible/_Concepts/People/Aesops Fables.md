# Aesop's Fables

A collection of moral fables attributed to Aesop, a Greek storyteller of the 6th century BC. Pastor Chuck Smith referenced these in his teaching as illustrations of practical wisdom. While not biblical, they offer simple parables about human nature that can complement scriptural teaching.
