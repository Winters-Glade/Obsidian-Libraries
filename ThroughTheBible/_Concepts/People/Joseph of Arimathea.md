# Joseph of Arimathea

## Description

Joseph of Arimathea was a wealthy member of the Sanhedrin and a secret disciple of Jesus. After Jesus' crucifixion, he boldly asked Pilate for Jesus' body. He provided the tomb where Jesus was buried, fulfilling Isaiah 53:9. His actions demonstrate that not all religious leaders rejected Jesus, and his provision of the tomb set the stage for the resurrection.
