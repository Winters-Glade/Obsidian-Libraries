# Rahab

## Description

Rahab was a Canaanite prostitute in Jericho who hid the Israelite spies and helped them escape. In return, she and her family were spared when Jericho fell. She is listed in the genealogy of Jesus Christ (Matthew 1:5) and is commended for her faith in Hebrews 11:31 and James 2:25. Her story demonstrates God's grace extending to all who believe.
