# Nicodemus

## Description

Nicodemus was a Pharisee and a member of the Jewish ruling council (the Sanhedrin). He came to Jesus at night to learn from Him, leading to Jesus' teaching on the new birth (John 3). He later defended Jesus before the Sanhedrin (John 7:50-52) and assisted Joseph of Arimathea in preparing Jesus' body for burial (John 19:39).
