# King of Israel

The rulers of the Northern Kingdom of Israel after the kingdom divided following Solomon's reign. In the context of 2 Kings 6, Jehoram (or Joram) was the king of Israel during Elisha's ministry, who received strategic intelligence through the prophet about Syrian military movements.
