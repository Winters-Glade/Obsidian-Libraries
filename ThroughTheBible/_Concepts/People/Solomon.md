# Solomon

## Description

Solomon was David's son, the wisest king of Israel, who built the Temple in Jerusalem. Chuck Smith examines Solomon's wisdom, his wealth, and his many wives who turned his heart away from God. Solomon's writings include Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and Song of Solomon. His life is both an example of God-given wisdom and a warning about the dangers of prosperity, compromise, and divided loyalties.
