# Potiphar

## Description

Potiphar was the Egyptian officer who purchased Joseph as a slave. Chuck Smith recounts how Joseph's faithfulness in Potiphar's house led to his advancement, and how Potiphar's wife's false accusation led to Joseph's imprisonment — all part of God's sovereign plan.
