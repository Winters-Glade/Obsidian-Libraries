# Joshua

## Description

Joshua was Moses' assistant and successor who led Israel into the Promised Land. His name means 'Yahweh saves.' He led the conquest of Canaan, dividing the land among the twelve tribes. His life exemplifies faithful leadership and courage, and his charge to Israel culminates in the famous declaration: 'As for me and my house, we will serve the Lord' (Joshua 24:15).
