# Ishmael

## Description

Ishmael was Abraham's son through Hagar. Chuck Smith notes Paul's allegory in Galatians where Ishmael, born according to the flesh, represents those who seek righteousness through works of the law, in contrast to Isaac, born through promise, who represents those who receive righteousness by faith.
