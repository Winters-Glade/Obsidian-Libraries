# Bathsheba

## Description

Bathsheba was the wife of Uriah the Hittite whom David took in adultery. Chuck Smith examines the complicated story of David and Bathsheba, the subsequent repentance of David, and Bathsheba's role as the mother of Solomon, through whom the Messianic line continued.
