# Boaz

## Description

Boaz was a wealthy landowner of Bethlehem who became the kinsman-redeemer for Ruth, a Moabite widow. He demonstrated godly character through his kindness, integrity, and faithfulness to the law of levirate marriage. He is an ancestor of King David and Jesus Christ, and his story illustrates God's redemptive love and the inclusion of Gentiles in His plan.
