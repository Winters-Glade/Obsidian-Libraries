# Gehazi

The servant and attendant of the prophet Elisha. Gehazi appears in 2 Kings 4-8 as Elisha's personal assistant who witnessed many of his miraculous works, including the raising of the Shunammite woman's son. However, he later fell into greed and deception when he pursued Naaman for payment after Elisha had refused it, and was struck with leprosy as a consequence.
