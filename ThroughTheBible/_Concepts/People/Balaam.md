# Balaam

## Description

Balaam was a non-Israelite prophet hired by King Balak of Moab to curse Israel. Despite intending to curse them, God compelled him to bless Israel instead. He later devised a plan to lead Israel into sin through Moabite women, and is remembered in Scripture as a negative example of someone motivated by greed.
