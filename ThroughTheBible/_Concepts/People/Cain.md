# Cain

## Description

Cain was the firstborn son of Adam and Eve and the first murderer. Out of jealousy, he killed his brother Abel when God accepted Abel's offering but not his own. God marked Cain to protect him from vengeance and sent him to wander east of Eden. His story illustrates the danger of unchecked sin and the gravity of human choice.
