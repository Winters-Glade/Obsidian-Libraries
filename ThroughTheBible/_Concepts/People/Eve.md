# Eve

## Description

Eve was the first woman, created by God from Adam's side. Chuck Smith teaches that Eve's deception by the serpent introduced sin into the human race. Her role in the Fall is examined alongside the promise of a Seed who would crush the serpent's head — the first gospel prophecy pointing to Jesus Christ.
