# Isaiah

## Description

Isaiah was the great prophet of the 8th century BC who ministered in Judah during the reigns of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, and Hezekiah. His book contains some of the most profound messianic prophecies in the Old Testament, including the virgin birth (7:14), the suffering servant (53), and the new heavens and new earth (65-66). His vision of God's holiness in chapter 6 shaped his entire ministry.
