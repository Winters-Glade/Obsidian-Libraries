# Agur

The son of Jakeh, identified as the author of Proverbs 30. His sayings include a confession of humility ('I am more brutish than any man'), numerical proverbs about four things too wonderful, and wisdom about the proper balance of wealth and poverty. Agur's words reveal profound spiritual insight.
