# Meshach

One of Daniel's three companions who, with Shadrach and Abednego, refused to worship Nebuchadnezzar's golden idol and was cast into the fiery furnace (Daniel 3). God delivered them so completely that they emerged without even the smell of smoke, demonstrating His power over earthly rulers.
