# The women at the cross

The faithful women followers of Jesus who stood near the cross during His crucifixion, including Mary the mother of Jesus, Mary Magdalene, and Mary the wife of Clopas (John 19:25, Mark 15:40-41). Their courageous presence at the cross while most disciples had fled exemplifies devoted discipleship.
