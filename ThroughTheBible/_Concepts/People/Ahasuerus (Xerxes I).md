# Ahasuerus (Xerxes I)

The Persian king who reigned from 486-465 BC and is the central royal figure in the book of Esther. Known historically as Xerxes I, he ruled from India to Ethiopia over 127 provinces, and his empire formed the backdrop for the story of Esther, Mordecai, and Haman. His summer palace was located in Susa (Shushan).
