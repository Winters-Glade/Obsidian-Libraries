# Hagar

## Description

Hagar was Sarah's Egyptian maidservant who bore Ishmael to Abraham. Chuck Smith uses Paul's allegory in Galatians where Hagar represents the covenant of Mount Sinai, bondage to the law, and the earthly Jerusalem. Her story illustrates the contrast between flesh and spirit, law and grace.
