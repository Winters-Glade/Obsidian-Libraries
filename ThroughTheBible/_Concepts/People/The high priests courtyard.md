# The high priest's courtyard

The location in the high priest's residence where Peter denied knowing Jesus three times before the rooster crowed (Matthew 26:69-75, Mark 14:66-72). This is the setting of Peter's deepest failure and the moment Jesus looked at him, causing Peter to weep bitterly—a scene of profound repentance.
