# Chuck Smith

## Description

Charles Ward Smith (1927–2013) was the founder and senior pastor of Calvary Chapel Costa Mesa and the leader of the Jesus Movement of the 1960s–70s. Known affectionately as "Pastor Chuck," he was a verse-by-verse expository teacher of the Bible whose "Through the Bible" (C2000/T2000) radio and tape ministry has reached millions worldwide. His teaching style emphasized letting Scripture speak for itself, with a focus on the plain meaning of the text, a premillennial and dispensational framework, and the practical application of God's Word to daily life. He shepherded the Calvary Chapel movement into a global fellowship of churches characterized by verse-by-verse teaching, contemporary worship, and an emphasis on the work of the Holy Spirit. His expository series through the entire Bible is preserved in the C2000 (Through the Bible) and T2000 (Thru the Bible) collections archived in this vault. His legacy includes mentoring many notable teachers and pastors, and his teachings continue to be distributed through The Word For Today and the Calvary Chapel distribution network.
