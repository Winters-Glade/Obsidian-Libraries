# Onesimus

## Description

Onesimus was a runaway slave who belonged to Philemon, a Christian in Colossae. He met Paul in prison and became a believer. Paul sent him back to Philemon with the letter bearing his name, appealing to Philemon to receive Onesimus not as a slave but as a beloved brother. The name Onesimus means 'useful,' and Paul wrote that he had become truly useful to both of them.
