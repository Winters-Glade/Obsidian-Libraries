# Habakkuk

## Description

Habakkuk was a prophet who wrestled with the problem of evil, questioning how God could use the wicked Babylonians to judge Judah. His book is structured as a dialogue with God and concludes with a beautiful affirmation of faith: 'Yet I will rejoice in the Lord' (Habakkuk 3:18).
