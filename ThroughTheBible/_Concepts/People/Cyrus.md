# Cyrus

## Description

Cyrus the Great was the Persian king who conquered Babylon and issued the decree allowing the Jewish exiles to return to Jerusalem. Chuck Smith emphasizes Cyrus as a striking example of God's sovereignty over world rulers — specifically named in Isaiah's prophecy over a century before his birth. Cyrus is a type of God's ability to use even pagan rulers to accomplish His redemptive purposes.
