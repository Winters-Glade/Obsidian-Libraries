# Satan

## Description

Satan is the adversary, the devil, who opposes God and His people. Chuck Smith teaches about Satan's origin as a fallen angel, his role as the tempter, and his ultimate defeat at the cross of Christ. Believers are called to resist Satan through the armor of God and the power of the Holy Spirit. His final judgment is certain.
