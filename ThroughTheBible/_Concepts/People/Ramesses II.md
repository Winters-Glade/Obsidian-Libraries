# Ramesses II

## Description

Ramesses II was the powerful Egyptian pharaoh often associated with the oppression of Israel preceding the Exodus. Chuck Smith references Ramesses in discussing the historical dating of the Exodus and the archaeological evidence supporting the biblical narrative.
