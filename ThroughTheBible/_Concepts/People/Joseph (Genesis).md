# Joseph (Genesis)

The son of Jacob and Rachel who was sold into slavery by his brothers but rose to become prime minister of Egypt (Genesis 37-50). Despite suffering betrayal, false accusation, and imprisonment, Joseph remained faithful to God and eventually preserved his family through a severe famine, becoming a type of Christ in his redemptive role.
