# Philip the Evangelist

## Description

Philip the Evangelist was one of the seven deacons chosen to serve the early church in Jerusalem (Acts 6). He preached the gospel in Samaria with great success and was guided by the Holy Spirit to explain the Scriptures to the Ethiopian eunuch, whom he baptized. He later settled in Caesarea and hosted Paul on his final journey to Jerusalem.
