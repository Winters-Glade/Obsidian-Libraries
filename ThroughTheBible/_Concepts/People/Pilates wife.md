# Pilate's wife

The wife of Pontius Pilate who sent a warning to her husband during Jesus' trial, urging him to have nothing to do with 'that just man' because she had suffered greatly in a dream because of Him (Matthew 27:19). Her testimony stands as an ironic witness from a Gentile woman to Christ's innocence.
