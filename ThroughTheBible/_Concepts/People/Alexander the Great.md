# Alexander the Great

## Description

Alexander the Great was the Macedonian king who conquered the ancient world and spread Greek language and culture. Chuck Smith notes Alexander's role in fulfilling Daniel's prophecy of the third world empire (the bronze belly and thighs of Nebuchadnezzar's statue). His conquest prepared the way for the spread of the gospel by establishing a common Greek language throughout the Mediterranean world.
