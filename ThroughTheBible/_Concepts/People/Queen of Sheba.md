# Queen of Sheba

## Description

The Queen of Sheba visited Solomon to test his wisdom with hard questions. Chuck Smith references Jesus's use of her visit as a contrast to those who reject One greater than Solomon. Her acknowledgment of Solomon's wisdom points to Christ, the embodiment of divine wisdom.
