# Jeremiah

## Description

Jeremiah was the prophet who ministered during the final years of Judah before the Babylonian exile. Known as the weeping prophet, his message called Judah to repentance and warned of coming judgment. He witnessed the fall of Jerusalem and wrote Lamentations. His ministry illustrates faithful proclamation despite rejection and persecution.
