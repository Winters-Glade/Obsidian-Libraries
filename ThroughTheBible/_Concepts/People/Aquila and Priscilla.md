# Aquila and Priscilla

## Description

Aquila and Priscilla were a Jewish Christian couple who were colleagues of the Apostle Paul in tentmaking and ministry. Chuck Smith highlights them as a model of faithful partnership in ministry, risking their lives for Paul and instructing Apollos in the way of God more accurately. Their home served as a house church in both Ephesus and Rome.
