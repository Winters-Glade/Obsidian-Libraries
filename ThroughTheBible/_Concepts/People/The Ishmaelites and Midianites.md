# The Ishmaelites/Midianites

The traders to whom Joseph was sold by his brothers (Genesis 37:25-28, 39:1). The terms Ishmaelites and Midianites appear interchangeably in the narrative—both were desert trading peoples descended from Abraham. They were traveling from Gilead to Egypt with spices when they purchased Joseph for twenty shekels of silver.
