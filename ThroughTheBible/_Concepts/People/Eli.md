# Eli

## Description

Eli was the high priest and judge of Israel who mentored the young Samuel. Chuck Smith teaches that Eli's failure to restrain his corrupt sons led to God's judgment on his household. His life serves as a warning about the responsibility of spiritual leadership and the danger of honoring one's children above God.
