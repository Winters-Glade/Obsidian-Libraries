# Apollos

## Description

Apollos was an eloquent Jewish Christian from Alexandria who taught powerfully about Jesus. Chuck Smith notes that Apollos was instructed more accurately by Aquila and Priscilla, demonstrating the importance of discipleship and sound doctrine. He became a significant leader in the early church, particularly in Corinth, though his popularity sometimes created factions.
