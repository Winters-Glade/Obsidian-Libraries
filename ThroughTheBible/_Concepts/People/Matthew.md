# Matthew (Levi)

## Description

Matthew, also called Levi, was a tax collector whom Jesus called to follow Him. He became one of the twelve apostles and is traditionally credited with writing the Gospel of Matthew. His Gospel emphasizes Jesus as the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecy and presents Him as the promised Messiah and King.
