# Gamaliel

## Description

Gamaliel was a respected Pharisee and teacher of the law, a member of the Sanhedrin. He was the teacher of the Apostle Paul (Acts 22:3). When the apostles were put on trial, Gamaliel counseled the Sanhedrin to leave them alone, reasoning that if their movement was of human origin it would fail, but if it was of God it could not be stopped (Acts 5:34-39).
