# Joseph

## Description

Joseph was the son of Jacob, sold into slavery by his brothers, who rose to become prime minister of Egypt. Chuck Smith presents Joseph as the clearest type of Christ in the Old Testament: beloved of his father, rejected by his brothers, falsely accused, exalted to the throne, and extending grace to those who wronged him. Joseph's statement that what his enemies meant for evil, God meant for good, encapsulates God's sovereign providence.
