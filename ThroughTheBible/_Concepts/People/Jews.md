# Jews

## Description

Jews (from Judah) are the descendants of Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, God's chosen people through whom the Messiah came into the world. Throughout the T2000 series, Chuck Smith traces the history of the Jewish people — their calling, their rebellion, their exile, and their preservation. He emphasizes that God's covenant with Israel remains irrevocable (Romans 11:29) and that the Jewish people continue to play a central role in God's redemptive plan, with a future restoration yet to come.
