# Hezekiah

## Description

Hezekiah was the king of Judah who instituted major religious reforms and trusted God when Assyria threatened Jerusalem. Chuck Smith highlights Hezekiah's prayer during the siege and God's miraculous deliverance as a testimony to faith. However, his later pride and the incident with the Babylonian envoys serve as a warning.
