# Noah

## Description

Noah was the righteous man who found grace in God's eyes and built the ark for the salvation of his household. Chuck Smith teaches Noah as a type of Christ — the ark as a picture of salvation through faith alone. The flood judgment prefigures the final judgment, and the rainbow covenant demonstrates God's mercy. Jesus referenced the days of Noah as illustrating the conditions at His return.
