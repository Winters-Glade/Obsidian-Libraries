# Mordecai

## Description

Mordecai was a Jewish man living in Susa who raised his cousin Esther and became a key figure in the deliverance of the Jewish people from Haman's plot. His refusal to bow to Haman triggered the crisis, and his wisdom and faith led to the salvation of his people. He later became second-in-command to King Ahasuerus.
