# James (Brother of Jesus)

## Description

James was a leader of the early church in Jerusalem and the author of the Epistle of James. He was a half-brother of Jesus (son of Mary and Joseph), initially skeptical of Jesus' ministry but became a believer after the resurrection. He presided over the Jerusalem Council (Acts 15) and his epistle emphasizes faith demonstrated by works.
