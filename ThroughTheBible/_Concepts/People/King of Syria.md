# King of Syria

The various kings of Aram (Syria) who interacted with Israel during the period of the divided kingdom. In 2 Kings 6, the unnamed King of Syria sent military forces to capture Elisha at Dothan after the prophet repeatedly revealed his military plans to the King of Israel. Notable Syrian kings include Ben-Hadad I, Ben-Hadad II, and Hazael.
