# Abednego

One of the three faithful Hebrew men in Babylon who, with Shadrach and Meshach, was thrown into Nebuchadnezzar's furnace for refusing to worship a golden image (Daniel 3). A fourth figure 'like a son of the gods' appeared with them in the fire, testifying to God's presence with His people in trial.
