# Thomas

## Description

Thomas, also called Didymus ('the twin'), was one of the twelve apostles. He is best known for doubting Jesus' resurrection until he could see and touch His wounds, leading to Jesus' appearance and Thomas' confession: 'My Lord and my God!' (John 20:28). His doubt gave way to one of the strongest declarations of Christ's deity.
