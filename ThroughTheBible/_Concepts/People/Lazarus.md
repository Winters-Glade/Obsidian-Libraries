# Lazarus of Bethany

## Description

Lazarus was the brother of Mary and Martha of Bethany whom Jesus raised from the dead after four days in the tomb (John 11). This miracle was the climactic sign of Jesus' ministry that precipitated the plot to kill Him. The raising of Lazarus demonstrates Jesus' power over death and foreshadows His own resurrection. The name also appears in Jesus' parable of the rich man and Lazarus (Luke 16).
