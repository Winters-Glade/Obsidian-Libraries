# Gabriel

## Description

Gabriel is an archangel who appears in both the Old and New Testaments as a divine messenger. He interpreted Daniel's visions (Daniel 8-9), announced the birth of John the Baptist to Zechariah (Luke 1), and announced the birth of Jesus to Mary (Luke 1:26-38). His name means 'God is my strength' or 'mighty one of God.'
