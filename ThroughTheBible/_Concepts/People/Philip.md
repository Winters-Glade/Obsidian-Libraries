# Philip

## Description

Philip was one of the twelve apostles. He was from Bethsaida, the same town as Andrew and Peter. He is mentioned in the Gospels for calculating the cost of feeding the 5,000 and for asking Jesus to show them the Father, prompting Jesus' response: 'He who has seen Me has seen the Father' (John 14:8-9).
