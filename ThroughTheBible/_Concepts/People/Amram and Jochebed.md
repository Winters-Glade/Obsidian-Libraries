# Amram and Jochebed

The parents of Moses, Aaron, and Miriam from the tribe of Levi (Exodus 6:20, Numbers 26:59). Jochebed hid baby Moses for three months and placed him in a basket on the Nile, trusting God's providence. Their faith preserved the deliverer of Israel.
