# The Servant Girl

## Description

The servant girl was a young female servant of the high priest who confronted Peter in the courtyard of the high priest's residence on the night of Jesus' arrest. She recognized Peter as being with Jesus and asked if he was one of His disciples, leading to Peter's first denial of Christ. Chuck Smith uses this encounter to illustrate how fear and pressure can cause even the most devoted followers to stumble, while also showing God's grace in Peter's restoration.
