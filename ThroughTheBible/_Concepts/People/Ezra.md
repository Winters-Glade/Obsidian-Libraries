# Ezra

## Description

Ezra was a priest and scribe who led a group of exiles from Babylon to Jerusalem. He was a devoted student of the Law of God and led a spiritual revival by teaching the Scriptures to the returned exiles. His ministry emphasized the importance of God's Word in reforming and renewing the covenant community.
