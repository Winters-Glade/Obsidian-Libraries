# Eliphaz

## Description

Eliphaz the Temanite was one of Job's three friends who came to comfort him in his suffering. He was the first to speak, arguing that suffering is always a consequence of sin. His theological framework was based on his personal experience and observation, though God later declared that Eliphaz and his friends had not spoken correctly about Him (Job 42:7).
