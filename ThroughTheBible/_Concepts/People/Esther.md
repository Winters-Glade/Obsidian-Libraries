# Esther

## Description

Esther was a Jewish queen of Persia who risked her life to save her people from extermination. Her story demonstrates God's providential care for His people even when His name is not explicitly mentioned. Her cousin Mordecai's words apply to all: 'Who knows whether you have come to the kingdom for such a time as this?' (Esther 4:14).
