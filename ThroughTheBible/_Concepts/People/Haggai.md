# Haggai

## Description

Haggai was a post-exilic prophet who ministered after the return from Babylonian captivity. He called the people to rebuild the temple, which had been neglected while they built their own houses. His message emphasizes putting God's house first and promises that the glory of the latter house would be greater than the former.
