# David

## Description

David was the shepherd boy who became the greatest king of Israel, a man after God's own heart. Chuck Smith frequently uses David's life to illustrate the dynamics of grace, repentance, and worship. David's psalms are explored deeply for their prophetic references to Christ and their honest portrayal of the human experience. David's covenant (the Davidic covenant) points to the eternal kingdom of Jesus Christ, the Son of David. His failures—particularly with Bathsheba and Uriah—demonstrate the devastating consequences of sin and the beauty of genuine repentance.
