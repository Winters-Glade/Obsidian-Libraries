# Philemon

## Description

Philemon was a Christian in Colossae who hosted a church in his home. Paul wrote him a personal letter (the Epistle to Philemon) appealing on behalf of Onesimus, Philemon's runaway slave who had become a believer. Paul's letter is a masterpiece of Christian diplomacy, urging Philemon to receive Onesimus as a beloved brother while respecting Philemon's legal rights.
