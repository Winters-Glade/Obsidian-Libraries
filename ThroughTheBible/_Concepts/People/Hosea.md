# Hosea

## Description

Hosea was the first of the 8th century BC minor prophets, ministering to the northern kingdom of Israel. His marriage to Gomer served as a living parable of God's covenant relationship with unfaithful Israel. His book emphasizes God's enduring love despite Israel's spiritual adultery and calls for repentance.
