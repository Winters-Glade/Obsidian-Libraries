# The Emmaus Disciples

The two followers of Jesus who were walking to the village of Emmaus when the resurrected Christ joined them incognito (Luke 24). Their eyes were opened to recognize Him when He broke bread, and they immediately returned to Jerusalem to report the resurrection to the Eleven.
