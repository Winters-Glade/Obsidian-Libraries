# Gallio

## Description

Gallio was the proconsul of Achaia who dismissed the charges brought against Paul by the Jews in Corinth. Chuck Smith references the Gallio Inscription as archaeological evidence that dates Paul's ministry in Corinth, confirming the historical accuracy of Acts.
