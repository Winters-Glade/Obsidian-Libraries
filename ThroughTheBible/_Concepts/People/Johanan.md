# Johanan

A Jewish military leader during the time of the prophet Jeremiah, following the fall of Jerusalem to Babylon in 586 BC. After Gedaliah was appointed governor of Judah, Johanan warned him of an assassination plot and later led a group of refugees against Jeremiah's warning to flee to Egypt (Jeremiah 40-43), taking the prophet with them to Tahpanhes.
