# Martha

## Description

Martha was the sister of Mary and Lazarus of Bethany. She is known for her hospitality and service, as seen when she hosted Jesus in her home while Mary sat at His feet (Luke 10:38-42). When Lazarus died, she confessed her faith in Jesus as the Christ, the Son of God. Her story illustrates the balance between service and devotion.
