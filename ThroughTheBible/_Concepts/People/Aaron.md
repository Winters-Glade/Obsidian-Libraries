# Aaron

## Description

Aaron was the elder brother of Moses and the first high priest of Israel. In Chuck Smith's teaching, Aaron's priesthood serves as a type of the greater high priest, Jesus Christ. Aaron's role in the wilderness journey, including his involvement in the golden calf incident and his consecration as priest, demonstrates both human frailty and God's provision for mediation between Himself and His people.
