# Melchizedek

## Description

Melchizedek was the king-priest of Salem who blessed Abraham. Chuck Smith explains Melchizedek as a type of Christ — without recorded genealogy, without beginning or end of days, a priest of a different order than Aaron. The author of Hebrews develops this typology at length, showing Jesus as a High Priest forever after the order of Melchizedek.
