# Agagite

## Description

Agagite refers to Haman, the enemy of the Jews in the Book of Esther, who was called 'the Agagite.' The term likely connects Haman to Agag, the king of the Amalekites whom Saul failed to destroy. Chuck Smith notes that Haman's title 'Agagite' links him to the long-standing enmity between Israel and Amalek, making his plot against the Jews a continuation of ancient hostility that ultimately fails because God protects His covenant people.
