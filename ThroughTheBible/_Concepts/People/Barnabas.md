# Barnabas

## Description

Barnabas, known as the Son of Encouragement, was a Levite from Cyprus who was an early leader in the Jerusalem church. Chuck Smith highlights Barnabas's generosity in selling his land for the church, his role in vouching for Saul (Paul) when others feared him, and his missionary journeys with Paul. His ministry exemplifies encouragement and willingness to give second chances.
