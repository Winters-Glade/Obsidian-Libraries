# Cleopas

One of the two disciples walking to Emmaus on the day of Christ's resurrection who encountered the risen Jesus without recognizing Him (Luke 24:13-35). Jesus opened their eyes to understand the Scriptures concerning Himself, and they recognized Him in the breaking of bread—a pivotal post-resurrection appearance.
