# Bildad

## Description

Bildad the Shuhite was one of Job's three friends. He grounded his arguments in tradition and the wisdom of the ancients, insisting that God is just and therefore suffering must be punishment for sin. His speeches emphasize God's justice and the certainty that the wicked are punished, though this framework fails to account for innocent suffering.
