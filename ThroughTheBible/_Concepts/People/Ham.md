# Ham

## Description

Ham was one of the three sons of Noah. After the Flood, Ham saw his father's nakedness and told his brothers, leading to Noah's curse on Ham's son Canaan. Ham's descendants traditionally include the peoples of Africa and southern regions. The names of his sons—Cush, Mizraim (Egypt), Put, and Canaan—correspond to regions in Africa and the Near East.
