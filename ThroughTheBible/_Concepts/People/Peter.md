# Peter

## Description

Peter, also known as Simon or Cephas, was the leading apostle among Jesus's twelve disciples. Chuck Smith follows Peter's journey from an impulsive fisherman to the bold preacher of Pentecost. Peter's confession that Jesus is the Christ, his denial of the Lord, his restoration by the Sea of Galilee, and his vision on the rooftop in Joppa are key episodes. Peter's epistles emphasize suffering for Christ, holy living, and the certainty of Christ's return.
