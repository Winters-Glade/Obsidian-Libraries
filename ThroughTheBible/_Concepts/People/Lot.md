# Lot

## Description

Lot was Abraham's nephew who chose the well-watered plain of Jordan and settled in Sodom. Chuck Smith uses Lot as a warning about the gradual drift of a believer who pitches his tent toward Sodom. Lot is described as righteous but ineffective, saved 'so as by fire.' His wife's retrospective longing for Sodom serves as a warning about divided affections.
