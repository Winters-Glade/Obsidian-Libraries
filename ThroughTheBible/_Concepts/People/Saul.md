# Saul

## Description

Saul was the first king of Israel, chosen for his stature and strength but rejected for his disobedience. Chuck Smith uses Saul's life as a tragic example of how outward qualifications do not guarantee spiritual success. Saul's jealousy of David, his consulting the medium at Endor, and his tragic end illustrate the consequences of pride and disobedience.
