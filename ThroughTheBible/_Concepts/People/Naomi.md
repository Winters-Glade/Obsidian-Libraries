# Naomi

## Description

Naomi was an Israelite woman who went to Moab with her husband and sons during a famine. After losing her husband and both sons, she returned to Bethlehem bitter but accompanied by her loyal daughter-in-law Ruth. Through Ruth's marriage to Boaz, Naomi's lineage continued, and she became an ancestor of King David. Her story demonstrates God's restoration after suffering.
