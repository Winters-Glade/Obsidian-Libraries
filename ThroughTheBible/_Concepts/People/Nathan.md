# Nathan

## Description

Nathan was the prophet who served during David's reign. Chuck Smith recounts Nathan's courageous confrontation of David regarding his sin with Bathsheba and Uriah, delivering the parable of the ewe lamb. Nathan also delivered God's promise of an eternal dynasty to David — the Davidic covenant that points to the Messiah.
