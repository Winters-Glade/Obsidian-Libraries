# Jacob

## Description

Jacob, later named Israel, was the son of Isaac and Rebekah, the twin brother of Esau, and the father of the twelve tribes. Chuck Smith traces Jacob's transformation from a deceiver and supplanter to a prince who prevailed with God. His wrestling with the angel at Peniel, his vision at Bethel, and his reconciliation with Esau are key episodes. Jacob's life illustrates God's sovereign election and His power to transform character.
