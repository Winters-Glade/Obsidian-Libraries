# Bartholomew (Nathanael)

## Description

Bartholomew (also identified with Nathanael) was one of the twelve apostles. He was introduced to Jesus by Philip and was described by Jesus as 'an Israelite indeed, in whom there is no guile.' He was present at the resurrection appearance by the Sea of Galilee. Tradition holds that he preached in India and Armenia and was martyred by being flayed alive.
