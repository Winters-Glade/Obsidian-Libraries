# Zechariah

## Description

Zechariah was a post-exilic prophet who ministered alongside Haggai, encouraging the rebuilding of the temple. His book contains rich apocalyptic visions, messianic prophecies (including the Triumphal Entry and the piercing of the Messiah), and detailed descriptions of future restoration and the millennial kingdom.
