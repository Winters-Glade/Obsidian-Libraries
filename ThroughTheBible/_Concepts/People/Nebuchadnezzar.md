# Nebuchadnezzar

## Description

Nebuchadnezzar was the great king of Babylon who conquered Jerusalem and carried the Jews into exile. Chuck Smith emphasizes Nebuchadnezzar's role in Daniel's prophecies — his dream of the great statue representing the succession of world empires. God humbled Nebuchadnezzar by making him like a beast until he acknowledged that 'the Most High rules in the kingdom of men.' His restoration illustrates God's ability to humble the proud and exalt the humble.
