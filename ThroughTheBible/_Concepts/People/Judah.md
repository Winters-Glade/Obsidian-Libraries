# Judah

## Description

Judah was the fourth son of Jacob and Leah, and the founder of the tribe of Judah. He persuaded his brothers not to kill Joseph but to sell him into slavery. He later offered himself as a substitute for Benjamin in Egypt. The tribe of Judah became the leading tribe of Israel, producing King David and ultimately Jesus Christ. The southern kingdom of Judah took its name from this tribe. The name Judah means 'praise.'
