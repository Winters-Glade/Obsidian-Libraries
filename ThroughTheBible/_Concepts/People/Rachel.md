# Rachel

## Description

Rachel was the beloved wife of Jacob, mother of Joseph and Benjamin. Chuck Smith recounts Rachel's story — her beauty, her father Laban's deception, her struggle with barrenness, and her tragic death in childbirth. She is remembered in Jeremiah's prophecy of Rachel weeping for her children.
