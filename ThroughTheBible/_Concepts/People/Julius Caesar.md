# Julius Caesar

## Description

Julius Caesar was the Roman dictator whose assassination led to the rise of the Roman Empire under Augustus. Chuck Smith references Caesar in the context of the historical backdrop of the New Testament. The census under Caesar Augustus brought Mary and Joseph to Bethlehem, fulfilling Micah's prophecy of the Messiah's birthplace.
