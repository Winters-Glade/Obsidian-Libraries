# Ananias of Damascus

## Description

Ananias was the disciple in Damascus whom the Lord sent to restore sight to Saul of Tarsus and baptize him. Chuck Smith highlights Ananias's obedience despite his fear of Saul's reputation as a persecutor of the church.
