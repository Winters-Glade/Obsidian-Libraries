# Abel

## Description

Abel was the second son of Adam and Eve, a shepherd whose offering of a firstborn lamb was accepted by God. Chuck Smith emphasizes Abel's offering as the first biblical demonstration that approach to God requires a blood sacrifice, pointing to Christ as the ultimate Lamb of God. Abel is the first martyr and is commended in Hebrews 11 as a man of faith.
