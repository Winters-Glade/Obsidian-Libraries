# Caiaphas

## Description

Caiaphas was the Jewish high priest who presided over the trial of Jesus. He played a key role in the plot to arrest and condemn Jesus, famously stating that it was expedient for one man to die for the people (John 11:49-50). He was the son-in-law of Annas and served as high priest from approximately AD 18 to 36. He later also persecuted the early church.
