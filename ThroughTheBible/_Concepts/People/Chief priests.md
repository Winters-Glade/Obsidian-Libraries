# Chief priests

The senior priestly leadership in Jerusalem during the Second Temple period, including the high priest and leading families of the priestly aristocracy. They played a central role in the arrest, trial, and condemnation of Jesus, conspiring with the Sanhedrin to deliver Him to Pilate.
