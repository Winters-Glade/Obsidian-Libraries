# Ruth

## Description

Ruth was a Moabite woman who became an ancestor of King David and Jesus Christ. Her story exemplifies loyalty, faithfulness, and God's redemptive work among the nations. Her words to Naomi are among the most beloved in Scripture: 'Where you go, I will go... your people shall be my people, and your God my God' (Ruth 1:16).
