# Sennacherib

## Description

Sennacherib was the Assyrian king who invaded Judah and besieged Jerusalem during Hezekiah's reign. Chuck Smith highlights the miraculous deliverance when the Angel of the Lord destroyed 185,000 Assyrian soldiers, demonstrating God's power to save His people.
