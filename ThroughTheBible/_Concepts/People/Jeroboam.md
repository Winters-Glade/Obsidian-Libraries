# Jeroboam

## Description

Jeroboam was the first king of the northern kingdom of Israel after the division. Chuck Smith highlights Jeroboam's establishment of golden calves at Dan and Bethel as the sin that characterized the northern kingdom, leading to its eventual judgment.
