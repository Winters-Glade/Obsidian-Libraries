# Lydia

## Description

Lydia was a seller of purple cloth from Thyatira who became Paul's first convert in Philippi (Acts 16). She was a worshiper of God who responded to the gospel as Paul spoke by the riverside. She and her household were baptized, and she insisted that Paul and his companions stay at her home. Her home became the meeting place for the church in Philippi.
