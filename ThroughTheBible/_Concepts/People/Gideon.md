# Gideon

## Description

Gideon was a judge of Israel who delivered the nation from Midianite oppression. Despite his initial reluctance and requests for signs, he led 300 men to victory against a vast army, demonstrating that salvation comes from the Lord, not human strength. His story is recorded in Judges 6-8.
