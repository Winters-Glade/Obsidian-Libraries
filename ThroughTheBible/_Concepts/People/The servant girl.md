# The servant girl

The young Israelite maidservant who served Naaman's wife and compassionately told her master about the prophet Elisha in Samaria who could cure his leprosy (2 Kings 5:2-3). Despite being a captive taken from Israel in a Syrian raid, she demonstrated remarkable faith and kindness by directing Naaman to God's prophet.
