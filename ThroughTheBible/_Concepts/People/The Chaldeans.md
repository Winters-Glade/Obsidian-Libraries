# The Chaldeans

The dominant ethnic group of the Neo-Babylonian Empire who conquered Jerusalem in 586 BC under Nebuchadnezzar. In the context of Daniel 3, the Chaldeans (or 'Chaldeans' as a class of wise men) accused Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego to the king. The term can refer both to an ethnic group and to a class of Babylonian astrologers/sages.
