# Samuel

## Description

Samuel was the last judge of Israel and the first prophet after Moses. Chuck Smith highlights Samuel's faithful ministry from childhood through the transition to monarchy. Samuel's statement 'To obey is better than sacrifice' is a key principle. He anointed both Saul and David as kings and served as a model of faithful leadership.
