# Adam

## Description

Adam was the first man created by God and the federal head of the human race. Chuck Smith teaches that through Adam's sin, death and condemnation entered the world, but through Christ, the second Adam, comes righteousness and life. The parallel between Adam and Christ (Romans 5) is a cornerstone of Paul's theology that Pastor Smith emphasizes in his teaching.
