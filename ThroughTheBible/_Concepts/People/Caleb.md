# Caleb

## Description

Caleb was one of the twelve spies sent by Moses to explore Canaan. Along with Joshua, he gave a faithful report and encouraged the people to trust God. Because of his faithfulness, he was one of only two adults from the Exodus generation permitted to enter the Promised Land. At age 85, he requested and conquered the hill country of Hebron (Joshua 14:6-15).
