# Benjamin

## Description

Benjamin was the youngest son of Jacob and Rachel. His birth cost Rachel her life, and Jacob renamed him Benjamin ('son of my right hand'), though Rachel had named him Ben-Oni ('son of my sorrow'). The tribe of Benjamin was known for its warriors and produced Israel's first king, Saul. The tribe remained loyal to the house of David after the kingdom divided.
