# Mary Magdalene

## Description

Mary Magdalene was a devoted follower of Jesus from whom He cast out seven demons. She was among the women who supported Jesus' ministry and was present at the crucifixion. She was the first to witness the empty tomb and the first to see the risen Christ, who commissioned her to tell the other disciples. She is a key witness to the resurrection.
