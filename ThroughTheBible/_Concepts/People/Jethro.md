# Jethro (Reuel)

## Description

Jethro, also called Reuel, was the priest of Midian and father-in-law of Moses. He gave wise counsel to Moses on establishing a system of judges to govern Israel (Exodus 18). He recognized Yahweh as greater than all gods and offered sacrifices to Him. His wisdom helped create the judicial structure that sustained Israel in the wilderness.
