# Mary (Mother of Jesus)

## Description

Mary was the virgin mother of Jesus Christ, chosen by God to bear the Messiah. The angel Gabriel announced to her that she would conceive by the Holy Spirit (Luke 1:26-38). Her response, 'Let it be to me according to your word,' models humble submission to God's will. She was present at key moments in Jesus' life, including the crucifixion, and was with the disciples at Pentecost.
