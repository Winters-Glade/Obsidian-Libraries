# Herodotus

The ancient Greek historian (c. 484-425 BC) known as 'the Father of History.' His work 'Histories' provides important extra-biblical context for the Persian period, including details about Xerxes I (Ahasuerus) and the Persian Wars that help illuminate the setting of the books of Ezra, Nehemiah, and Esther.
