# Timothy

## Description

Timothy was Paul's spiritual son and trusted co-worker in the gospel. Chuck Smith highlights the pastoral epistles to Timothy as a guide for church leadership and sound doctrine. Timothy's genuine faith, first dwelling in his grandmother Lois and mother Eunice, and his assignment to oversee the church in Ephesus are key themes. Pastor Smith often references the charge to Timothy to 'preach the word' as a mandate for all ministers.
