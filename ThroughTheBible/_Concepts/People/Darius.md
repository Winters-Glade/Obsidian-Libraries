# Darius

## Description

Darius the Mede was a ruler who conquered Babylon and is mentioned in the Book of Daniel. He appointed 120 satraps over the kingdom and set Daniel as one of three administrators. He was tricked into signing a decree that led to Daniel being thrown into the lion's den, and later rejoiced when Daniel was miraculously delivered (Daniel 6).
