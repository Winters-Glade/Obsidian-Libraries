# The Roman Jewish Leaders

The Jewish religious authorities during the Roman occupation of Judea who cooperated with Roman governance. In the New Testament context, this included members of the Sanhedrin, Pharisees, Sadducees, and the high priestly families who exercised authority under Roman oversight in legal and religious matters.
