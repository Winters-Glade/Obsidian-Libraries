# Nero

## Description

Nero was the Roman emperor who persecuted Christians, including the martyrdom of Peter and Paul. Chuck Smith references Nero as the embodiment of imperial persecution. The historical context of Nero's reign illuminates many passages in Paul's later epistles and the Book of Revelation.
