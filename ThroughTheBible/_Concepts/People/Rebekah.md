# Rebekah

## Description

Rebekah was the wife of Isaac and mother of Jacob and Esau. She demonstrated hospitality to Abraham's servant at the well and agreed to marry Isaac without meeting him first. Her story includes her role in securing the blessing for Jacob rather than Esau, fulfilling God's prophecy that the older would serve the younger. She is one of the four matriarchs of Israel.
