# Jesus Christ (the Word)

The theological title for Jesus as the eternal Logos (Word) of God, as revealed in John 1:1-14. This doctrine declares Christ's pre-existence, His role in creation, and His incarnation as God dwelling among humanity. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that Jesus as the Word reveals God's nature and redemptive plan.
