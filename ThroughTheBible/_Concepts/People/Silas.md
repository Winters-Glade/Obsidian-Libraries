# Silas

## Description

Silas was a leader in the Jerusalem church who accompanied Paul on his second missionary journey. Chuck Smith notes Silas's role in the Philippian jail incident where they sang hymns at midnight. He is co-author with Paul of the Thessalonian epistles.
