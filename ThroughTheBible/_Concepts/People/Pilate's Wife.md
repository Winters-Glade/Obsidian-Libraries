# Pilate's Wife

## Description

Pilate's wife, traditionally named Claudia Procula, is the unnamed woman who sent a message to her husband Pontius Pilate during Jesus' trial, warning him: "Have nothing to do with that just Man, for I have suffered many things today in a dream because of Him." Chuck Smith notes this as a remarkable instance of God speaking through a Gentile and through a dream, warning the Roman governor against condemning the innocent Son of God.
