# Ezekiel

## Description

Ezekiel was a prophet and priest who was among the exiles taken to Babylon in 597 BC. His book contains dramatic visions of God's glory, the valley of dry bones, and a future temple. He served as a watchman to Israel, calling the exiles to individual responsibility and covenant faithfulness. His visions emphasize God's sovereignty and the restoration of Israel.
