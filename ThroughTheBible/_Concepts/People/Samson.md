# Samson

## Description

Samson was a Nazirite judge of Israel known for his superhuman strength, which came from his uncut hair as a sign of his dedication to God. His life was marked by both great victories over the Philistines and personal moral failures. His story illustrates God's sovereignty in using flawed individuals to accomplish His purposes.
