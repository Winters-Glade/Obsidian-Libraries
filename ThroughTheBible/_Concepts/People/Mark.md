# Mark (John Mark)

## Description

Mark, also known as John Mark, was a companion of Paul and Barnabas and later of Peter. He is traditionally credited with writing the Gospel of Mark, which is believed to reflect Peter's eyewitness testimony. His Gospel is characterized by its fast-paced narrative and emphasis on Jesus as the suffering Son of Man.
