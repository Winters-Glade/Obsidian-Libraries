# Jehu

## Description

Jehu was the king of Israel appointed by God to destroy the house of Ahab and eradicate Baal worship. Chuck Smith notes that while Jehu executed God's judgment zealously, he did not depart from the sins of Jeroboam, showing that partial obedience is insufficient.
