# Andrew

## Description

Andrew was one of the twelve apostles, the brother of Simon Peter. He was initially a disciple of John the Baptist before following Jesus. Andrew is known for bringing others to Jesus, including his brother Peter and the boy with five loaves and two fish. He is traditionally believed to have been crucified on an X-shaped cross.
