# Esau

## Description

Esau was the elder son of Isaac and Rebekah, the twin brother of Jacob. Chuck Smith often uses Esau as a warning example of one who despised his birthright for a single meal, illustrating the danger of trading eternal blessings for immediate gratification. The writer of Hebrews warns against being 'profane like Esau.'
