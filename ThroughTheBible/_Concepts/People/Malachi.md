# Malachi

## Description

Malachi was the last of the Old Testament prophets, ministering after the temple was rebuilt. He confronted the people's spiritual apathy, corrupt worship, and social injustice. His book contains prophecies of the coming messenger (John the Baptist) and the Sun of Righteousness (Christ), bridging the Old and New Testaments.
