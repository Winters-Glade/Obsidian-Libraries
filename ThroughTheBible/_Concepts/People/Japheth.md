# Japheth

## Description

Japheth was one of the three sons of Noah, along with Shem and Ham. After the Flood, Japheth's descendants are traditionally understood to have populated Europe and parts of Asia. Noah's blessing over Japheth declared that God would enlarge Japheth and that he would dwell in the tents of Shem (Genesis 9:27).
