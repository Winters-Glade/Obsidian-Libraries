# Nehemiah

## Description

Nehemiah was a Jewish cupbearer to King Artaxerxes of Persia who became governor of Judah. He led the rebuilding of Jerusalem's walls in just 52 days despite intense opposition. His book records his leadership, prayer life, and reforms, providing a model of faith-driven initiative combined with practical wisdom.
