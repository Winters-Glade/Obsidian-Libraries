# Cornelius

## Description

Cornelius was a Roman centurion stationed in Caesarea who is described as a devout man who feared God (Acts 10). He received a vision instructing him to send for Peter, which led to Peter's visit and the outpouring of the Holy Spirit on Cornelius and his household—the first Gentiles to receive the gospel. This event opened the door for Gentile inclusion in the church.
