# courage

The strength of character to face fear, danger, or opposition with faith in God. Scripture repeatedly commands courage, especially in the context of God's calling: 'Be strong and of good courage' (Joshua 1:6, 9). Biblical courage is not the absence of fear but the determination to obey God despite fear. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that courage comes from trusting God's promises rather than human ability.
