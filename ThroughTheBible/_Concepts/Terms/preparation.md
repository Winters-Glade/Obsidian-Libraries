# preparation

The act of making ready for future events, emphasized in Scripture as a spiritual discipline. John the Baptist prepared the way for the Lord (Matthew 3:3). Paul instructed believers to be ready in season and out of season (2 Timothy 4:2). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that spiritual preparation involves prayer, study of Scripture, and maintaining a heart ready for Christ's return.
