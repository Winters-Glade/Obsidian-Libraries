# Jesus Christ (the Messiah)

## Description

Jesus Christ is the Messiah (Christos, "Anointed One") of God, the fulfillment of Old Testament prophecies concerning the promised deliverer of Israel and the Savior of the world. Chuck Smith teaches throughout the T2000 series that the entire Old Testament points forward to Jesus — the prophets, the sacrifices, the feasts, and the covenants all find their fulfillment in Him. As the Messiah, Jesus is Prophet, Priest, and King — the anointed Servant who came first to suffer and die for sins, and will return to reign in glory.
