# Herodotus

## Description

Herodotus (c. 484-425 BC) was the ancient Greek historian known as the 'Father of History.' His work 'The Histories' provides valuable information about the Persian Empire, including details that corroborate the biblical accounts of Esther and Ezra-Nehemiah. Chuck Smith references Herodotus as an extra-biblical source that confirms the historical reliability of Scripture's accounts of Persian rulers and customs.
