# The Gentiles

## Description

Gentiles (from Latin gentilis, 'of the same clan or nation') in Scripture refers to all nations other than Israel. Chuck Smith traces the biblical theme of the Gentiles — from God's promise to Abraham that all nations would be blessed through him, through the prophets' vision of the nations streaming to Jerusalem, to the New Testament's inclusion of Gentiles in the church. The gospel breaks down the dividing wall between Jew and Gentile, making one new humanity in Christ.
