# The Ishmaelites/Midianites

## Description

The Ishmaelites/Midianites is referenced in the T2000 Topical Series by Pastor Chuck Smith. The Ishmaelites and Midianites were trading peoples of the ancient Near East. They purchased Joseph from his brothers and sold him into slavery in Egypt (Genesis 37).
