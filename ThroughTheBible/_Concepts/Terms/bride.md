# bride

A central biblical metaphor for the relationship between God and His people. In the Old Testament, Israel is portrayed as the bride of Yahweh (Hosea 2:19-20, Jeremiah 2:2). In the New Testament, the church is described as the bride of Christ, prepared as a bride adorned for her husband (Ephesians 5:25-27, Revelation 21:2). This imagery speaks of covenant love, intimacy, and the coming marriage supper of the Lamb.
