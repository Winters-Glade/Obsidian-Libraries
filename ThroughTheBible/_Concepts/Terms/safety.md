# safety

The condition of being protected from harm or danger, which in Scripture is ultimately found only in God. The Psalms repeatedly affirm that those who dwell in the shelter of the Most High are safe (Psalm 91). True safety is not the absence of physical threats but the assurance that one's life is secure in God's hands regardless of circumstances.
