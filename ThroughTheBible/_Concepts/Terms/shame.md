# shame

The painful emotion of humiliation or disgrace that entered human experience with the Fall (Genesis 3:7-10). Adam and Eve felt shame for the first time after sinning. Scripture promises that those who trust in Christ will never be put to shame (Romans 10:11), and that God will ultimately wipe away the shame of His people. The gospel replaces shame with honor in God's family.
