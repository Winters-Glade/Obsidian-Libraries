# Deity

## Description

Deity refers to the divine nature and essence of God. In Christian theology, the term is central to understanding both the nature of God the Father and the deity of Jesus Christ. Chuck Smith emphasizes that Jesus Christ is fully God — not a lesser divine being or created entity — affirming the orthodox Christian doctrine that in Christ all the fullness of the Deity dwells in bodily form (Colossians 2:9). The deity of Christ distinguishes Christianity from all other religions.
