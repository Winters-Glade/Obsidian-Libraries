# Refuge

See 'refuge.' In Scripture, God is described as 'a refuge for the oppressed, a stronghold in times of trouble' (Psalm 9:9). The concept permeates the Psalms, where David frequently declares God to be his refuge and fortress. Believers are called to make God their refuge, running to Him for safety from life's storms and from the judgment due for sin.
