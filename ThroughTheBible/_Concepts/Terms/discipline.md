# discipline

The training, correction, and instruction that shapes character and produces godliness. Scripture presents discipline as an expression of God's love for His children (Hebrews 12:5-11, Proverbs 3:11-12). Godly discipline includes self-control, training in righteousness, and divine correction. Chuck Smith taught that God's discipline is always purposeful, designed to produce the peaceful fruit of righteousness.
