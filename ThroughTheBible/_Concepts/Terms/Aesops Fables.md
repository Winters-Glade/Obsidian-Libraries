# Aesop's Fables

## Description

Aesop's Fables are a collection of moral tales attributed to Aesop, a Greek storyteller who lived around 620-564 BC. These fables, featuring animals with human characteristics, teach moral lessons through short narratives. Chuck Smith may reference them as examples of universal moral truths found outside Scripture, while emphasizing that only God's Word provides ultimate moral and spiritual authority for life.
