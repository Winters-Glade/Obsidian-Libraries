# Eternity

## Description

Eternity is the timeless, infinite duration of God's existence and the future state of both the righteous and the wicked. Chuck Smith teaches that God has set eternity in the hearts of humanity (Ecclesiastes 3:11), giving every person an innate sense that there is more than the material world. The choices made in this temporal life have eternal consequences — either everlasting life with God or eternal separation from Him. Eternity gives weight and urgency to the gospel message.
