# deity

The divine nature and essential being of God. In Christian theology, the term affirms that Jesus Christ possesses full deity—He is God in the flesh (John 1:1-14, Colossians 2:9, Philippians 2:6). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that the deity of Christ is essential to the Christian faith; a Savior who is less than God cannot save, and a Christ who is not divine cannot atone for sin.
