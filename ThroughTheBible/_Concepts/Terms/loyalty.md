# loyalty

Faithful devotion and steadfast commitment, particularly to God and His people. Scripture celebrates loyalty as a godly character trait, exemplified by Ruth's devotion to Naomi and Jonathan's friendship with David. God Himself is described as loyal and faithful to His covenant. In a world of shifting allegiances, loyalty to Christ is the mark of genuine discipleship.
