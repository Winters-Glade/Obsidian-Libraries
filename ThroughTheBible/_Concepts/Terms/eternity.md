# eternity

The timeless, unending existence of God and the future state of all creation. Scripture declares that God inhabits eternity (Isaiah 57:15) and has set eternity in the hearts of humanity (Ecclesiastes 3:11). Every person will exist eternally—either in the presence of God or separated from Him. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that how one responds to Christ in this life determines their eternal destiny.
