# offering

A gift presented to God as an act of worship, thanksgiving, or atonement. The Old Testament system of offerings included burnt offerings, grain offerings, peace offerings, sin offerings, and trespass offerings (Leviticus 1-7). These offerings prefigured the ultimate sacrifice of Christ. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that the New Testament call is to present our bodies as a living sacrifice (Romans 12:1).
