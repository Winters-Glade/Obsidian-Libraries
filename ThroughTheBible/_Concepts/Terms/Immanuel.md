# Immanuel

## Description

Immanuel (also spelled Emmanuel) means "God with us." It is the prophetic name given in Isaiah 7:14, foretelling the virgin birth of a child who would embody God's presence with His people. Matthew's Gospel explicitly identifies Jesus as the fulfillment of this prophecy. Chuck Smith emphasizes Immanuel as one of the most profound truths of Christianity — that God did not merely send a messenger but came Himself in the person of Jesus Christ to dwell among humanity, bringing salvation and the very presence of God to earth.
