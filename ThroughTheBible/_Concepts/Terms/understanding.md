# understanding

The spiritual capacity to comprehend God's truth, discern right from wrong, and apply wisdom to life. Proverbs repeatedly urges the pursuit of understanding as more valuable than silver or gold. In the New Testament, Paul prays for believers to be filled with spiritual understanding (Colossians 1:9). Understanding is a gift from God that grows through studying Scripture and walking in obedience.
