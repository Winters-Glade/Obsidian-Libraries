# giving

The biblical practice of generosity as an expression of worship and trust in God's provision. Scripture teaches that giving should be cheerful, sacrificial, and systematic (2 Corinthians 9:7, 1 Corinthians 16:2). Jesus said 'It is more blessed to give than to receive' (Acts 20:35). Pastor Chuck Smith taught that giving is not about obligation but about participating in God's work.
