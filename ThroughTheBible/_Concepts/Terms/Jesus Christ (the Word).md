# Jesus Christ (the Word)

## Description

Jesus Christ is the Word (Logos) of God — the eternal, divine self-expression through whom all things were made. John's Gospel opens with this profound declaration: "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God." Chuck Smith teaches that Jesus as the Word means He is the full and final communication of God to humanity. As the Word, Jesus is both the message and the messenger, the revelation of God in human form, speaking God's truth and embodying God's grace.
