# Heavenly Army

## Description

The Heavenly Army (also called the "host of heaven" or "armies of heaven") refers to the angelic forces that serve God and carry out His purposes. The most dramatic visible appearance in the T2000 series is at Dothan, where Elisha's servant saw the mountain full of horses and chariots of fire surrounding the city. Chuck Smith emphasizes that the heavenly army is always present, even when invisible to physical sight. These angelic beings are ministering spirits sent to serve those who inherit salvation, far outnumbering any earthly force arrayed against God's people.
