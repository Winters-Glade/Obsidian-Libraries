# witness

One who testifies to the truth, particularly to the truth of the gospel. Jesus called His followers to be His witnesses in Jerusalem, Judea, Samaria, and to the ends of the earth (Acts 1:8). The Holy Spirit empowers believers for this witness. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that every believer is called to be a witness, not by eloquence but by authentically sharing what Christ has done.
