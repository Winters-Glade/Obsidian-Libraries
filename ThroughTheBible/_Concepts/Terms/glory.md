# glory

The manifest weight and brilliance of God's presence, character, and majesty. The Hebrew word kabod literally means 'weight' or 'heaviness.' God's glory filled the Tabernacle and Temple (Exodus 40:34, 1 Kings 8:11), and is revealed supremely in Jesus Christ (John 1:14). Believers are called to glorify God and are being transformed from glory to glory (2 Corinthians 3:18).
