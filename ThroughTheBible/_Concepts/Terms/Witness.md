# Witness

See 'witness.' In the New Testament, the Greek word martys emphasizes the legal and sacrificial nature of Christian testimony. Many early believers became witnesses even to the point of death (martyrdom). The Holy Spirit is the primary witness to Christ, and believers are secondary witnesses, testifying to what they have seen and heard.
