# purpose

The divinely ordained reason for which each person exists. Scripture teaches that God has a purpose for every life (Jeremiah 29:11, Psalm 139:16, Ephesians 2:10). Discovering God's purpose is the key to meaningful living. Pastor Chuck Smith emphasized that the ultimate purpose of humanity is to glorify God and enjoy Him forever.
