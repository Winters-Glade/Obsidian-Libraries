# Loyalty

See loyalty. Faithful devotion and steadfast commitment to God and His people. Scripture celebrates loyalty as an attribute of God Himself—His covenant loyalty (chesed) is the foundation of His relationship with Israel. Human loyalty, though imperfect, reflects God's faithful character and is a mark of true discipleship.
