# Bible

The divinely inspired collection of sacred Scriptures comprising the Old and New Testaments, which Christians believe is the written Word of God. The Bible is 'profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness' (2 Timothy 3:16). Pastor Chuck Smith dedicated his ministry to teaching the Bible verse by verse, emphasizing its authority and sufficiency for faith and practice.
