# Dunamis (Power)

The Greek word dunamis from which 'dynamite' derives, used in the New Testament for the miraculous power of God. Jesus told His disciples they would receive dunamis when the Holy Spirit came upon them (Acts 1:8). This power is not human ability but God's supernatural enablement for witness, service, and spiritual transformation.
