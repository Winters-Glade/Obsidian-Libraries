# refuge

A place of safety, shelter, and protection, used throughout Scripture as a metaphor for God Himself. God is repeatedly called a refuge for the oppressed, a stronghold in times of trouble (Psalm 9:9, 46:1). The cities of refuge in the Old Testament (Numbers 35) prefigure the safety found in Christ. Pastor Chuck Smith taught that believers find their ultimate security in God alone.
