# unity

The state of being one in Christ among believers, despite diversity of background, gift, or perspective. Jesus prayed for His followers to be one as He and the Father are one (John 17:21). Paul urged believers to maintain the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace (Ephesians 4:3). True Christian unity is not uniformity but harmonious diversity under Christ's headship.
