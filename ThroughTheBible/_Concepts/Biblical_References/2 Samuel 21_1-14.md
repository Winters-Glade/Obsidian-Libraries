# 2 Samuel 21:1-14

## Description

2 Samuel 21:1-14 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the reign of David and God's covenant with him. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
