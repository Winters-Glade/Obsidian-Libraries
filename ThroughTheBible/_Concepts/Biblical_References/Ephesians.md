# Ephesians

## Description

Ephesians presents the cosmic scope of God's plan of salvation in Christ and the believer's position in Him. Chuck Smith highlights Paul's prayer for spiritual revelation, the unity of Jew and Gentile in one body, the mystery of Christ and the church, and the armor of God for spiritual warfare.
