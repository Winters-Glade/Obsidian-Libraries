# 1 Chronicles

## Description

1 Chronicles retells Israel's history from a priestly perspective, emphasizing the Davidic covenant and Temple worship. Chuck Smith notes how the genealogies establish continuity from Adam to David, and how David's preparation for the Temple dominates the narrative.
