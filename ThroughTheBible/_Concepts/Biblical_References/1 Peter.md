# 1 Peter

## Description

1 Peter is written to suffering believers scattered throughout Asia Minor. Chuck Smith emphasizes Peter's message of hope in suffering: believers have a living hope through the resurrection, are called to holiness in a hostile world, and can rejoice in suffering as participants in Christ's sufferings.
