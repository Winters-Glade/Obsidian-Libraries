# 1 Samuel

## Description

1 Samuel records Israel's transition from the period of the judges to the monarchy, focusing on the lives of Samuel, Saul, and David. Chuck Smith highlights key themes: God's sovereignty in raising up leaders, the danger of rejecting God's rule, and the heart of David as a man after God's own heart.
