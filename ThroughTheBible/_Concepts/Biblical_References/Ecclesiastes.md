# Ecclesiastes

## Description

Ecclesiastes is Solomon's reflection on the meaninglessness of life 'under the sun' — apart from God. Chuck Smith emphasizes its message that true meaning is found only when life is lived in the fear of God. 'Vanity of vanities, all is vanity' apart from the Creator.
