# Proverbs 30:7-9

## Description

Proverbs 30:7-9 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of wisdom, the fear of the Lord, and righteous living. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
