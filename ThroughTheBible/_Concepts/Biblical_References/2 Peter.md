# 2 Peter

## Description

2 Peter is Peter's final letter, warning against false teachers and emphasizing the certainty of Christ's return. Chuck Smith highlights Peter's call to grow in grace and knowledge, his reminder that the Lord is not slack concerning His promise, and his expectation of new heavens and a new earth.
