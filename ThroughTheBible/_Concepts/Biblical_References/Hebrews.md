# Hebrews

## Description

The Epistle to the Hebrews presents Jesus Christ as superior to all that came before — greater than angels, greater than Moses, greater than the Levitical priesthood. Chuck Smith emphasizes its central theme: the superiority of the new covenant established by Christ's once-for-all sacrifice. Hebrews calls believers to persevere in faith, looking to Jesus the author and finisher of our faith.
