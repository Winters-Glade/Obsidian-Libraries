# Luke

## Description

The Gospel of Luke, written by the physician and traveling companion of Paul, is the most comprehensive account of Jesus' life and ministry. Chuck Smith highlights Luke's attention to detail, his emphasis on prayer, the Holy Spirit, and Jesus' compassion for the poor, women, and outcasts. Luke presents Jesus as the perfect Son of Man who came to seek and save the lost.
