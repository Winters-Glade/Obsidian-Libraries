# Hebrews 9:11-12, 28

## Description

Hebrews 9:11-12, 28 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the superiority of Christ and the new covenant. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
