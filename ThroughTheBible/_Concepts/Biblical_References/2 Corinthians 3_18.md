# 2 Corinthians 3:18

## Description

2 Corinthians 3:18 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of apostolic ministry, suffering, and the comfort of God. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
