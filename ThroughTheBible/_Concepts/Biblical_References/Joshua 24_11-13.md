# Joshua 24:11-13

## Description

Joshua 24:11-13 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of conquest, faith, and the inheritance of God's promises. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
