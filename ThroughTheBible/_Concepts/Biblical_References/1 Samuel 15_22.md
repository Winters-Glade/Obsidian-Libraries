# 1 Samuel 15:22

## Description

1 Samuel 15:22 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the establishment of the monarchy and prophetic ministry. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
