# Leviticus

## Description

Leviticus is the handbook of holiness for God's people, detailing the sacrificial system, priesthood, and laws of purity. Chuck Smith shows how Leviticus prefigures Christ — the various offerings, the Day of Atonement, and the feasts all point to His perfect sacrifice and ongoing intercession.
