# Deuteronomy

## Description

Deuteronomy records Moses' final speeches to Israel before entering the Promised Land. Its name means 'second law' — a restatement and expansion of the Law for a new generation. Chuck Smith emphasizes its themes of covenant faithfulness, blessing and cursing, and the central commandment to 'love the Lord your God with all your heart.'
