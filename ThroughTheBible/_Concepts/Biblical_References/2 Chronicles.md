# 2 Chronicles

## Description

2 Chronicles continues the history of the Davidic dynasty in Judah, focusing on the Temple and the spiritual reforms of kings like Jehoshaphat, Hezekiah, and Josiah. Chuck Smith traces how faithfulness brought blessing and disobedience led to judgment, culminating in the Babylonian exile and Cyrus's decree of return.
