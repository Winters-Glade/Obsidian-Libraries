# Ecclesiastes 12:13-14

## Description

Ecclesiastes 12:13-14 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the meaninglessness of life without God. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
