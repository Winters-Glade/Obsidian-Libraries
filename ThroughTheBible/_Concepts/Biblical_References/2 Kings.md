# 2 Kings

## Description

2 Kings continues the history of Israel's divided monarchy, recording the ministries of Elijah and Elisha, the fall of the northern kingdom to Assyria, and the fall of the southern kingdom to Babylon. Chuck Smith traces the progressive decline caused by idolatry and covenant unfaithfulness.
