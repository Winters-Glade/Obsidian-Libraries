# Psalm 73

## Description

Psalm 73 is a wisdom psalm of Asaph that wrestles with the age-old problem of the prosperity of the wicked and the suffering of the righteous. The psalmist admits his envy of the arrogant until he enters the sanctuary of God and understands their end. Chuck Smith uses this psalm to teach that perspective changes when believers view life from God's sanctuary — understanding the eternal destiny of the wicked transforms how we interpret temporary circumstances.
