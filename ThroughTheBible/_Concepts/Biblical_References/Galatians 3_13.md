# Galatians 3:13

## Description

Galatians 3:13 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of freedom from the Law and justification by faith alone. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
