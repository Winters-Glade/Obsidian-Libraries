# Philippians 4:11-13

## Description

Philippians 4:11-13 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of joy in Christ, humility, and the mind of Christ. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
