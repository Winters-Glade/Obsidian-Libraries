# Jeremiah 50:34

## Description

Jeremiah 50:34 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of judgment, the new covenant, and the call to repentance. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
