# Romans 8

## Description

Romans 8 is perhaps the most beloved chapter in the Bible, declaring that there is no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus. Chuck Smith highlights its themes: life in the Spirit, sonship and adoption, the groaning of creation, God's foreknowledge and predestination, and the unshakable assurance that nothing can separate us from the love of God in Christ Jesus.
