# Luke 12:16-21

## Description

Luke 12:16-21 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the humanity of Christ and His compassion for the outcast. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
