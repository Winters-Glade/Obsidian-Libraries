# Ephesians 2:13

## Description

Ephesians 2:13 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the church as the body of Christ and spiritual blessings. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
