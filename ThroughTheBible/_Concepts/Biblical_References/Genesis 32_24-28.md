# Genesis 32:24-28

## Description

Genesis 32:24-28 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of creation, the patriarchs, and God's covenant promises. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
