# Leviticus 14:1-32

## Description

Leviticus 14:1-32 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of holiness, sacrifice, and worship. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
