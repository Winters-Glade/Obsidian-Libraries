# 1 John

## Description

1 John was written to assure believers of their salvation and to combat false teaching. Chuck Smith highlights its themes of walking in the light, love for the brethren, and the tests of genuine faith — right belief (orthodoxy), right behavior (obedience), and right relationships (love).
