# Leviticus 25:47-49

## Description

Leviticus 25:47-49 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of holiness, sacrifice, and worship. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
