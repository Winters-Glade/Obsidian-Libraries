# Genesis

## Description

Genesis, the book of beginnings, lays the foundation for all of Scripture. Chuck Smith traces the major themes through Genesis: creation, the fall of man, the flood, the call of Abraham, and the patriarchs. Every major doctrine of Scripture has its seed in Genesis — creation, sin, judgment, covenant, faith, and redemption.
