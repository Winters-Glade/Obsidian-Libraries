# Genesis 22

## Description

Genesis 22 records the Akedah — Abraham's binding of Isaac on Mount Moriah. God tested Abraham by commanding him to offer his beloved son as a sacrifice. At the last moment, God provided a ram as a substitute. Chuck Smith teaches this as one of the most powerful types of Christ in Scripture — the beloved son carrying the wood, the father offering his only son, and the substitute provided by God Himself.
