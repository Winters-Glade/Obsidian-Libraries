# Psalm 37

## Description

Psalm 37 is a wisdom psalm exhorting believers not to fret because of evildoers but to trust in the Lord and do good. Chuck Smith draws from this psalm the repeated theme of waiting on God — 'Rest in the Lord, and wait patiently for Him.' The psalm contrasts the temporary success of the wicked with the secure inheritance of the righteous who delight in the Lord.
