# Psalms

## Description

The Book of Psalms is the hymnbook and prayer book of Israel. Chuck Smith draws extensively from the Psalms in his teaching, finding in them the full range of human emotion expressed in worship — praise, lament, thanksgiving, confession, and prophetic anticipation of the Messiah. The Psalms teach believers how to pray honestly and worship authentically.
