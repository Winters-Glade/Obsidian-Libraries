# Deuteronomy 6:1-3

## Description

Deuteronomy 6:1-3 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the covenant relationship between God and His people. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
