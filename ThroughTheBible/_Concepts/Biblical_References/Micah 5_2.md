# Micah 5:2

## Description

Micah 5:2 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of justice, mercy, and the coming Messiah from Bethlehem. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
