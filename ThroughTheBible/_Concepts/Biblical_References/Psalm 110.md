# Psalm 110

## Description

Psalm 110 is one of the most quoted psalms in the New Testament, prophesying Christ's priesthood after the order of Melchizedek and His position at the right hand of God until His enemies are made His footstool. Chuck Smith emphasizes this psalm as a key messianic prophecy — Jesus Himself quoted it to confound the Pharisees, asking how David could call the Messiah 'Lord' if the Messiah was merely David's son.
