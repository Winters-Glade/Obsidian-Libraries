# 2 Kings 21:6

## Description

2 Kings 21:6 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the decline and exile of Israel and Judah. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
