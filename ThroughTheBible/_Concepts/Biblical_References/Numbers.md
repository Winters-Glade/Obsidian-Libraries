# Numbers

## Description

Numbers chronicles Israel's wilderness wanderings due to unbelief. The book records the census of the tribes, the mixed multitude's complaining, the rebellion of Korah, and Balaam's oracles. Chuck Smith emphasizes the lessons of faith versus unbelief — how the generation that doubted God did not enter the Promised Land.
