# Proverbs

## Description

Proverbs is a collection of wisdom sayings that teach practical godliness for everyday life. Chuck Smith highlights the book's central theme: 'The fear of the Lord is the beginning of wisdom.' The proverbs contrast wisdom and folly, righteousness and wickedness, diligence and laziness, offering guidance for living in a way that honors God and leads to blessing.
