# Judges 4:1-24

## Description

Judges 4:1-24 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of cycles of sin and deliverance under God's sovereign hand. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
