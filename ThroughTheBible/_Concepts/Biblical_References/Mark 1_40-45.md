# Mark 1:40-45

## Description

Mark 1:40-45 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the servanthood and sacrificial ministry of Jesus. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
