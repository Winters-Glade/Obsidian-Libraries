# Genesis 3

## Description

Genesis 3 records the fall of man — the serpent's temptation, Eve's deception, Adam's transgression, and God's judgment. In the midst of judgment, God gave the protoevangelion (first gospel) — the seed of the woman would crush the serpent's head. Chuck Smith traces all of redemptive history from this chapter, showing how God's plan of salvation was set in motion immediately after the fall.
