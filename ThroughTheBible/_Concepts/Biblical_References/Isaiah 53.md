# Isaiah 53

## Description

Isaiah 53 is the greatest messianic prophecy in the Old Testament, describing the suffering Servant who would bear the sins of many and make intercession for the transgressors. Written 700 years before Christ, it describes His rejection, suffering, death, and resurrection in detail. Chuck Smith calls this chapter the gospel in the Old Testament.
