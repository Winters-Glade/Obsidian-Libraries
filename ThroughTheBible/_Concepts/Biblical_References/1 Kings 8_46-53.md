# 1 Kings 8:46-53

## Description

1 Kings 8:46-53 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the kingdom of Israel, wisdom, and the divided monarchy. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
