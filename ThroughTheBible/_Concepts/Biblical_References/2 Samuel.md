# 2 Samuel

## Description

2 Samuel records David's reign over Israel — his victories, his great sin with Bathsheba, and its consequences. Chuck Smith highlights both David's heart for God and the devastating ripple effects of unconfessed sin, while pointing to God's faithfulness to the Davidic covenant.
