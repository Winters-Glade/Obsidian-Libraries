# Lamentations

## Description

Lamentations is a collection of five sorrowful poems mourning the destruction of Jerusalem by Babylon. Chuck Smith emphasizes that even in judgment, Jeremiah's hope rests in God's character: 'Through the Lord's mercies we are not consumed, because His compassions fail not. They are new every morning.'
