# 1 Peter 1:18-19

## Description

1 Peter 1:18-19 teaches that believers were redeemed not with corruptible things like silver or gold but with the precious blood of Christ, as of a lamb without blemish and without spot. Chuck Smith emphasizes the costliness of redemption — nothing less than the blood of the Son of God could pay the penalty for sin, making salvation both free to the believer and infinitely costly to God.
