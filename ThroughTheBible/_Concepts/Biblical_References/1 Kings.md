# 1 Kings

## Description

1 Kings records the reign of Solomon, the building of the Temple, and the division of the kingdom under Rehoboam and Jeroboam. Chuck Smith emphasizes the tragedy of Solomon's downfall through compromise and idolatry, and the ministry of Elijah confronting Ahab and Jezebel.
