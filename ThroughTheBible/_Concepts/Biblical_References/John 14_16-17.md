# John 14:16-17

## Description

John 14:16-17 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of the deity of Christ and eternal life through faith in Him. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
