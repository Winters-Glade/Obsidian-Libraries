# John

## Description

The Gospel of John presents Jesus Christ as the divine Son of God, the eternal Word made flesh. Chuck Smith emphasizes John's unique perspective — focusing not merely on what Jesus did but on who He is. From the prologue (In the beginning was the Word) to Thomas's confession (My Lord and my God), John's purpose is clear: 'that you may believe that Jesus is the Christ, the Son of God, and that believing you may have life in His name.'
