# Zechariah 7:9-10

## Description

Zechariah 7:9-10 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of visions of restoration and the coming Messiah. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
