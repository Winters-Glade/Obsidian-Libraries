# Book of Exodus

## Description

The Book of Exodus is the second book of the Torah and the Old Testament, recounting God's deliverance of Israel from slavery in Egypt through Moses. It includes the ten plagues, the Passover, the crossing of the Red Sea, the giving of the Law at Mount Sinai, and the construction of the Tabernacle. Chuck Smith treats Exodus as a foundational book of redemption—the pattern of salvation by grace through the Passover lamb, the establishment of covenant relationship, and God's desire to dwell among His people.
