# Exodus 32:30-33

## Description

Exodus 32:30-33 is a key passage in the teachings of Chuck Smith. This verse highlights important themes of redemption, deliverance, and the giving of the Law. Smith often referenced this passage to illustrate the nature of God's relationship with His people and the principles of Christian living.
